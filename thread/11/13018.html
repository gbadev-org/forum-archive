Help Wanted > Pixel artist needed for DS game.

#126008 - BovineFury - Thu Apr 19, 2007 3:17 am

Game name:
Voltz DS

Who I need:
Pixel artist to work on a home brew DS game.
I'm looking for someone who has done DS art before and knows the ropes. The lucky artist would need to design art for a game already in development. I'm willing to give a lot of free rein with the art style and content. The game needs to be appropriate for all ages (no bare bouncing boobies, demon disemboweling, etc).

What art is needed:
I am very, very art challenged, so you would need to do everything. That means menus, backgrounds, action animations, etc. The art extent would be mainly up to you.

Art format:
Do you have tools you like to use? Great. Let me know what they are, the formats, etc and I'll see about working them in.

Game concept:
The player is shown a board with energy storing nodes. Random nodes are set with random charges, and the player must clear all charges from the board. This is done by adding energy to nodes until they reach a critical charge. When this happens, the node releases all the energy it contains onto the board. Released energy travels until it hits a charged node or goes off the board.
The player starts with a set amount of energy. The energy is depleted every time the player charges a node. Extra energy can be earned by causing chained node bursts.
An example would be the player charging a node which bursts, and the released energy hits other nodes and causes them to burst.
