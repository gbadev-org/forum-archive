DS PROGRAMMER
Title: Programmer (DS)
Application deadline: 16. March 2008
Starting date: ASAP
Location: Kristiansand/Norway (may also be open for online work)
Company: AGENIUS INTERACTIVE AS (www.agenius.no)

Information
Are you a passionate programmer who wants to develop world-class game titles? AGENIUS is looking for a DS programmer who wish to develop an exciting new breed of DS titles. There may also be an opportunity to move on to other platforms.

This position is initial for 6 months, but with good chances for expansion for the right person.

Initial responsibilities are
- Designing, debugging and implementing code for Nintendo DS
- Collaborating with other team mates in various projects to develop great entertainment

The position requires a person who
- is fluent in C/C++ programming with good debugging and optimization skills
- are able to document, schedule and scope own programming tasks
- are able to deliver quality work within project milestones
- have good math skills
- are able to learn fast and are motivated to continuously expand own knowledge and skills
- are self-motivated, loyal and have strong work ethic
- are well organized and with great attention to details
- have good communications skills and can communicate in English, both written and verbal

We are also looking for a great person who
- are dedicated problem solver who never quits
- are passionate about games and creating world-class innovative entertainment
- preferably can relocate to Agenius HQ in Kristiansand/Norway, but this is not a requirement

AGENIUS can offer
- Competitive salary
- Integration into a professional team and an experienced management
- A company focusing on a good work environment, fun and recreation
- Flexible work hours
- Relocation support and assistance with finding private accommodation
- Great career and training/education opportunities for the right person
- Good benefits (perks, outings) and lots of fun

How to apply
- Your application letter
- Your resume
- Your portfolio showing you relevant projects (if mail attachements exceed 2 MB please include links to online resources instead)
- In collaborative work examples please include a work breakdown explaining your contribution
- Send mail to career(@)agenius(.)no

ABOUT AGENIUS INTERACTIVE
AGENIUS Interactive AS is an authorized NINTENDO developer located in Kristiansand, Norway.

AGENIUS are the creators of IntelliGames(TM), a new generation of games focusing on enjoyable entertainment adding non-intrusive learning mechanisms.

The company is focusing on creating well-designed game play and highly enjoyable and immersive entertainment experiences. The company credo is:

"do what we love, do it better than anybody else and contribute to bring more smiles into the world"

THE TEAM
The AGENIUS team consists of highly skilled and passionate designers, programmers and artists, and we are continuously looking for the best and the brightest technical and artistic talents around the globe to strengthen our core development team.

We take pride in our unique down-to-earth atmosphere cultivated by an enthusiastic, skilled and fun group of individuals. We invite you to apply to become a member of our team.

ENVIRONMENT
Being comfortable at work is top priority at AGENIUS Interactive. Focusing on work environment is important in order to cultivate creative energy. We don't like to call our workplace the "office", or not even a "workplace". We like to call it our second home.

ORGANIZATION
We believe the success of our projects is built, in part, upon the friendships that support the projects, and partly in how we organize the workplace.

As a result, the space we chose is very open, fostering a free exchange of ideas, discussions and brainstorming.

OPEN FRIDAYS
Every Friday we set aside a few hours to empty our minds and refill with new creative inspiration. This can be a team session or workshop about drawing, painting, sculpting, animation, photography, video, design, acting or even music. Or it can be looking into a movie, comic, book, magazine, artwork or other cultural or social elements that we just find cool to share with each other.

PERKS & OUTINGS
Our staff invests a lot of passion and energy into our projects and we reward this by re-investing in our employees through perks in and out of the office.

Regular company trips help bond the team together, and it includes annual weekend trips, parties and social outings like movie nights, shows, poker nights, game tournaments etc. The idea is to foster an environment in which the employees can pursue a healthy and well-balanced lifestyle.

SELF-DEVELOPMENT
"From new input comes new output". This is the core idea for innovative thinking at AGENIUS. We build our entire business on innovation and creativity, and individuals with an open and hungry mind will fit well with our group.

AGENIUS supports talented and loyal team members with education and courses.
