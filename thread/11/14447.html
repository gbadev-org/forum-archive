DS PROGRAMMER
Title: Senior Programmer (DS)
Application deadline: 16. November 2007
Starting Date: 3. January 2008
Location: Kristiansand/Norway
Company: www.agenius.no
Pay range: 60 000$ - 85000$ (DOE)

INFO
Are you a passionate programmer who wants to develop world-class game titles? If the answer is yes, AGENIUS is looking for talented, experienced and great staff to work on an rewarding project in Kristiansand, Norway. This is a great opportunity to experience the exotic, prosperous and beautiful nordic part of europe.

AGENIUS is looking for a DS programmer who wish to develop an exciting new breed of DS titles. There may also be an opportunity to move on to other next-gen gaming platforms.

The DS Programmer is responsible for leading the programming tasks on the DS console within the company. The programmer must have excellent knowledge and high personal standards in order to ensure top notch quality. Programming responsibilities may include several aspects of game development ranging from user interface, gameplay, graphic and audio.

This position is initial for a 1 year project, but with good chances to full-time expansion for the right person.

Responsibilities:
- Designing, debugging and implementing code for Nintendo DS games
- Collaborating with other teammates in various projects to develop great entertainment

Professional Requirements:
- Fluent in C/C++ programming with good debugging and optimization skills
- Professional game programming experience, preferably with a published title.
- Handheld console game development experience, such as Game Boy Advance or Nintendo DS
- Experience in one or more of the following disciplines: 2D graphics, gameplay, database, interface
- Able to document, schedule and scope own programming tasks
- Good math skills

Personal Requirements:
- Last learner and motivated to continuously expand own horizon
- Well organized and with great attention to details
- Self-motivated, loyal and strong work ethic
- A dedicated problem solver who never quits
- Good communications skills and fluent in English, both written and verbal
- Passionate about games and creating world-class innovative entertainment
- Willing to relocate to Agenius HQ in Kristiansand/Norway

AGENIUS can offer:
- Competitive salary
- Project bonuses
- Integration into a professional team and an experienced management
- A company focusing on a good work environment, fun and recreation
- Flexible work hours (core hours 09.00-15.00)
- Paid insurance
- 5 weeks annual holidays
- Excellent health care benefits
- Relocation support and assistance with finding private accommodation
- Great career and training/education opportunities for the right person
- Good benefits (perks, outings) and lots of fun

About Us
AGENIUS Interactive AS is located in Kristiansand, Norway.

AGENIUS is dedicated to provide a new breed of games, which combine fun and training in a revolutionary way. We call these games INTELLIGAMES (iG).

The team consists of highly skilled and passionate designers, programmers and artists, and we are continuously looking for the best and the brightest technical and artistic talents around the globe to strengthen our core development team.

We take pride in our unique down-to-earth atmosphere and we invite you to apply to become a member of our team.

For more info, contact information and how to apply please visit http://www.agenius.no
