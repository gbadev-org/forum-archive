PREFFERED:

- Prior experience working with tools programming a bonus.

TO APPLY:
Include PROGRAMMER - TOOLS & UI (Nintendo DS) in the subject line of your email.

Provide your resume, at least two work references, and any additional materials, and your rates and availability.
