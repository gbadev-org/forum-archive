I am a massive portal fan.
I have had a GameBoy Advance for about one year now, and have been wishing for portal on it for ages, so when I saw this site, I thought it would be perfect.

I instantly started planning a portal game and found this picture : http://senior-x.deviantart.com/art/Portal-meets-the-GameBoy-325847901, but realised I had to have experience in coding which I had never done before. This why I posted this thread in hope that someone could get inspiration and make this game.

I may be able to do texturing (I may not have enough time, and am not a very good artist), but I really don't think I can do anything else that would help.

Here is the layout for the controls.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Left button: pick up object or push button. Right button: crouch.


Up: move North. A: shoot blue portal.

Left: move West. Right: move East.

Down: move South. B: shoot orange portal.


Select: selects things in sub-menu. Start: opens sub-menu.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The controls for the menu are scrolling (up and down), A (select) and B (back)

Thanks for reading this thread, and I hope this gets made.

#177953 - gauauu - Tue May 28, 2013 2:45 pm

Cute picture.

But just so you know, it's REALLY unlikely that anyone would run with your idea -- most GBA developers have their own projects they are working on, so unless you had something really significant to offer (ie a complete set of graphics and sound assets that you made for the game), people have no incentive to stop working on their own project and work on yours.

If you want to make it happen, learn to code :) I'm not saying that to sound rude, but as an encouragement: learning to code is fun.

Good luck.
