Just a little tip: gameplay is propbably more important to think about at this point than the story. Figure out the main game mechanics...that tells us and your players a whole lot more about your game (and whether your game will be interesting) than the story.

If anyone had told me the story for Yars Revenge before I played it, I would have rolled my eyes and told them to go away. But the game mechanic made it a favorite.

Swordquest: Earthworld (also for Atari 2600) had an amazing story, but was probably the worst video game I have ever played.
