Help Wanted > Nuclear Power: Take Control

#95688 - Iteo - Sun Jul 30, 2006 7:49 pm

It is the middle of World War 3 and all countries are fighting alone. No one is safe now that nuclear power i being used. In this epic battle with an overhead 3-d view, you control the troops(tanks, soldiers, and more) with you own commander voice. The troops will respond to various commands involving movement, placement of bombs, and stategy. If needed, you can use the touchscreen to change the view or to also control the game. When you win the war, you think everything is over and youreturn home to your family. But 3 years later, there are asassins looking for revenge on YOU. You have to get away and you have to race in a car through traffic, fly a plane over uncharted waters, and control a submerine to the depths of the sea in order to preserve your life, but more importantly you family's life. You have choices to make. They might lead you to peace....or destruction. It is up to you to decide....
