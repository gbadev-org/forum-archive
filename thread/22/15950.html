#161905 - tepples - Mon Aug 18, 2008 5:57 am

You'll need a DS and a Motion Card/Pak for testing, even if you just want to make sure that the motions you are programming are actually doable. Once you've bought one, the following are educated extrapolations based on what I've read about the Wii Remote accelerometers:

Noda wrote:
- how are scaled the motion_read_x/y/z and motion_acceleration function? (what is their value range)

Hold the DS still, such as by placing it on a table. The read value corresponds to 9.8 m/s^2.

Quote:
- how are mapped the motion axis? (is the Y axis up/down or is it the Z one)?

Up/down is whichever one is large when the DS sits still on a table.
