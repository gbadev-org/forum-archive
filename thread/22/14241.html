#141767 - KeithE - Sat Sep 29, 2007 4:46 pm

The DS Motion Pak will fit in the GBA slot of the DS. It also comes with an extra case that is sized to fit flush in the GBA slot of the DS lite.

There are two case colors for the DS Motion Pak - white and black. Most of the lite cases are white, and all of the large size cases are black.
