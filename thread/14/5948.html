#44858 - unrequited - Mon Jun 06, 2005 3:02 am

Here's my easy 2-line serve batch file.

Edit a file called serve.bat in your wmb directory. In it type:

serve.bat wrote:

wmb -data electroplankton_demo_e3_2005.nds;nDoS.nds;subPixel.nds
serve.bat


Then just doubleclick serve.bat. It'll keep reserving after a successful upload until you close the window. (change the names of your .nds files as you want and make sure they're in the data directory of course). Awesomeness subpixel is in there if you look carefully heh.
