#36601 - tepples - Fri Feb 25, 2005 6:25 pm

Link cable can transfer up to 115200 bps half duplex with "multiplayer" framing, 115200 bps full duplex with UART framing, or 262144 bps with "normal mode" framing.

The CowBite spec and GBATEK should help you determine the capabilities of the hardware.
