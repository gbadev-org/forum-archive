#20247 - poslundc - Wed May 05, 2004 4:27 pm

Your macro is being translated into code as follows:

Code:
(u16*)0x6000000[i++] = 31;


... which is illegal. To fix it, put parentheses around your #define:

Code:
#define      VideoBuffer   ((u16 *)0x6000000)


... and it should work.
