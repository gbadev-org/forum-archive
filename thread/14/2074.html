#10768 - tepples - Mon Sep 15, 2003 3:31 am

No, flashing compressed games does not work. Zipped games would have to be decompressed to some sort of volatile memory, and the carts don't have that much RAM.

Yes, flashing compressed .mb programs could be made to work. A .mb program is smaller than 250 KB and runs from the RAM built into the GBA. I just might write an upgrade to my Multiboot Menu program that allows use of GBA-LZ77 compressed programs, once I've finished a project for ddrei.com.
