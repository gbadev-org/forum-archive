#12401 - tepples - Tue Nov 11, 2003 12:18 pm

With the MBV2 cable
Some people have reported successes programming F2A 256 Mbit carts (but not the smaller carts) with the MBV2 cable.

With the F2A linker
If you buy an F2A cart, it'll usually come bundled with a linker similar to MBV2 except that it plugs into USB. The most recent F2A host program can transfer any .mb program to the GBA as well; you may not even need the MBV2 cable at all.
