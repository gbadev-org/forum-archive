#106214 - sgeos - Mon Oct 16, 2006 8:47 pm

If you want to force it to have 3 digits, use
Code:
#define BUFFER_MAX 20
char buffer[BUFFER_MAX];
int value = 2;
sprint(buffer, "%03d", value);
This will give you "002".
