Tip #1: You can end some DOS programs by pressing Ctrl+C.

Tip #2: Major label PCs that came preinstalled with Windows XP are new enough that the manufacturer cared more about USB than the "legacy" ports, and as such, they tend to have substandard parallel ports. A USB cable might work better.

Tip #3: You can take your multiboot programs on the road with a GBA Movie Player CF.
