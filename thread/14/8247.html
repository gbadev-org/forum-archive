Honey and Mumford (1995) says that activists will learn best when they are given new experiences to learn from, and when there is a range of diverse activities to tackle.

Habeshaw T. Gibbs G. (1989) states that, ?simply giving students information by telling them, or asking them to read, will have no impact on their understanding unless they can have sport with this information?. It later goes on to say, ?Also, the meaning which students construct will be unique. This is because their existing understanding of the world, which they are using to make sense of new ideas, is itself quite unique.?
