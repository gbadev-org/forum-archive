#135839 - keldon - Thu Jul 26, 2007 7:39 pm

The GBA SDK is not capable of creating GB Roms, it only targets the GBA! The GBDK is capable of creating real GB Roms.

Autoexec.bat (does not usually have run) is what DOS and Windows9x runs when booting up. It's probably just changes in the path, which you do differently in Windows XP.

You will probably find a whole load of broken links, for example all of the IDE's are no longer on-line. I used to have a backup CD with lots of GB development tools but I think I threw them away by accident.
