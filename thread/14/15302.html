By "devkitPro" the first time you meant Updater, right?

You need to install devkitARM, MSYS, libnds, libfat, Programmer's Notepad, and libnds examples. Then you can open pnproj files.

To make a new project, copy the C:\devkitPro\examples\nds\templates\arm9 folder somewhere on your computer's hard drive (but not with space characters in the path). Then rename the folder and open "template.pnproj".
