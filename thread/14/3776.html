Can somebody tell me how do I change the syntax colors in VisualHAM and if it's possible to change the white background too?

Thanks!
cochese

#24010 - Krakken - Mon Jul 26, 2004 1:46 pm

Open the program.
In the top menu go to "Advanced -> Options -> Syntax Highlighter".
In the new window double click the line where it starts with ".c".

This is the syntax highlighter editor. To change the background colour go to the root label in the left hand box and on the far right under "Default" change the background colour to whatever.
