I think someone is skipping the basics of programming. You don't run before you can crawl and you don't do GBA programming before you know how to make a game.

Not to sound rude, but this really isn't the forum for that kind of stuff. We handle more of the technical side of the GBA: vblank, hblank, DMA, pixel routines, etc..

This is your best bet: www.gametutorials.com
