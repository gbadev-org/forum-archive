when i bought my xg-flash set on the cart there were two movie trailers. how do you put movie files on the xg-flash cart

#8278 - tepples - Sun Jul 06, 2003 8:16 pm

VirtualDub can turn most movie clips into uncompressed AVIs, but you'll need a GBA-specific codec to turn your uncompressed AVI file into a GBA ROM. The GBA codecs currently available are not consumer-priced; try $2000. The common PC codecs such as DivX run way too slow on GBA hardware.
