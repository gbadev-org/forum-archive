#154349 - josath - Tue Apr 15, 2008 1:24 am

try octopart.com and findchips.com. also the 7400 series ICs are usually in the form like 74xx123. eg I used some 74HC595's before. the letters are different versions like high speed or low power or something i think. Also, try searching for the name of the chip, perhaps you have the wrong part number (eg "3 to 8 demultiplexer" or "serial in parallel out shift register" or whatever)
