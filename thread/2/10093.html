I've got a suggestion:

I think t'admin should consider creating a forum purely for long threads that deal with support for specific applications. There's the Moonshell and ScummVM DS threads stickied in the DS Development forum for example. Maybe the subforum can be open for replies, so people can ask questions on the application, but creating new threads is disallowed, and threads can only be moved there from one of the other DS subcategories.
