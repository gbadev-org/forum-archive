What is the magical contribution phrase used to place something in the public domain? I know I've read it, probably in the creative commons license. I've tried google for a little too long, so I've decided to cut my losses and just ask. =)

-Brendan

#80638 - keldon - Mon Apr 24, 2006 9:23 am

# "this document is in the public domain"
# "this page is in the public domain"
# "this information is in the public domain"
# "this text is in the public domain"
# "placed in the public domain"

Also any copyrighted material that is handed over to the governments ownership becomes public domain in US and UK law. Check out http://www.ethicalpublicdomain.org/
