dagamer34 wrote:
Search for Meteo. That's all I know.


Meteo is only good for short 2 to 3 min videos. At 1/2 hour or longer, it complains about a bad input file after the encode is done and it will not be playable. Also, if your going to use Meteo, don't let it do any scalling or cropping of the video and don't let it do any audio resampling unless you want to destroy the audio.
