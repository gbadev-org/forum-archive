#5243 - joemmac - Wed Apr 23, 2003 7:05 pm

Yes, you can use the multi-boot method, but then you're restricted to the internal RAM. A flash cartridge is required to play any game/program that requires more space (ie. comercial games).

I am in the process of designing something similar to the GameWallet that wouldn't need a flash cartridge. It would allow users to play games/programs up to 32MB from a SmartMedia card. There's a post in the Hardware section ("SmartMedia to GBA adapter") with more information.
