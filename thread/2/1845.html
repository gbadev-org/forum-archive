#9530 - Saj - Thu Aug 07, 2003 7:18 pm

Is it stuck in one position or just won't work?

If it's stuck, the best thing to clean it with is a slightly damp (not wet!) cloth.

If it presses but does nothing you can just leave it (that button isn't used much anyway) or open your snes and simply solder the wire back onto it (it only has 2 wires so a 10-year old could handle this most basic solder-job)

you could also stick the wire back on with a bit of tape if you're not feeling brave...

Also you might need a security screwdriver to open your snes/super famicom. Just do a search on google.com for a supplier.

Hope this helps...
