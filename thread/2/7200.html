#57727 - tepples - Tue Oct 18, 2005 12:41 am

Do you know anything about soldering? A good first hardware hack would be an adapter from the "standard" 1/8" 3.5mm miniplug to the DS's rectangular microphone jack. A software hack on top of that would be some sort of soft-modem that can convert data to audio and then back to data. Practical applications for a modem might include the ability to use other input devices with the DS.
