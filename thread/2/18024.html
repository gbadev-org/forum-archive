Yo. I made this thread to share my custom borders for blargSNES. For those that don't know, blargSNES is an SNES emulator for homebrew-enabled Nintendo 3DS handhelds. A custom border is essentially a wallpaper that is visible on the top screen of the Nintendo 3DS when using the emulator, if you have the scaling set in 1:1. So far, you see this wallpaper during ROM selection right when you boot the emulator, and during the actual playtime.

Also note that the custom border that you decide to use must be placed in the root of the SD Card on the Nintendo 3DS and must be named "blargSNESBorder.bmp". You can view the custom border before downloading it, just click on "Download from Dropbox" to see a low-quality preview. On the bottom right corner there are three dots, if you click on the dots and select "View Original" you can see the border in its original quality.
