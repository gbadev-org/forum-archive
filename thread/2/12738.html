You guys are missing the point. He doesn't want to analyze his network traffic. Free or not. If he knew how to do that, he wouldn't have posted the question..

Bottom line, without having some type of logging software running on your router to keep track, your not going to get what you want. Even with sniffer software, without knowing the network configuration, it's hard to say he will get the results he is looking for. If someone is "borrowing" his network using WiFi a wired sniffer isn't going to see it. If he wants to monitor wired, he'll need a hub to be able to see all the traffic. If he want's to monitor both, he'll need a hub between WiFi, wired, and the router. And the router can't accept any traffic except from the WiFi and Wired network through the hub.
