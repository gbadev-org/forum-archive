#108930 - josath - Mon Nov 13, 2006 7:26 am

generally, it's more along the lines of:
1) give them your info
2) complete 1-3 'offers' (like signing up for credit cards, joining various memberships like netflix, or aol, etc)
3) get 2-7 of your friends to also complete 1 & 2.

The middleman (omgfreestuff.com) gets money for each 'offer' they complete, and they make enough money that they can buy you a ds lite (ipod, tv, etc), and still make a tidy profit.
