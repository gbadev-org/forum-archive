#77790 - Bojangles - Mon Apr 03, 2006 12:33 am

It's next to impossible to get a homebrew game published on a console. Publishers have that racket all tied up. Your best bet is to create an awesome homebrew game, and try to get a job at an already established developer. Or, start a company, and try to get a publisher to give you work (license games for cell phones etc).

A publisher doesn't just publish "cool games." They have to fit all their other requirements for a game that they would normally pay to ahve developed for them. The chances your game does that are slim. Sure, you can try and really sell it to them, and maybe make a go of it, but it's slim.
