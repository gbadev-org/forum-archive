It's in french so I think a little translation could help:

Dt dungeon fr is just a translation of a fantastic game made by a norvegian : j?rg Rockstroh, where the player have to find a way to escape of a labyrinthic dungeon.

Elixir is the project I am currently working on: It's rpg based on Elisource a game engine I made who allow to battle against hundred diff?rent monsters (what is quite uncommon for a calculator who have 64kb of memory) by a programming tip, make hundred different potions, walk in a large variety of landscapes ond so on. Elixir make you embody an alchemist who have to get back a potion which was robbed from your Master.

Metalwar is a strategy game based on the gameplay of Advance Wars. The only thing i didn't implement was fog of war, scrolling and move range depending of the ground type. Because of the restrictions of the calculator. The adaptation work I had to do can be find here: http://grabag.free.fr/bordel/textes/Design%20Metalwar/Document%20MWv1.00.pdf

Metalwar2 is just an improvement with gameplay fixes, code acc?l?ration, new maps and new units.

Pathfinding is as it name suggests a pathfind program made from A to Z.

Progra-pack is a compilation of 7 programs who make the life of the Casio programmer easyer (a list browser, a picture viewer, a standard program starter, etc.)

Quadra-compress is an image compressor who break up a picture into rectangles (a solo pixel is a rectangle with dimension 1x1, a line of 10 pixels is a rectangle of dimensions 1x10, and so on) and store their coordinates in a matrice. Sometime it allow to gain really a lot of space, sometime not (with very morcelled pictures especially)

Reflex is a little minigame i made in my first year of programming. A number appear on the screen and the player have to press the same number on the keyboard as fastly as he can
