I've personally gotten hardware and related replacement cables through Jandaman's Import Videogame Accessories at jandaman.com. They've got a selection of different flash carts (most sites stick to one like glue) they ship anywhere, and stocks replacement EZFA cables when nobody else does!

99% of carts come with the hardware and software (some prefer to offer an online download for the most up to date software) required to flash your ROMs onto the hardware. Be careful though, from my experiences, cables and software aren't compatible between cards. Choose your card carefully, my son! o.o

Failing that, you could try your luck on the old e-to-the-Bay.
