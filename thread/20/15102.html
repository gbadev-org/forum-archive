#151709 - tepples - Mon Mar 03, 2008 8:55 pm

Holding Select skips FlashMe's SLOT-2 booting. Holding Start forces the firmware menu to show up if the SLOT-1 card's autoboot flag isn't set.

DS Lite, FlashMe v7, insert R4 card, hold buttons, power on DS
Result (Select): It goes straight to the R4 menu.
Result (Start): It goes straight to the R4 menu.
Result (Select+Start): It goes straight to the R4 menu.
