DS Misc > Connect DS to laptop

#136042 - killers8 - Sat Jul 28, 2007 7:15 pm

Is it possible to connect a DS directly to a wireless laptop and transfer files from the laptop to the DS without cables.

#136051 - dantheman - Sat Jul 28, 2007 7:54 pm

As laptops are unlikely to have the wireless cards needed for WMB, your main options are DSFTP and Lilou FTP. There's also a few wifiloaders that load games into RAM before execution without writing to the card. You could also use HTTPexec in a pinch if you rename files often.

#136192 - Dood77 - Mon Jul 30, 2007 12:05 am

The programs that dantheman is suggesting are all run through a wireless router, not directly between your DS<->PC. This means you will most likely need to own the wireless router, e.g. not be using free wifi at a cafe.
