#97241 - tepples - Tue Aug 08, 2006 8:59 pm

MaHe wrote:
But since MMP/MMD are just ripoff's of GBAmp

No it isn't. It's an ATA adapter, and it just looks similar to the GBAMP v2 CF at some level because all ATA adapters would look that same at that level. The GBAMP v2 CF has a 512 KiB firmware chip; MMP/MMD doesn't.

Quote:
, if we had the proper registers, I think modifying chishm's GBAmp firmware wouldn't be so hard ... would it?

Yes it would. The MMP/MMD doesn't have built-in firmware. It boots from the included MAX Media Launcher. Of course, you could boot custom firmware from a traditional GBA flash cart and then pak-swap to the MMP, but that defeats the purpose, right?
