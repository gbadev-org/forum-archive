#129462 - OrR - Wed May 23, 2007 1:00 am

Is there piece of DS homebrew software to help you learn words in other languages? I haven't found anything... It should not be to difficult to make and I'd love to see someone do it! It would be great if it could take file input in a simple format, for example something like this:
Quote:

title=Lesson 1
apple/Apfel
tree/Baum
green/gr?n

There should be a choice in which direction the program should check your knowledge (German-->English or English-->German). There could be different modes: One where you have to input the correct translation with an on screen keyboard, another one where you can reveal the correct answer with a button press. The first mode could keep scores and in a more elaborate version even measure your performance over the course on a few days and remind you to repeat certain stuff after a while. Overall it should be functional and flexible. Support for Japanese letters would be cool because that is a language many people in the gaming community want to learn.
Anyone up to the challenge? I want to motivate my little brother to learn Latin for school. ;)
