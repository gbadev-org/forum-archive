#131676 - dantheman - Tue Jun 19, 2007 12:47 am

I backed up my firmware with DSLinux and followed chuckstudios's instructions and was able to access the firmware (though it didn't launch the .nds file I originally double-clicked on). Perhaps you need the DS BIOS files as well, or maybe the GBA BIOS must also be present (I have all three BIOS files in the folder). I don't really know why it isn't working for you.

EDIT: it appears that you must have BIOSNDS7.ROM and BIOSNDS9.ROM in the no$GBA directory for the firmware to launch correctly, based on my limited testing.
