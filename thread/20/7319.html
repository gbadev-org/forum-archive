#59055 - Smiley - Fri Oct 28, 2005 1:32 pm

I have disassembled 2 DS already to make some repairs and I must say that the bottom part is pretty easy to take apart if you have a tri-wing screwdriver. Top is a little harder because the screws are hidden under the little pads that surround the top screen. They are not easy to remove without breaking them, and that's what happened when I tried. I would try heating them a little to loosen the glue first if I had to try again.

And Mr. Pickesworth, I hate breaking your bubble, but there is no cover on the bottom screen. You have the LCD display and the touch screen glue on top of it. There is nothing more, no cover. You are directly on the touch screen when you play, so don't do something stupid, cause you won't be able to change it that easily :)
