#68591 - Joat - Wed Jan 25, 2006 10:55 pm

NiFi, simply put, doesn't exist (the WMB protocol isn't the same as the pictochat protocol or the in-game comms of the pre-WFC games, they're all raw comms on top of wifi). WMB and pictochat share similarities in their beacon advertisment design, but more because they were written by the same people, rather than because they use the same protocol.

WifiMe *is* wifi, since WMB (and every other piece of comms out there using the DS wireless) adheres (tenuously) to the 802.11 standard, trademarked Wifi.

Wifi just means layers 1 and 2, it doesn't say shit about TCP/IP, etc...
