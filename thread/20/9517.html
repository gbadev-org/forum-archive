It's the M3 Mini.. look down further on the line, it lists the M3 Slim as well... they discontinued the old M3 like the one I have, so when it just lists the M3, it's for the CF model... however, firmware and softwarewise, the M3 Mini SD, M3 Slim SD and the M3SD Perfect are all identical.

you shouldn't have anything referencing EXFS, either... ensure that the first option you select, when the smaller dialog box is brought up, is the drive your SD card is in.
