Sounds like some kind of a problem with the filesystem on your SD card. I assume you are using something on your PC to write to the card? One thing you could try to make certain all the file is being written is to (with recentish XP versions anyway) right click on the little icon that appears in the task bar with the green arrow on it. Should appear when you insert the SD reader into your computer (assuming it's USB and it probably is). Then choose 'Safely Remove Hardware' and let the computer remove it, then take it out of the reader.

I don't think it'd be MoonShell screwing it up as MoonShell isn't writing anything to the SD card so it shouldn't be messing with the filesystem. Do you have another SD card or card reader you could try?
