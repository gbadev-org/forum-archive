strange cause there wasn't a nesds.bin file when I downloaded the emu.
but that's not your prob.
do not choose any nes file at all, just press "save". a nesds.gba.ds file should appear.
Now start "Build.bat"(make sure you have downloaded "ndstool.exe" first and put it in the same dir) and it should build you a clean .nds-file. Rename it to "_boot_mp.nds" and put it (with the roms of your choise) on your CF card.
Boot the your DS(you have PassME or FlashME...haven't you) and a rom-selection menu should pop up on the lower screen.Choose game and press A to boot game.
