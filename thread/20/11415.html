#105800 - tepples - Wed Oct 11, 2006 11:29 pm

Yes it could be done, if someone had the time. However, because the DS doesn't have enough RAM to hold all the key sounds at once, the key sound system would need to either be rewritten to use ADPCM compressed samples, use samples played back at multiple pitches, load samples from the file system into RAM during playback, or all three. The MP3 background music might need to be replaced with something less demanding as well, such as MP2 or GSM or even just ADPCM.

But what about 3-key times 6-player, where the Control Pad switches players, and once you play two measures of one player's part with no misses, bads, or poors, it sets the next 11 measures of that part to autoplay? Imagine a drum track, a bass track, a guitar track, a piano or synth track, a voice track, and a sixth track of some sort, with just a repeating hi-hat in the background music.
