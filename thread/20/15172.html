I'm using No$Gba DS emulator since it's the most accurate I tried. I now want to take few screenshots of my game, but the game is less colorful in this emulator than in any others.

Options > Emulation Setup > GBA Mode, change it from GBA, GBA SP, or DS to "VGA poppy bright", which more closely resembles how a DS Lite or Game Boy Player displays things.
