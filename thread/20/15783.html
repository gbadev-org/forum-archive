#159956 - 11X_daemon_X11 - Tue Jul 08, 2008 9:04 pm

I have no experience with this card, but it sounds like you need to scan for bad sectors and stuff like that. Just put the card in your computer, right click on it and goto properties. Then, goto the Tools tab and click Check Now and check both boxes and start the scan. After this, you'll end up with a bunch of deleted files (in a found.000 folder) that you can probably delete. Now, after this, reformat your card, and then put stuff back on. Thats how I got my GnM card to start working after gettin No Media Device found, which is probably the same equivalent to your error on your card.
