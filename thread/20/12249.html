Howdy there DS Devrs :)

I've set up a Nintendo DS Developers Group on the Great Games Experiment.

Go and sign up for the GGE and join our group: NDS Developers

Info on GGE:
The Great Games Experiment is a mySpace type affair but for gamers and game developers and game publishers to hang out at. It is currently in Beta, but anyone can request to sign up.

The site is split into sections home, games, groups, developers and publishers. Each section is pretty self-explanatory and I found it very simple to use.

Once in there, you can join any group, rate other users, games, groups, and even chat.
Developers and Gamers alike can show their stuff while being members of some of the most active groups around.

Hope to see you all over there ;)
