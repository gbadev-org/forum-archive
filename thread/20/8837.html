You know where those eBay people get these replacement parts? They disassemble brand new DSes (and the occasional used one, probably) and sell them for parts.

Notice how the most thought-after parts are the most expensive? Smart, eh?

The sum of all parts is greater than what they pay for the DS, since they naturally want to make some profit. I can see how you might combine cheapest parts from different "businessmen" to try building a DS cheaper than buying a new DS, but shipping costs really kill that idea.

Additionally, I don't think "the man" likes them doing that stuff. Go figure :)
