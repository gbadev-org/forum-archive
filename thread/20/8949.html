To activate the clock plugin, replace your MoonShell NDS file with the _BOOT_MP.nds, place the BIN, INI and MSP files of your clock skin of choice in your shell/plugins/ dir, and add the following lines to your shell/global.ini:
Quote:
[ClockPlugin]

Enabled=1

; --------------------
