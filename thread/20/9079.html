-- Crash on Luigis Mansion(contributed by dualscreenman, unknown origins)
Get on the stairway in Luigis Mansion press A+B there. when you now press left or right, itll crash(for everyone, if you play mp)



-- M:PH
-- Highjump(by thunderstrike (only))
1. Equip the Magmaul (be sure your not Spire, I'll tell you later).
2. Look straight down.
3. Charge it up until it shakes. (fully charged)
4. Release, then you should shoot in the air up a bit
5. At the top of this, double touch the screen to jump AGAIN
6. Find a cool hard-to-reach ledge to get on top of!
This should get you to jump about double height!
And you CAN be Spire if you're willing to let the Magmaul's affinity effect damage yourself.

-- Fly(by swimgod)
to make sylux float (well fly, since he doesn't have to come down, lol)
1)pick sylux
2)play a level with "outer space"{IE: headshot}
3) fall off the edge and release 3 bombs FAST
4) countinue to release bombs,
and you will stay up there...
ONLY sylux can fly from this,
only he can make ALL his bombs explode from releasing 3.



-- Super Mario 64 DS
-- 149 stars bug
some people can only get 149 stars, because the star in the castle courtyard with the 8 red coins disappears after a while, so get the f*****g star as fast as you can!

#79415 - shaz - Thu Apr 13, 2006 2:41 pm

How to get on top of the castle with just mario, luigi or wario:

You can't do this with Yoshi.
Go where the rocket is outside the castle.
Look directly straight of the sign next to the rocket and on the left of the sign is a tree.
Go back near the water (not in the water!)
Now run towards the tree and jump and press A so you slide in the air and onto the green area near the tree.
When sliding on the grass, make sure u try to slide onto the tree.
Once your hanging, hold down and you'll be in water (Yes!, underneath the grass!)
You can swim underneath places, go underneath the castle, get stuck in the wario lvl in the courtyard, swim out of the mushroom kingdom and many more.
To go on top of the castle, once underneath the rocket, jump and you'll be boarded on the rocket.

This will likely not work on the first attempt.
