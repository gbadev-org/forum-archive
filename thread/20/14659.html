since i took some time to create some icons for iconless apps and games, i thought it wouldn't hurt to share them out.

i hosted them here
http://orrinw.007ihost.com/

most are completely original, drawn in photoshop.

there were a few existing homebrew icons which I modified to suit my taste better;
-DS2Win: had 2 odd transparent pixels in the middle, which i don't believe were intentional. also moved one pixel down.
-DSLinux: whitish surrounding pixels didn't look very good on darker backgrounds
-Colors: same whitish surrounding pixels, and also general minor modifications.

also a couple used images from google search, haven't really provided credit there...

generally magenta or green is what i used to indicate the transparent pixels, but in a few cases white or black -_- whoopsies for not being consistent.

lov'em or hat'em, i'm using them.
