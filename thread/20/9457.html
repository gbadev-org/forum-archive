#82026 - ethoscapade - Wed May 03, 2006 4:49 pm

it absolutely is, just don't use the most recent (supercard SD) release. that one dropped support for the superdat, anyway, and you kind of really need the superdat nine times out of ten.

compile it as you would for a flashcart, producing a .ds.gba binary, then rename (DON'T convert) it to .nds.
