#142877 - calcprogrammer1 - Sun Oct 14, 2007 8:25 pm

Without any flash equipment, no. Unless your computer has a Ralink chipset (which is unlikely, most WiFi cards use Broadcom these days), you need to buy some flash equipment. With Ralink cards you can use WiFiMe.

For flash equipment, I recommend the Datel Games n' Music, which is a cheap device that is available in stores (Best Buy, Wal Mart, etc) for like $20 to $35. They aren't the greatest quality cards out there (slow microSD port, slots tend to break, etc) but they include a USB reader and microSD card (128MB) in one package.
