Alrite...

What you need to do is convert those VOB and DivX files. DivX files are coming out with no sound because the sound format has to be Mp2. I use a converter called Xilisoft Video converter to convert my video files to .wmv although I did need to change the audio format to mp2. Moonshell DPGenc can encode video files that are Mpeg, avi or wmv although they need to have mp2 audio or lower.
