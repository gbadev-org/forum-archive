#94608 - JimmyL - Tue Jul 25, 2006 2:25 pm

I would play online, but I lend my ds to a friend, and returned the other ds I was using for testing to another friend. :)

A Touch of War is a little more complicated to setup a game, since the host player needs to be online sitting in his 'lobby' waiting for a player to join since there's no join in progress, and odds are no one is sitting around with a ds turned on running atow refreshing the server list waiting for a game to be hosted. The best bet would be to coordinate a game with a friend, ask someone in IRC, or post a day/time you're going to run a server in the chat room on my site, and hope someone sees your message and joins your game. And, of course, you need to be forwarding port 9999 to your ds if you want to host.

With Explosive Gas, Lynx has been making life a bit easier by running a server almost constantly, that way people that want to play don't need to worry about port forwarding, and they can at least constantly kill lynx's zombie player while they wait for new players to join.

And ya, I've never played explosive gas with more than 5 players, I'm curious to see if it works with 8 :) Let's set up "Homebrew Wednesday's" and all get together to play some of these games at, say, 7pm EST every week... or at least this week...
