#127542 - dantheman - Thu May 03, 2007 4:13 am

Supercards and the SD cards they use can be picky. Try patching the .nds file with SCSD.dldi first, and if that fails, retry SCLT.dldi, and if that fails use SCSD_moon.dldi

I've got a miniSD card that only works with SCSD_moon.dldi while the other works with both SCSD_moon and the regular SCSD one.
