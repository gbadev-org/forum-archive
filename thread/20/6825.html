#54950 - tepples - Fri Sep 23, 2005 7:58 pm

A regular flash cart can do it; someone just has to write a loader capable of bankswitching the card to the beginning of a .gba or ds.gba file. Cards that use the Visoly protocol, such as F2A, are likely the first to be supported, as the Visoly protocol is reasonably well documented such as in the PogoShell source code.
