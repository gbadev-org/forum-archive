Actually the power led is two colour.

There are three leds on the board.

Orange (Charger) Led
Red (Low Battery) Led
Green (Power) Led.

But the green can change to orange too. Touch the pins at the bottem of the Power Management Chip. One of the pins will make the led change colour. Some pins do different things, even makes the ds restart etc.
