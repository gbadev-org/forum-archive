#67137 - DsPet - Sun Jan 15, 2006 8:35 pm

> I have a SuperCard SD and FlashMe V6
As mentioned, RAIN will work for both BACKUP and RESTORE (I've used it with a SuperCard+CF)
Some people have had problems with older versions. Get version 9.
BTW: you must remove the PassMe before inserting the ACWW DS cartridge directly in the unit for reading/writing.

The save files should be exactly 256KB (262,144 bytes). They can be loaded into my Map editor (http://www.aibohack.com/nds/ac_mapedit.htm) or Thulinma's pattern editor (http://www.xs4all.nl/~aviator/acww )
If the MapEditor fails to load the file, it may be incomplete (don't restore it to the ACWW cartridge or you may lose your town)
Once you get a good backup, save an extra copy on your PC.

As mentioned other hardware devices should work with RAIN, but each one is different
