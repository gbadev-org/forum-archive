sakura's firmware isn't 90 MB, that's the trippleboot firmware. each of the 3 firmwares is ~30MB each, thus equating to around 90..

the trippleboot is a fatty firmware, though, altogether. it still takes up almost no room on an 8 gig card, leaving a plethora of space for movies and comics and all your other larger files. (those two file types seem to be the bulk of my consumed space)

once sakura is a little better off with it's homebrew compatibility, using the trippleboot won't be necessary, whereupon it'll all be slimmed down.

itouch firmware is boring and unintuitive. it's like a regurgitated version of m3 real.

m3 real firmware can suck it. bleh..
