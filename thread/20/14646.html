#146784 - tepples - Sun Dec 09, 2007 2:17 am

Precedent on forum.gbadev.org, as I understand it, is as follows:
  • You MAY discuss copying commercial DS games' Download Station demos (which are smaller than 4 MB) to SD cards.
  • You MAY discuss copying commercial games' saved data (e.g. your town in Animal Crossing: Wild World) to and from SD cards.
  • You MUST NOT discuss copying entire commercial DS games (which are usually 8 MB or larger) to SD cards. This is called "warez talk" and is forbidden even on September 19. Even if such copying does not violate local law, it's off-topic on forum.gbadev.org.

(The key words "MAY" and "MUST NOT" are defined in RFC 2119.)
