#147260 - TJ - Mon Dec 17, 2007 12:14 pm

The dswifi test application has basic support for capturing packets (well, displaying them at least) and some other interesting functions.

But the principle problem is that the DS's hardware is limited to 2 Mbps, so unless you have authenticated to the AP (and thus forced it down to 2 Mbps), the data will be going by too fast to accurately capture.
