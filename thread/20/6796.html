#53511 - cybereality - Thu Sep 08, 2005 10:38 pm

I think its a good idea to split off the forum sections, but just adding a DS misc section doesn't seem to fit some of the posts. My suggestion is to map the sections out like this:

DS Development: For posts about actual DS development, coding questions, dev environment setup, etc.

DS Hardware: For posts about Flash equipment, PassMe, FlashMe, new products like M3, G6, etc.

DS Software: For homebrew releases, downloads, questions/suggestion about existing software, ideas for new games/apps.

DS General Chat: For posts that dont belong anywhere else. This would include posts like "Should I buy a DS?", general DS/Nintendo news, discussion of commercial games ("Metroid Hunters is the Shite!!!"), and other chat topics.

Some of the topics moved to this section dont exactly belong here either and it seems like it may cause some confusing to new users. The 4 sections I outlined above would solve both problems and be a good solutuion for the time being.
