#159756 - jackman - Sun Jul 06, 2008 10:20 am

POP3 Server: pop.googlemail.com

POP3 Port: 995

POP3 User: <Your-GoogleMail-Email-Address>

SSL: POP3 needs SSL login, so we set this option to yes/enabled

POP3 Password: <Your-GoogleMail-Password>

SMTP Server: smtp.googlemail.com

SMTP Port: either 465 or 587 (try one of them)

Email Address: <Your-GoogleMail-Email-Adress>

Display Name: The title of this Account displayed in NDSmail

Hope this helps.
