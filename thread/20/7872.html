#63591 - mymateo - Wed Dec 14, 2005 7:28 pm

You must know the right place to fish, or not know about the magic rock. Every day, one rock in your town will give you money when you hit it.

(The following is information for just anybody, but also in case you didn't know). The shovel is your friend. When you hit the rock with your shovel, you will get knocked back a little. So place a hole behind you. Place another hold beside you and run up against it so when you get hit, you will be pushed towards that hole. Ultimately, you will not move enough to have to re-position yourself. Se diagram below.

() = hole
. = you (money also appears here)
(_) = rock
$ = where money will appear (eventually)

Code:

   ()
  $ . ()
  $(_)$
  $ $ $


You only have so much time to get money once you hit the rock. The most I've been able to get from a rock is 7 bags. The more bags you get, the more money will be in each bag. Starting with the first bag, you will get 100, then 200, then 400, 800, 1000, 2000, and 4000. So in total, you can get 8,500 bells each day. This is why I time travel. It takes a minute to start my game, anywhere from 10 seconds to 3 minutes to find the right tock, another 45 seconds to hit it and grab the money, and another minute to save and change the date on my DS. So in total, I average about 8500 bells in under 6 minutes, sometimes 4 minutes.

Bewarned, though, because if you don't hit the rock fast enough you won't get that 4000 bell bag, and that one counts for a lot.

So KoD, if you already knew about the magic rock, I beg you to tell me where and when you fish 'cause I'm always on the lookout for a faster way to make some cash. I liked the GC version w/ Animal Island. I use a duplicating trick, and I tell you that duplicating 25 bags with 30,000 bells in each sure adds up fast!
