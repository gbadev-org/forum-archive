#154742 - dantheman - Mon Apr 21, 2008 4:14 am

Two major points here:

1. SylphAmp is fairly old, and like simon suggested, the wifi library it uses may not support your new DS (Lite). The fact that DSO can connect but not this makes me suspect this may be the case. A better app to try would be DSHobro, as it too has the whole "DS binary connects to server on PC" thing going on, which could help you determine if it's possibly your firewall or router causing the issue rather than the wifi library.

2. Try v0.03, not the latest v0.04. I tried as hard as I could get get v0.04 working on my network and it kept freezing on me, so I went back to v0.03 and it worked fine.
