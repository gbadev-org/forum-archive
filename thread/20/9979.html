clone dad wrote:
I hear people saying the DS lite is better than the DS. tell me: is it really better? is it more comfortable to hold?

Signs point to yes, but on tetrisconcept.com they're saying that its more sensitive +Control Pad causes too many accidental presses of Up.

Quote:
now, i hear that i can't flash a ds lite because it will brick it.

As long as your SL1 shorting tool doesn't touch the side of the SL1 hole closest to the GBA slot, no bricking will happen.

Quote:
If i get a Max Media Launcher, will i not have to flash my DS?

You can use MAX Media Launcher (or any other NoPass) to boot DS homebrew from the GBA slot. See the NoPass sticky in DS Flash Equipment.
