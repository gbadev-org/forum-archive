#70846 - MaHe - Thu Feb 09, 2006 1:55 am

Download the LATEST version (GBAmp one doesn't seem to work). Download ndstool.exe from darkfader.net and put it in the same folder where you extracted SnesDS files. Run make_nds.bat (or something like that), when a rom manager opens, just click save. Twice :) . Copy the result (snesds.nds) to your CF card.

You can try out some games from pdroms.de now :>
