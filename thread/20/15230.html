#162004 - sonny_jim - Sat Aug 23, 2008 12:36 am

If you use Linux (or can find a windows version), you can use MPD on your PC and use ncmpc bundled with DSLinux to control it.

You can also take this one step further and output via ESD to a ESD server running on the DS, meaning you can listen and control the music all from the DS.

There's more information on the DSLinux wiki here
