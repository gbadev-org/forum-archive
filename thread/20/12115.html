The only way I think of is by installing this card into PC and looking for driver that is installed for it (there is 2 drivers on disk v.4.0 RT2500 & v.4.1 RT61).

But that`s really doesn`t matter now. Here`s a tutorial for configuring your Linksys WMP54G v.4.1 (RT61 chip) for DS:
1. Install card & drivers from CD.
2. Go to Ralink`s site -> Support -> Windows & download appropriate pack.
3. Install it, you should have Ralink Wireless Lan Card icon in your tray. Right click on it, select switch to soft AP.
4. Share your internet connection (ethernet card).

That`s all. Commercial games & homebrew should work. Good luck.
