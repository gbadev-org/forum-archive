First off, this is not a Warez related topic. I have with me, at this very moment, a Japanese copy of Opera DS Lite imported with the help of Lik-Sang. Keep that in mind with regards to what follows...


Out of pure curiosity I downloaded the Opera DS rom and stuff it on my SC: mSD. The rom itself ran through the SC converted app fine, coming out a valid size.

Unsurprisingly enough, the rom didn't actually work.

What was interesting, a a little surprising though, was the fashion in which it didn't work. Specifically, after loading the rom I recieved...

Nothing...

Nada...

Two white screens...

Yeah, that's right, not even a message in japanese to the effect of "You need the memory expansion plugged in for this to work"...

So right now I'm wondering how much of the Opera DS functionality actually resides on the DS cart and how much resides on the GBA cart.

There was some mention, somewhere, of Opera DS being upgradeable. A wild rumour? Who knows...

Anyway, does anyone have any thoughts. I've got two ideas...

1: Opera DS resides mostly on the GBA cart
2: Opera DS tried to access it's memory via the SC and had a hernia...

I'm more inclined to think 2 is the case, but if anyone else has any ideas...

I'll update later with some news on what happens when I test out the actual Opera DS carts (I only picked them up an hour ago from the PO, you se :-) )
