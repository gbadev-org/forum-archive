No, wireless is enough as long as each has a different IP.

I havent used pointy remote in a while, but If I recall correctly, the.se are the instructions:

Turn on Server.exe thingie
Open Command Prompt and type "IPconfig /all"
Grab your NDS and execute pointyremote.nds or .ds.gba
Configure your access point, set IP similar to your computers (example, if you PC is 192.168.0.1, then set your DSIP as 192.168.0.X), set the mask same as your PC's (normally its 255.255.255.0), set your gateway same as your pc's, and DNS same as provided from IPconfig /all.

Finally, click on Connect on the DS. it should connect automagically, and if not, press B or Start or A or X or Y :P
