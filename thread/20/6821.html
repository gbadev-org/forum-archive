#53715 - cybereality - Sun Sep 11, 2005 12:50 am

I ordered a NeoFlash 512Mb cart from Divineo and the service was great. It came literally 2 days later, from Canada to US. I was impressed. The NeoFlash does everything you need it to do. It is a little expensive, but everything comes in one nice package and it works as advertised. Don't bother sending your DS to get flash. Get a PassMe/MagicKey/SuperPass and flash it yourself. They can be bought for like $30. If you dont have one of those devices you will only be able to play homebrew roms. If you are really unsure of what to get, I recommend the NeoFlash Kit as it comes with everything you need (though it will set you back $160). The NeoFlash is really a re-branded XG2 cart, so I believe you can use that, but you'll still need to purchase a Magic Key from NeoFlash ($40).

Check out the info on this site: [Warez URL removed by MOD]
They have reviews of all the major development devices so you can make an informed purchase. Also, there are new devices coming out this month (G6,M3,SuperCard) so you may want to wait for the better technology to come out. I hope that helps.
