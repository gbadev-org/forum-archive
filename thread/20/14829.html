#148908 - tepples - Fri Jan 11, 2008 8:39 pm

One thing to watch out for: There are two different "Action Replay" devices that do something with DS games. The SLOT-2 "Action Replay MAX Duo" is good for backing up DS saves without needing an extra SLOT-2 card, but not for booting SLOT-2 cards in DS mode. The SLOT-1 "Action Replay DS" is a NoPass if you hold Select, as Sektor pointed out.
