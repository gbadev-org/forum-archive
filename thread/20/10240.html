#91301 - takieda - Thu Jul 06, 2006 4:21 am

Interestingly enough, there's no language lock on the pdaDS application, so you can run the chinese PDA with English firmware (and I'm assuming vice versa when the English version comes out).

I've tested it out myself and it works fine. You'll have to download the entire 40MB package and extract it to a folder on the root of your media card called "pdaDS" and then just click on pdaDS on your M3 menu. Of course, without the folder, it will give you an error message, so I can only assume that at least some of them are on your media card.
