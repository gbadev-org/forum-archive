#62624 - m2pt5 - Sat Dec 03, 2005 3:18 am

Assuming you're using a standard flashcart (not Supercard / G6 / M3):

Put FlashMe v5 on the cart. Put it in the DS, and boot it while holding A+B+Select+Start. Reflash as usual. (You'll have to short SL1 again.)

You won't have the problem again as long as you use v5.
