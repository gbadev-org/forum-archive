#87806 - tepples - Thu Jun 15, 2006 7:23 pm

You'll need a Nintendo DS, a GBAMP (CF version, not SD version), a MAX Media Launcher or other NoPass card, and a CF card. Then you can use NDSMP.gba to make your GBAMP work in DS mode, and then you can run the MoonShell installer on the CF card.

#87809 - HyperHacker - Thu Jun 15, 2006 7:33 pm

If you have firmware V1-V3 you can use PassMe or WifiMe instead of a NoPass too. If you plan to make DS apps I highly suggest WifiMe if you can, because being able to use the card to send your apps to the DS is really useful. Pass(Me/Key/etc)2 doesn't work with GBAMP though, and WifiMe/Pass*1 doesn't work on V4+, so if you have that you'll need a NoPass.
