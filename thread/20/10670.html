#96580 - MaHe - Thu Aug 03, 2006 11:46 pm

Skype uses a mix of SIP/P2P protocols and since there's no public dev. library for it yet, you can't do it. On the other hand, there are tons of similar VoIP providers, a lot of them offering free calls to most of the world (e.g. Gizmo Project: http://www.gizmoproject.com/learnmore-allcallsfree.html - it's not fake, I can call myself, it works, despite the fact that URL looks like a spamsite).
All we need is a SIP client - all minor/major VoIP providers (except Skype) are based on SIP.
