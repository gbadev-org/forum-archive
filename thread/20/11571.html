What exactly does it does? I know you can use it to transfer saves from an official DS cart to a save file on your flashcart, and viceversa. But what else does it do?

#107517 - MaHe - Mon Oct 30, 2006 1:52 pm

That's about it. A few examples:

- a game doesn't support erasing the saved data and you want to start from scratch or resell it; you can format it using REIN(Moon)
- you want to borrow the Game Card to someone else and want to keep your game data; just transfer the save file on your flashcard
- you are lazy and don't want to complete the game just to unlock something; download a completed save file and restore it to your DS Game Card

And so on and on ...

P.S.: I prefer REIN over ReinMoon. I just like the simplistic interface.
