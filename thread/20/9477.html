#82292 - Neccy78 - Fri May 05, 2006 12:22 am

Fixed it.

I followed the problem back to my archiver (powerzip or somthing its called, a compression tool that covers many archive types) Anyway even though i stored them as uncompressed, and even though scummvm finds the zip file, it wasnt adding any games...

Instead I used Winrar's Zip mode to Store them as uncompressed (store mode) and even though the filesize was the same as how I did it before it seems to of corrected the problem.

So any of you out there having similar problem try this solution.
