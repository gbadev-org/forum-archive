#157747 - thedopefish - Wed May 28, 2008 1:52 pm

modchamp wrote:
Ok got one last question, you guys say it has to support B mode I take that to mean like 802.11B? But i've heard elsewhere that G is backwards compatible with B, so do G routers work?


802.11G routers generally have a setting to switch modes between "B only", "G only", and "mixed" (which is the default). If you have it set for "G only", it will not work with a DS; otherwise you should be fine.
