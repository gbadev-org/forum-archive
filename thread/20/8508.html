#80126 - tepples - Wed Apr 19, 2006 12:39 am

Mic data can be sampled at 8000 Hz and sent as 8-bit A-law (PAL) or mu-law (NTSC), or it can be compressed with IMA ADPCM. You can get the codec working and then wait for sgstair to figure out ad-hoc mode (for local area connections) or DHCP (for Internet connections).
