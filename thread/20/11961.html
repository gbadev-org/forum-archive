#112466 - tepples - Sat Dec 16, 2006 5:27 pm

Jaguar is only "64-bit" in its blitter and in its data bus.

There are three 32-bit CPUs:
  • "Tom" CPU - 26 MHz
  • "Jerry" CPU - 26 MHz
  • "Moto" CPU - 13 MHz
PicoDriveDS does its best just to emulate the Sega Genesis's CPU, which is identical to "Moto" but clocked at a slower speed.
