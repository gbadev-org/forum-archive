There is an Article saying that:

http://www.ndswelt.com/2005/10/11/warnung-nintendo-ds-virus-zerstort-die-firmware/

It's a German Page, here is the translation:

Warning, Nintendo DS Virus destroys Firmware

Like before on the PSP, the first Virus exists for the DS now. The Program was programmed and published by a guy named DarkFader. The virus is camouflaged as Hentai Viewer (taihen.zip) or as Rom Loader (r0mloader.zip) and flashes parts of the firmware - for most user this means that the DS becomes unusable after the Attack. Remedy creates a Tool named FlashMe that must be installed already before - thus it is possible to put the firmware on again. Generally thus caution is required, if new Roms are installed - beginners should inform first in detail about Homebrew (privately programmed NDS software).
