#88519 - tepples - Mon Jun 19, 2006 10:59 pm

MAX Media Launcher has sleep issues with some of the official WMB demos (such as those captured at E3 or downloaded from a DS Download Station). If you close the DS, they will repeatedly sleep and wake. To work around this, remove the MAX Media Launcher (while the power is still on) and put an authentic DS Game Card into SLOT-1 before you launch the WMB demos.
