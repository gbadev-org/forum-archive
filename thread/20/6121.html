Shoxz wrote:
What the hells MSX?


I suggest using google sometimes... :D

MSX is a z80 computer which was released 1983. ASCII are the ones who made it together with Microsoft. Coding language was called MicroSoft eXtended BASIC. Konami among other companies released games on the MSX computers. MSX cpu z80 is clocked at 3.58mhz. CPU is double on MSX with TurboR.
Audio chip is the AY-3-8910, same one as in Sinclair ZS Spectrum 128. This provides 3 channels.
Konami released Metal Gear 2 on the MSX which is alot better than the NES version due to the fact that they actually tried writing a great story :D
Gradius is pretty much the same IMO.
