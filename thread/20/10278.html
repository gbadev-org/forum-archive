#91674 - tepples - Sat Jul 08, 2006 5:36 pm

You might want to try the following to uncorrupt your Wi-Fi:
  1. Don't do anything that I erased from your post and replaced with (avast ye), which is piratese for "hold it right there".
  2. Clear out your Wi-Fi settings. Look in Wi-Fi settings > Options > Erase Nintendo WFC Configuration.
  3. Clear out your firmware settings, as HtheB suggested. Open the battery box, pull out the battery, wait ten seconds, replace the battery, and next time you start your DS, it'll go into setup mode.
  4. Run flashme.nds again to reinstall version 7. If some of the firmware gets corrupted, a reinstall might fix it.
