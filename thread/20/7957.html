#65261 - FireSlash - Sun Jan 01, 2006 5:47 pm

The NDS Wi-Fi games work over a standard 802.11 b/g network, but they do not support ad-hoc mode. Just make sure you have an access point somewhere on your network and you're fine. Configuration should match that of your other PCs on the network.

Some wifi cards also support an Access Point mode, with certain software. If you have a spare wifi card floating around, check its driver CD, or check out the SoftAP software available online.
