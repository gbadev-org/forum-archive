#114286 - quadomatic - Sat Jan 06, 2007 8:23 am

just go get a codec pack and have ffdshow installed. download youtube videos using this: http://javimoya.com/blog/youtube_en.php

open up the "VFW Codec Configuration" in ffdshow from the start menu. You should automatically be in the "Codecs" menu from the sidebar. Under the "Format" column in the main part of the window, you should be able to find a codec labeled "FLV1". Just click where it says "Disabled" in the same row and select "libavcodec" from the drop down menu.

Then just convert the videos with dpgenc like you normally would
