#140286 - tepples - Sat Sep 15, 2007 12:51 am

MAX Media Dock is not stolen.

I thought we already hashed that out in another topic, either on this forum or on That Other Forum. GBA Movie Player and MAX Media Dock are both CompactFlash adapters. Now CompactFlash is just (parallel) ATA in a different form factor, and all ATA controllers have to have the same registers. GBAMP just happens to use the most obvious way of laying out these registers in GBA ROM address space, and it's more than likely that the MMD's design was conceived independently.
