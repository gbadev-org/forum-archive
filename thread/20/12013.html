I control iTunes with my DS (on my Mac) using DS2Key and Sizzlingkeys which is an itunes controller app (map controls for itunes through keyboard shortcuts) I set all the buttons to F1-F4 and some others and then mapped those keys on DS2Key OSX and now i have a wireless iTunes controller :D Im assuming your running Windows?
