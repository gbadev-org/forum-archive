#149205 - Metaluna - Wed Jan 16, 2008 7:06 pm

StarLite 0.03 has been released!!!

What's new:

- StarLite is now using 3D sprites. Gfx are the same, but it's just a tricky way to be able to have 1024 sprites on the screen

- Wifi mode implemented! If you have 2 DS, you can play against someone :

* Copy StarLite.nds on the 2 DS (so far, it's rather logical)

* on DS #1, put a equipe.txt file at the root of your card with just the number 1 in the file (without making any space or pressing ENTER)

* On DS #2, put a equipe.txt file at the root of your card with just the number 2 in the file.

This way the program will be able to know if you are player 1 or 2 despite the fact the authors didn't make a menu yet. It will come with the next release.

Launch StarLite and wait for both games to be ready to play. Enjoy!

It's still very buggy (the DS might crash from times to times and when you kill the opponent, your own marine happens to die too), but it's just to show it can work!
