#63431 - Suddenly_Dead - Tue Dec 13, 2005 1:51 am

psionicgeek wrote:
Ok but what about aduio?
also does it have a usb port?
Audio is fine. Since you should be able to run Moonshell on an M3 as well, you'll definately be able to get perfectly good sounding audio. The Play-Yan, from what I've heard, has pretty poor audio quality, though a firmware update may have changed that.

There is no USB port, you need to get a CF and/or SD card reader to access the cards. No one has mentioned the fact that you need to buy a card in the thread yet, so I'll toss that out too. They range greatly in price depending on capacity, I think I got a 512 megabyte CF card for $40CDN.
