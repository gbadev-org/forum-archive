#68396 - Sektor - Tue Jan 24, 2006 7:12 am

WEP is not supported, so you will have to disable it on your router.

To get the IP settings run cmd.exe and type ipconfig

C:\>ipconfig

Windows IP Configuration


Ethernet adapter Local Area Connection:

Connection-specific DNS Suffix . :
IP Address. . . . . . . . . . . . : 192.168.0.5
Subnet Mask . . . . . . . . . . . : 255.255.255.0
Default Gateway . . . . . . . . . : 192.168.0.1

For this example, in ds2win:

DS IP:
192.168.000.006 (the third digit can usually be anything not in use)

Gateway Address:
192.168.000.001 (same as Default Gateway)

DNS Address:
not important since we won't be resolving any host to IP

Subnet Mask:
255.255.255.000 (just leave it as is)

Server IP:
192.168.000.005 (this is the IP of the PC running win2ds.exe)

Firewalls can cause problems and so can routers that don't transmit at 2mbit.

#84941 - spacepirate - Thu May 25, 2006 11:23 pm

Try using the manual configuration and entering in your connection information through that. It won't work for me unless I connect in that manner. Try going into the start menu, clicking run, typing in "cmd" (no quotes), and then typing in "ipconfig" (no quotes) to get the information you need. Also, if your router has an option to assign a static ip to a mac address, try giving your DS a static ip. It generally makes wifi homebrew work better.

By the way, you can increase the refresh speed by pressing start and changing the speed from there. You may have already known this.
