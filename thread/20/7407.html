From IGN:

"A compilation of challenging card games titles, based on the GBA line of Ultimate games from Telegames and Cosmigi. The game features vastly upgraded graphics to make use of the 3D potential of the Nintendo DS (including a 3D "Person Creator" for designing your own avatar and full 3D environments of play), and includes playable versions of the following: Solitaire (with additional Solitaire variations/rule options not seen on GBA), Video Poker (also with multiple variations), Hearts, Spades, Euchre, Cribbage, Canasta, Bridge, Go Fish!, Crazy 8's, Blackjack, 5-Card Draw Poker, 7-Card Stud Poker, Texas Hold'em Poker, Pinochle, Whist, and Gin Rummy."

From a review off of GameFAQs about the GBA version:

"I really haven't touched the Solitaire games, but there are 15 of them. Klondike, Free Cell, Canfield, Pyramid, Golf, Spiderette, Baker's Dozen, Bristol, Castle, Aces Up, Baroness, Eagle Wing, Eight Off, Calculation, and Betsy Ross. Not being a Solitaire fan, I have no idea what those games are, but all are presumably done correctly."
