Since we have a customized skins for moonshell thread, I thought why not have a customized clock thread for moonshell?!

To turn on your clock, change the global.ini file in the shell folder like this:

Modify this entry in the global.ini:
Code:
[BacklightTimeout]

WhenStandby=5
WhenPicture=0
WhenText=0
WhenSound=5

**When Standyby allows you to set the # of seconds until the clock shows up. It is just like a screensaver

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Again I stress that you must rename downloaded files to clk.ini, msp and bin

I hope you like it! This is very easy to do and I am sure we could have a conglomerate of clocks if everyone contributed. Please support!

For US users, to change the date format from 2006/07/03 to 07/03/2006,

Open your global.ini. At the very end of the file there should be

FormatDate=DD/MM/YYYY

Change that to M/D/YYYY.

jbullfrog wrote:
To change the date format from 2006/07/03 (weird format) to 07/03/2006 (correct format)

What you call the "weird format" (2006-07-03) is ISO 8601, a logically big-endian format. The American format (07/03/2006) is the weird format, as it is neither little-endian nor big-endian.
