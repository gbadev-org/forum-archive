#71175 - tepples - Fri Feb 10, 2006 8:46 pm

In your access point's access control configuration, specify that Nintendo brand devices (use the first 3 octets of your DS's MAC address) don't have to use WPA or the captive portal. If you do not control the access point that you wish to use, then you'll have to wait until TCP, SSL, and w3m are ported to the DS.
