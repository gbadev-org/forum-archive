#135286 - LiraNuna - Sat Jul 21, 2007 10:02 pm

from ARM7:

Code:

bool isBattWeak = readPowerManagement(1);


or if you like fancy defines
Code:

bool isBattWeak = readPowerManagement(PM_BATTERY_REG);
