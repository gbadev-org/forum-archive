#128469 - Gunnex - Fri May 11, 2007 7:41 pm

This is what I know:
NO
1) FlashMe won't detect a phat as a Lite, and it would (likely) crash upon backlight change.
2) Due to multiple backlight levels, the connectors are different.
3) Due to general hardware differences, the connectors would be in different places.
4) The firmware knows what screens are plugged in and will adjust as such. It might get all wacky if the connected screens are wrong.
