If you're asking what the GBA uses, then it would be 8x8 pixel tiles, just like some 2D consoles (**SNES**). On systems like the GBA, 16x16 pixel tiles are actually metatiles (or a group of tiles put together, usually in a rectangular fashion) of 4 8x8 pixel tiles.
