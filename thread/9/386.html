Link wrote:
I have to save it as bmp, yes but 8 or 24 bit?

When you're first starting out, save your graphics as 8-bit BMP. Then use gfx2gba (available from gbadev.org). Later, once you're familiar with GBA's tiled modes, you'll probably want to switch to 4-bit graphics.

It'd be a good idea to read through The Pern Project GBA Tutorial.
