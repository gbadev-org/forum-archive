#54695 - ScottLininger - Wed Sep 21, 2005 6:12 pm

If you can get your hands on Photoshop, it's a snap.

Open your Actions window to record the series of steps you want to do to the image. Then go to File > Automate > Batch, and you can select a folder and it will perform the transformation on all images.

If you don't have access to photoshop... consider downloading PHP and using its Image Functions to do the changes.
