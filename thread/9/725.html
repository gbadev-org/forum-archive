#3771 - tepples - Sat Mar 08, 2003 5:00 am

arog wrote:
The best part is, everything is free!

But the fonts themselves may not be. Font designs can be patented in at least the United States, and they fall under an automatic copyright upon creation in the European Union for the life of the author plus 70 years and in Japan for the life of the author plus 50 years. I'd suggest talking it over with your font foundry's legal department before you commit to using a font in a program that you intend to distribute.
