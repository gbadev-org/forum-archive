#16831 - tepples - Wed Feb 25, 2004 6:14 pm

Not always. Sprite cels in many GBA programs are compressed, and especially for larger sprites, they may not be ordered in left-to-right and top-to-bottom reading order but instead some sort of optimized scattering produced by tools such as Objective.

Still, if a program uses the most straightforward storage for its sprites (uncompressed, 4-bit or 8-bit packed, left-to-right and top-to-bottom), you can rip the data with any tile editor such as 8TED, Tile Molester, etc. This probably won't give you the palettes though.
