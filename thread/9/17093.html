#172372 - Cearn - Wed Feb 03, 2010 9:41 am

My guess is that the image is actually a direct-color (bitdepth > 8). If this is the case, the image doesn't actually have a real palette (the one you see is constructed on the fly), so any palette-changes you make won't end up in the saved image. Save it as a 4bpp or 8bpp image if the palette order matters.
