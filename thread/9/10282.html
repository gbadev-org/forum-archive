#91707 - Dwedit - Sat Jul 08, 2006 11:29 pm

The only logo embedded into the cartridge is the Nintendo logo. The game will refuse to boot if it doesn't exactly match the nintendo logo the BIOS expects to see. You can go ahead and change it, but then the GBA won't boot.
