#32046 - dj-ceejay - Fri Dec 17, 2004 3:16 pm

Open your sprite.
Go to 'Image -> Mode -> Index Colours'.
Click OK if it asks you to 'Flatten Layers'.
In the 'Palette' drop down box choose 'Custom'.
Click on the bottom right hand square of the 'Color table'.
Click the transparent colour on your sprite or use the color picker then click OK.
If your transparent colour appears twice in the colour table remove the other one - click on it and select another colour.
If you do not do this step Photoshop will use the first colour in the palette it comes to (the top left).
Click OK to the 'Colour Table'.
On the 'Indexed Color' dialog 'Colors' should be 256.
Click OK to the 'Indexed Color'.
Save your sprite as a PCX file.
