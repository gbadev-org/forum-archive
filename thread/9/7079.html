#56264 - tepples - Fri Oct 07, 2005 3:17 pm

Are these designs copied straight from Bandai's Lumines? I don't want to use designs that are too similar because of legal concerns. Remember how none of the homebrew Tetris clones have similar backgrounds or block decorations to Nintendo's Tetris for NES or Game Boy.

As for the template, those 'pf' and 'bg' are the files that are fed into the skin compiler. The skin compiler rearranges the blocks into the format that the game engine uses. The only difference is that the skin compiler can only read .bmp, .pcx, and .tga files, and there are restrictions on the number of colors that go into one thing, with extra restrictions on the background in the GBA version. I should track down an Allegro extension to load .png files sometime soon.

And what are those boxes and dashes at the top?

MaHe: Yes, the skin compiler will look for all skin description files in a folder, just as GSM Player currently does.
