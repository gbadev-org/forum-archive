I've been using Mappy and found that it's pretty good. There's a few things it doesn't do well that I wish it did (I don't like its exporter, and it doesn't do tile-flipping the way I want it to), but I always end up using fmp2gba in my build process for the export part of things. For the most part, it works well and doesn't get in my way.

I'm not going to say it's the best, but it's the best that I've found for my purposes.
