#11018 - torne - Tue Sep 23, 2003 3:25 pm

Flashcarts and their apps measure things in bits. Your 256mb cart is 256 megabits == 32 megabytes. Thus, a file that Windows displays as being 8MB is indeed 64 megabits, and will use up one quarter of your cart.
