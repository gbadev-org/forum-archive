#162214 - tepples - Thu Aug 28, 2008 3:22 am

There is a build of FlashMe v8 that doesn't search for the "PASS" code to boot SLOT-2 in DS mode; instead, you have to hold A+B+X+Y to force it when you need it. Or even on the version you have, you can hold Select while turning on the DS to skip the search for "PASS".
