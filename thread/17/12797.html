#123348 - Lynx - Tue Mar 27, 2007 3:32 pm

Quote:
Can a card for slot 1 do anything a card for slot 2 can't?


Only if the author of the software you want to run has designed it to only work on a slot-1 media.

But, that doesn't take the future into account. When using a slot-2 device (with FlashMe) you have an empty slot-1 that can be used for the current NDS Motion Card or DSerial. If you have have a slot-1 device, you have to take it out to insert either of these options, which you will no longer have read/write access to your media (obviously). But, as slot-1 soltion acceptance continues to grow and they become fully homebrew compatible, then we will see more slot-2 addons and the reverse will be true.

So basically, you have to decide if you want what is available now, or want to look toward the future and what "may" be available then.
