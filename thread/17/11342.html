3. Will 'Sandisk/Kingston 1g Micro SD Transflash Cards' be fast enough to run NDS/GBA backups without slowdown?

Backups of what?

Any card that supports GBA mode can load backups of your music CDs into GSM Player, and any card that supports the REIN FAT library (an extension of Chishm's gba_nds_fat with additional drivers for various SD adapters) can load backups of your music CDs into MoonShell. There probably won't be much of a problem with loading backups of your DVDs (SmartRipper FTW) into MoonShell if you encode them properly.
