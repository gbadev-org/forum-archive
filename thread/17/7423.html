#58667 - notb4dinner - Tue Oct 25, 2005 5:42 am

There's a standard that specifies that KB, MB etc are used for base 10 sizes while KiB, MiB etc are used for base 2 sizes. ie. 1KB = 1000 bits, 1KiB = 1024 bits.
