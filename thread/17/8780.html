#74149 - tepples - Thu Mar 02, 2006 9:23 pm

If you have a small demo (under about 250 KB), and it is designed for multiboot, then it will work with just a linker cable (on GBA, GBA SP, or Game Boy Player only) or just a GBA Movie Player (and CF card). But not all small demos are designed for multiboot, either because they need to use EWRAM as scratch space or because the developer was lazy.
