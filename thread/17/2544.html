Yes the chips are on the cartridge (on the back of the cart)...
[Images not permitted - Click here to view it]
I assume this is the first version since it has 4 chips.
If your EZ-Flash has 4 chips, you can download the drivers here: http://www.ezflash.cn/zip/ezdriverps.zip

And, if your cart only has two chips on the back, it is the second version. Those drivers can be downloaded from here: http://www.ezflash.cn/zip/ezdriver.zip

Should hopefully work....if not you can always contact the retailer who sold the device to you.
