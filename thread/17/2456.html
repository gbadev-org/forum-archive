#13566 - bomberman - Wed Dec 17, 2003 12:35 am

Yeah I get this often as well with my USB Flash Linker.
What I usually do in this case is I go to Start/Control Panel, click on System and then choose Hardware tab. Here I click on Device Manager and you should see your GBA USB device with an icon showing there is a problem. Then I just disable the driver and enable it again. And then everything works fine until next reboot... Strange but efficient !

BTW, I run it on XP... Good luck !
