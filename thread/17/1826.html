#14493 - malone - Wed Jan 07, 2004 1:55 pm

stardust, are you talking about EZF-Advance vs. EZ-Flash II PowerStar?

The latter has a 1.8v memory chip which consumes less power from the GBA, yielding ~5 hours of longer play (battery life). EZF-Advance has a 3.3v memory chip which consumes power rather quickly.
