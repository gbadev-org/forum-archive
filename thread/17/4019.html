#26158 - ampz - Wed Sep 08, 2004 6:04 pm

Equivalent: no. Compatible: yes.
Many different carts work with different linkers. The only thing preventing any cart from working with any linker is the software.

For example, littlewriter supports several different linkers, and several different carts. I think Jeff also has a good piece of flasher software which supports many different cart/linker combinations.
As far as I know EZ/XG stuff is generally incompatible with other hardware/software.
But many visoly/f2a/xrom carts are compatible with each others linkers. You just need the appropriate software.

A compatibility chart would indeed be nice.
