#17994 - sg2k - Thu Mar 18, 2004 4:19 am

I was able to get pogoshell to work on my F2AU 512

You have to set the shell.bin as your boot rom in the config screen of the F2AU PowerWriter, and then flash your rom.bin on the card.

But yeah, it only supports up to 256M so you'll only be using half your card if you do that. For now if you want to use your whole card you have to use PowerWriter
