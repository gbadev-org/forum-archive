#20934 - ScottLininger - Thu May 20, 2004 5:46 am

I've had to uninstall the drivers and reinstall before... this might be what you're experiencing

Go to Control Panels > System > Hardware Manager

Then find the thing under the USB tree, right click and UNINSTALL.

You may have to do this two or three times.

Then plug the cable back in and go through it all again. When it barks at you about "Driver is not digitally signed" be sure to say "Install anyway."

Hope that helps.
