#9594 - tepples - Sat Aug 09, 2003 6:07 pm

The loader might be finding .mb programs hidden in the ROMs, which are used for single-pak multiplayer.

The loader might be finding programs in the space after the ROMs, which wasn't entirely erased when you rewrote it with just two small ROMs.

If you're using a 256 MB F2A cart, I recommend PogoShell over the loader that comes with the F2A (which is probably no more sophisticated than the old "bluescreen" loader that came with Visoly carts).
