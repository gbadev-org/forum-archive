#15108 - tepples - Sat Jan 17, 2004 7:13 am

It's a limitation in your linker software. The linker I use to backup carts (MBV2) can read the cart and automatically discover the cart size, comparing the ROM to itself at 32 Mbit intervals. Try backing up 128 Mbit and then using an overdump correction program. To learn more, visit a forum dedicated to GBA backup; forum.gbadev.org isn't one.
