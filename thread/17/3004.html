#17435 - tepples - Mon Mar 08, 2004 3:38 am

Emulators that run on the GBA don't do GBC mode yet, and their GB mono compatibility is nowhere near perfect.

If you want the absolute best performance on GBC games, go get a GBC flash cart. The GB Bridge to F2A carts is reported to work, but I'm not sure that it works on the new F2A Ultra carts.
