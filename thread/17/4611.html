Hi,

it?s nearly the same way using the "supercard" (if you know this product).

You format your CF/SMC card on your pc in FAT mode (not FAT32 !) and then you can copy your files on the card.

After this you plug in your gamewallet into the gba/sp and plug in your f2a/pro/ultra card into the gba/sp.

Then you start with select+start (if i remember right) and you will reach a little menue. You select the file(s) you want and the file(s) will be copied to the f2a card and now you can play them....etc. etc.
