I use a GBA Movie Player (CF adapter for SLOT-2) and an R4 (microSD adapter for SLOT-1). If you want the equivalent of a G6 with expandable memory, get the M3 Perfect Lite (microSD adapter for SLOT-2 with RAM). If you need another 8 Gbit, just buy a 1 GB (8 bits = 1 byte) microSD card at an office supply store or on an auction site.
