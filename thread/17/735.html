#3764 - pelrun - Fri Mar 07, 2003 11:06 pm

Not quite. The "heads" of most of the GBA flashcarts are oversized compared to an official game cart. The cart slot in the SP is recessed more than the original, and the larger head just won't fit.

People have successfully filed down the sides of the head to make it fit, since only a little bit of plastic needs to be removed.
