#38658 - tepples - Wed Mar 30, 2005 1:02 am

utilizes wrote:
If the cart won't accept .xls files or whatever, just rename them, as it won't change the actual file, really. Mind, I might be wrong...

However, the cart might attempt to header-fix your .xls files, thereby corrupting them. A better choice is to stick them in a .gbfs file and then append them to some random small GBA ROM.
