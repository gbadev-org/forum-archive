... or it may have something to do with the fact that during shipment the cart goes thru various temperature (possibly extreme cold or extreme heat). It is best to write to a fash memory after it has been stabilized at room temperature for some time, and hence the suggested 8 hour wait.

Either way, I would definitely wait the minimum amount of time suggested ... possibly a little more, just to be on the safe side.
