Quote:

I'd be surprised if you could find a writable cart/linker in a local store (unless you live in Hong Kong), meaning the best way to get one is order over the internet, just check around to make sure that a company is fairly well known so you don't get your money stolen.

These questions are better for the GBA programming forum, but there are ham names for the keys (I believe they are in mygba.h) which are something like F_CTRLINPUT_UP_PRESSED, F_CTRLINPUT_START_PRESSED , and so on. (http://www.aaronrogers.com/ham/index.php for a tutorial on programming for the GBA in C/C++)

And yes, you can use both C and C++ (or a combination of both) using ham / vham (available for free download at ngine.de)
