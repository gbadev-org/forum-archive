1. Please punctuate. Because you did not mark where one thought ends and another begins, your comment was hard to read for a native English speaker, and would be impossible for somebody who's not entirely fluent.

2. Yes. If your cart has some sort of bankswitch, you can put multiple games on your cart if they total smaller than the cart size. F2A and EZF type flash carts have bankswitch; Wise Card doesn't. However, if you're using replacement menu software such as PogoShell, the menu software must support your flash cart brand's bankswitch protocol. PogoShell's F2A support is much more mature than its EZF support because F2A bankswitching is based on the bankswitching of the Visoly carts that preceded it.
