#21802 - dagamer34 - Sun Jun 06, 2004 4:15 pm

You are not going to find any flash carts in a store (unless you live in Hong-Kong) so ordering off of the Internet is the only way to get one. There are some reliable retailers in the "Retailer Feedback" section of this site, so take a look at them and read the reviews. If you have a PayPal account, life is much easier when ordering as most site accept it as a method of payment. A word to the wise: It is always better to order in your native country as shipping won't be as expensive.

Jandaman's I heard was a good place to shop and has a shipping address located in the U.S. too. Good luck in finding a online store.
