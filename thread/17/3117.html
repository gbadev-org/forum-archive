#18466 - SimonB - Fri Mar 26, 2004 9:15 pm

I have had a chance to try out most of the gba flash kits available and if
you have decided to go with either the EFA or the EZF-Advance you have
made a good choice IMO. The EZF-Advance might be cheaper but the EFA
enjoys a much better writing speed. However, as far as I can tell, you cant
leave the EFA cartridge connected to a GBA when you write to it. Thats
something I like about the EZF-Advance, that you can leave the thing
connected and not have to keep removing it and connecting it all the time.

Please stay (far, far, far) away from the EZF-Advance II though!
