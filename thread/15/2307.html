#12245 - poslundc - Wed Nov 05, 2003 2:33 pm

Can't say I know the game which you are referring to, but simple arcade games like the one you are describing usually make for good tutorials/demos, because you can incrementally add new features and complexity to them.

For example, once you've got the base engine working you might decide you want to add a way to catch powerups of some sort. Or you may want to experiment with animation techniques for the status display. Or you may decide to give it a scrolling backdrop. Or make a boss-teabag appear every five rounds. Or who knows what. Point is that you can start off with coding a very simple engine and add complexity to it as you see fit.
