#4801 - killerapp - Thu Apr 10, 2003 5:59 am

I had this problem originally, but I fixed it by using a 9 volt adapter for the writer. The manual says not to use the wall plug while using usb, but this is the only way i could get my linker to work. I've been using it for months and months without any problems, so I'm not sure why it says you should not use the plug in the manual. It has worked perfectly ever since I got the plug, so I'm assuming that my usb port wasn't providing consistant power, and yours probably does the same. Just get the 9 volt adapter. Although, it's up to you to use the plug since it says not to in the manual.
