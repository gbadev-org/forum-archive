#88223 - tepples - Sun Jun 18, 2006 5:20 am

xproductions wrote:
most monitors are multisync (can accept any kind of video in).

But most consumer multisync monitors can't accept a horizontal sync below 30 kHz, unlike the 15.7 kHz of JAMMA and DS display signals and the 13.6 kHz of GBA display signals. You'll probably need at least a line doubler in the video path.
