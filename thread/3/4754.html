#33418 - tepples - Mon Jan 03, 2005 4:19 am

If you want to test multiplayer games, use VBA Link or the shareware version of no$gba.

The MBV2 cable lets you connect your GBA to a PC's parallel or serial port, but unavoidable timing issues in a PC environment ensure that it probably won't work between a GBA emulator and GBA hardware.
