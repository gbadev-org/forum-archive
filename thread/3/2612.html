#14478 - tepples - Wed Jan 07, 2004 5:55 am

I'm pretty sure that the XBOO cable would let the user load a program that transfers the contents of the current cart's SRAM to the PC.

Unless the multiboot program has been configured to replicate itself, you can't transfer a running multiboot program from the GBA to the PC with the XBOO cable. If you're trying to intercept a GameCube game's multiboot extras on their way from the GameCube to the GBA, try disassembling the BIOS, learning the details of the multiboot protocol, and bringing in somebody with electronics experience to help you build something like the XBOO cable to let you sniff the transfer so that your PC can act as a client rather than as a server.
