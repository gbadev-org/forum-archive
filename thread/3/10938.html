#100101 - tepples - Sat Aug 26, 2006 3:47 am

To run your own mb.gba files on a GBA, all you need is
  1. GBA, GBA SP, Game Boy Player, Game Boy micro, or Nintendo DS
  2. GBA Movie Player CF (check online auctions for a good deal but watch out for S&H)
  3. CF card
  4. CF writer
Owners of some digital cameras may already have 3 and 4.
