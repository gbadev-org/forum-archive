#996 - ampz - Mon Jan 13, 2003 3:15 am

The battery is for savegame support. If the battery runs out, you will loose your savegame data when you turn off power to the GBA.

Intel strataflash (used by visoly) have a guarateed minimum of 10'000 or 100'000 cycles. (number of times you erase the cart).
