#3592 - cosmic4z - Fri Feb 28, 2003 8:19 pm

Problem Solved !!

CPU is in ARM mode and i was calling the SWI with something like:

swi #20 (works fine on the EMU)

and not

swi #20<<16 (works fine on both EMU and hardware)
