Hi. It's me again.

In the GBA booklet it warns against the use of any sort of rechargable batterys. I know that Nickel Cadium(Ni-Cad) batterys have some problems (cell reversal and overtime they also cause copper wiring to go black therefore build up resistance). But Nickel Metal Hydride (Ni-MH) don't seem to have any problems and until recently have been used in mobile phones. Would it be safe to use Ni-MH batterys in my GBA? I'm getting sick of buying expensive disposibles.
