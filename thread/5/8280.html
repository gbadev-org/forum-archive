A very nice Tetris clone has been sent in by Andrew Best:

This is a game I have been working on since 2003. I am an undergrad cs student
hoping to submit this as an independent project for credit, therefore your comments
and criticisms would be especially appreciated.

-About the game-
Yes, Polyominoes, is indeed the one millionth Tetris clone known to man. However, it
features unique modes, and for being a homebrew, it is fairly complete.

-Features-
- 4 unique modes of game play
- 3 different sets of shapes to play with
- menus and presentation
- artificial intelligence
- 2 player link play (not fully tested)
- many options
- sound, music, and special effects

See the instructions in the zip for more detailed documentation.
