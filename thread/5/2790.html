Here is a GBA tonal guitar tuner sent in by MadBob:

I have written my first GBA program and it is Tonal Guitar Tuner,
you play the note and tune your string along with the tone,

You do have to have a fairly good ear to tune along to a tone
but even if you haven't yet developed a 'good ear' it will still
help you get the strings very close, then you can tune across
the strings as you would normally do.
Thanks to OrR for his GBAkkord which inspired me to write FreeTune Adv.

It covers:
Standard, Standard b(flat), Standard bb(double flat)
and Dropped D Tunings.

Two versions are included in this archive:
FreeTuneAdv.gba for VBA or Flash Cart.
FreeTuneAdv.mb for Multiboot Cables.

You can grab the GBA guitar tuner straight from the main site!
