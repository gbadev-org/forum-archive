A demo for the GBA released in Easter has been announced by Raimo Gratseff:

Last easter we released a demo for game boy advance which won the console demo competition at breakpoint2003 demoparty in germany and I was thinking about submitting it to gbadev.org as there might be people who are interested to see it. (even if they don't know it)

The demo has a wide range of effects and a 3d engine which is able to render fullscreen, full resolution, fully texturemapped 3d scenes. It has also two parts (with different music) and has a total running time of almost 6 minutes. This also makes the rom file as big as some of the commercial games, 4MB.

9 persons participated in the making of the demo but most of the work was done by dairos, an 18-year-old coder who coded everything alone. other people were 2 Musicians and 6 Graphics artists (who i count myself in).

Name of the demo: Phloam
Made by: unique
