News > Dragon Basic v1.2.3 released

#4989 - SimonB - Wed Apr 16, 2003 9:14 pm

mail from Jeff Massung, author of Dragon Basic:

Just wanted to update everyone on the current development status of the Dragon
BASIC Compiler. It is now up to version 1.2.3, and since the last post, here
is a small list of what has been added:

- An IDE (project wizard, syntax highlighting, etc.)
- Palette color manipulation
- Palette rotation (16 & 256 colors)
- PCX support
- Mosaic effects
- Sprite collision detection
- Support for 16-bit WAV files

Feel free to drop over at http://www.simforth.com and check it out. There is
now a great fansite at http://www.dragonbasic.com, too.
