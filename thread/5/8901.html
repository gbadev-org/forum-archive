A classic puzzle game Xor, has been remade for the GBA by Mick Waites:

I have just made available from my website a GBA re-make of an old favourite puzzle
game of mine called Xor. Xor is a puzzle game fairly reminiscent of Repton or
Boulderdash except that it requires no hand-eye co-ordination or fast reactions. All
you need is brain power. It is a very challenging game. It runs on hardware or
emulator and is completely free and fully functional.

The website is www.gbagames.dsl.pipex.com I intend to add more games to it at
some point in the future, but no promises!

You can download GBA Xor straight from the main site!
