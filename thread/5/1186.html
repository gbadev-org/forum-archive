#5846 - krom - Fri May 09, 2003 10:52 pm

Justin Ronco has announced a new GBA game project:

Over the past few months myself and two fellow students have developed a
GBA game based around stealth and mini-games. We have hosting for the
game on SourceForge already, but were hoping to let people know that
it's there.

The game is called "CODENAME: Monkey-Bots".
The url is at: http://cmbgame.sourceforge.net/

"CODENAME: Monkey-Bots is a 1-4 player sneaky/puzzle game. Players work
cooperatively to complete levels by sneaking by security bots and
playing mini-games. The game was developed for a class project by a
team of students; the semester is now over so we're releasing the game
under the terms of the GPL. Please check it out and let us know what
you think."
