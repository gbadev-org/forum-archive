Dave Mejia has released his first game for the GBA called Touch!:

Touch! can be thought of as breakout with a few
twists...
The goal of the game is to move all the blocks to the
bottom of the screen. Your paddle can move up, down,
left or right? and you can control the speed of the
ball.
To clear the level you must move all the blocks within
the three-minute time limit. Each time the ball hits
the red line you will be penalized by a certain
deduction of time.

The game includes:
+ A High Score system (not saved to SRAM though)
+ Instructions are built into the image
+ Proper credit to the people who made the game possible.

Download the game from the main site.
