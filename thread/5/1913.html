A new demo from Matt Current has been announced by Janne Toivanen:

Matt Current (popularly also known as Matti Nykyinen) is a finnish demogroup
making art for a great range of platforms. We released a new demo for
Gameboy Advance at Assembly 2003 and we're here to promote it.

Some features included this time are antialiased lines, radial blurring, raycasting,
radiosity lighting and transparency effects. Only mode 4 is used and everything except
radial blur runs in full-resolution. Krawall is used to play a ten channel module and
graphics are converted with our own utils.

Greetings to the deadline, to the polyfiller bug from Poland, and to the people feeding us.
To contact Matt Current, mail to the coder (janne.toivanenATkolumbus.fi).
We like constructive feedback and massive amount of beer.
