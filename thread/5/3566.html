Andreas Timmermann has submitted a nice realtime 3d texture mapping demo.
Here is an excerpt of what the author says in the readme.txt file:

this is a little texturemapping-demo.
it demonstrate the difference bitween
linear- and perpective mapping.
just a test.

You can press the start button to flip between linear and perspective
texture mapping modes in the demo, which highlights the differences.
The demo also includes the complete source code in C.

You can download the binary and source straight from the main site!
