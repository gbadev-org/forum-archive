Jake Reardon has released his first gba game called Pac-Run:

Its a fairly simple game whereby the player tries to escape 6 ghosts
moving around on the sceen chasing him. To stay alive , you are able
to drink a small bottle of health as well as use a warp at the bottom
of the screen to one of 4 random locations.

You can download the game straight from the main site!
