Nearly complete, fully playable version of Sierra's 1983-classic "Oil's Well". I haven't done any work on this one for almost a year now, and although it doesn't seem too likely that I'll have the time to wrap up it's loose ends any time soon, I figured it'd be a pity if nobody out there would ever know of my puny GBA efforts. Therefore I am hereby releasing 'my' recreation of Oil's Well upon the public, featuring:
- all 8 original levels (repixeled by hand for 240x160)
- hiscore-list
