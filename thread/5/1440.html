A Scrabble Dictionary has been released by Rob Blanding:

I hacked this dictionary together in an afternoon to supplement my travel
Scrabble board. It has been tested on GBA SP hardware and the VisualBoy
Advance emulator.

I am using the Enable2k word list. This is a very complete list of over
173,000 words. I have yet to find any words included in the official
Scrabble dictionary that are not included here.

You can see the authors other GBA projects at:
http://staff.washington.edu/robertb/GBA/

You can download it straight from the main site!
