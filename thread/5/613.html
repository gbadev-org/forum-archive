Uranus Zero is an enhanced version of freeware Uranus 2
The great particularity of U0 is the "OnLine Highscore Code" that you can
put and view your score directly on the Uranus Zero web site.
This feature is simple, play the game and do the best score, at the
gameover, the game generate your score code.

- Highscore Online Code

- Fun Mode

- Special Attack & upgradable shoot
- 4 types of sub weapons
