I'm working on my own 4-bit ADPCM codec, which I will release as free software. I've already tweaked it to sound quite a bit better than IMA ADPCM (mostly through measures to hide slope overload), and it takes 15% CPU for 18157 Hz mono playback. A half hour of music would fit in a 128 Mbit cartridge.

EDIT: I recompile with DevKit Advance R5 beta 3, and it's down to 6% CPU. I'm busy packaging it for public release.
