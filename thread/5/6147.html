Donnie Russell II has released version 1.2 of AGB_Hack:

I've compiled a new release of AGB_Hack using devkitARM. I've learned a lot
about GBA programming since I first made this port of Hack, the predecessor
to NetHack. Version 1.2 has graphics optimizations, interface improvements
(which will be continued in the next release), sound effects for certain
events such as attacks and gaining experience, and an animated intro screen.
I also changed the way your pets behave when you walk into them (they move
out of your way).

You can visit the authors website for more information at:
http://www.freewebs.com/drussell/

Download AGB_Hack v1.2 straight from the main site!
