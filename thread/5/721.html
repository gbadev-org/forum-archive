News > MASO System - new music and sound lib

#3683 - SimonB - Wed Mar 05, 2003 3:31 am

The MASO System library is a complete system to play sound and music on the Gameboy Advance.

MASO System can play tracker modules (the "native" format for MASO System is Fasttracker II .XM, but many other formats can be converted to XM) in stereo. MASO System can also play samples to add effects to your productions. And MASO System is FAST.


MASO System is available for commercial as well as non-commercial products.

For more info visit: http://maso.r0x.free.fr/
