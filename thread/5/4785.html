The screenshot is from the julia set. To get something like this zoom in on the mandelbrot view twice then zoom in somewhat on the structure on the left.
Chose a point near the edge of the mandelbrot set (use the right shoulder button for better acurracy) then press SELECT to switch to the julia set.
Then zoom on more or less anywhere to get something like the screenshot. Experiment with different julia fixpoints. Have fun :)
