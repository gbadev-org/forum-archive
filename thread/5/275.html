Saikou has just completed his first ever GBA game called Jomo.

Jomo is a puzzle game, similar to the classic Sokoban, but with some
differences, such as rolling blocks, and 'mover' tiles. It has 100
levels, some pretty nice graphics and sounds, and is my first ever GBA
game, so don't be too critical ;).

This game is very polished and features a great intro, with good music
and lots of levels for you to play. It even includes a password system!
