as reported on devrs.com/gba by Juan Pablo Ugarte

This is a multi format pixmap converter for use in GBA projects,it has almost the same features of bmp2c but its structure is better and cleaner, so you can easily modify it to fit your taste
* supported input filestypes: bmp(8 and 24 bpp).
* available outputs: C array and raw.
* makefile ready
