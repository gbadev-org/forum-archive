Mini GBAlinker.com review

Shipping

I ordered a Flash2Advance on a friday, very early. I received an order confirmation immediately via auto-respond. I suppose they took liberty with their "next business day delivery" poilcy because it was not shipped until late monday. I received the package around noon on thursday. The Flash2Advance box was wrapped in bubble wrap. Overall, I was pleased with shipping and ordering, it took less than a week to get the package, and I live in a remote area of British Columbia!

Customer Service

I emailed them a day after it was shipped to ask if it would take longer because I live in a remote area. I received a human reply (no joke) about 5 minutes after I sent the email! He replied in broken english and somewhat rudely, but answered my question nontheless.

Well, I guess that sums up my experience with them, but I am quite pleased overall. Highly recommended.
