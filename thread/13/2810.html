#16035 - radjago - Fri Feb 06, 2004 9:24 pm

I recently bought a 128Mb XG1 card and linker set from Peegou. The price was the best I could find on any card or linker set on the web. Shipping was fast, only 3 days after I ordered it.

However, I did have to contact them to get a confirmation of my order other than the standard Paypal receipt, but they replied to my inquiry within a day, so support is pretty good. I can't say anything further than that, regarding returns or exchanges other than they charge a 15% restocking fee, which is pretty standard these days.
