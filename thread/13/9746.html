#88630 - ck - Tue Jun 20, 2006 6:23 pm

I sent them an email with the subject heading "Order Status" on Sat. the 17th, and they replied that Mon. the 19th. Wouldn't you know they DO have a tracking number for every order. They gave me the tracking # in the response e-mail - it's via UPS.com. I don't know why they don't provide it on their website when you go to log in to check your order.

I forgot to mention there are two types of shipping. Standard which says it requires up to 10 business days, and Express, which requires up to 5. Of course I stuck with Standard, and it cost me a little under $7 for the shipping fee.

Today is Tues. the 20th, and I have received the MML in the mail. The "FROM" address is from a 'Linda Smith C/O Datel' in Clearwater, FL 33760. The packing slip inside of course had England on it, since I suppose that is where CJ is based.

Item took 12 days total from the day I placed the order, to the day it arrived. Not counting Sat. and Suns., it took 8 days, and not counting Suns, it took 10.

Not as bad as I thought, but shipping from Clearwater, FL, it could of came here a bit faster.
