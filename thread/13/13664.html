It seems there's an 8 hours shift between Paris/FR and Kitchener/CAN.

To sum things up
- i placed the order on Thurdsay, January 10th on 3:23pm (Paris/France time) which is 7:23am at the seller's place
- parcel was dropped at the Canada Post office on Friday, 11th
- i received the parcel in my mailbox on Thurdsay, 17th.

Pros :
- Shipped to France in a week
- Untracked shipping arrived safely
- Cheap untracked shipping method
- Packages in a A4-sized bubble-enveloppe
- Full content detail on the custom invoice
- Exact pricing shown on custom invoice
- Classified as "samples"
- Pay in US$, my bank's commision was 2.2%

Cons:
- None !

Another note to french people :
- if you receive the package to "metropolitan" France
- if any item in the parcel has a value above 45 euros
- You will have to pay the 19.6% VAT on the total declared value plus the shipping price plus insurance fee.

In my case, each item was below 45 euros, so even if the total amount was above 45 euros,
the customs consider you could send each in it's own parcel and as a consequence consider you package
is worth less than 45 euros. And when a parcel is worth less than 45 euros and is for personnal use,
it's import tax and value added tax (VAT) free.[/u]
