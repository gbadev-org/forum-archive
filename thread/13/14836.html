#148922 - nornagon - Fri Jan 11, 2008 11:23 pm

I recently ordered two M3 Simply cards and a USB power cable for the Lite, as well as some microSD cards from this retailer.

Originally I'd ordered an M3 DS Real, but too late I realised that it wouldn't work in my friend's phat DS. I emailed support, and they responded within five minutes, with a useful suggestion for an alternative to the Real (for playing GBA homebrew) that *would* work on a DS phat. I decided to just change the order to an M3 Simply, which Paul happily did and refunded me the difference.

The merchandise arrived promptly (ordered on Tuesday, arrived on Friday) and in good condition. The only gripe I have is that I couldn't work out how to track the package, but that's not really 01media's fault (it's rather the Netherlands postal service's).

5/5. Would buy again.
