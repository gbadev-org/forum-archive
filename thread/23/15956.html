#161971 - DayDream - Thu Aug 21, 2008 8:49 pm

We finally got our (sumiguchi & me) latest game done: Video Games Hero. It is a music/rythm game with 65 songs, and support for the Guitar Grip, Motion, Dpad/buttons or stylus.

Its been a long project and we are very pleased to have it release in time for the Neoflash compo here and the GBATemp homebrew bounty.

Please check our game and all the other games release for these fine competitions! Smiley From the screenies and descriptions it looks like the competition is going to be tough!

We've also launch a game site:
http://www.videogameshero.com where you can download the game, get game info/updates and check out the online leaderboards.

Enjoy!
