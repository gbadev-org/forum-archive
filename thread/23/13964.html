I've finished my first DS game in C without using PAlib or anything, called Robot Arm DS. :) It's a puzzle game where you have to make a simple "program" to tell the arm where to go and what to pick up/drop to try to deliver the flag to the end. It's actually a "clone" of an old game made in QBASIC by Robin Ohm.
