PuzzleManiak is a partial port of the Simon Tatham?s Portable Puzzle Collection (http://www.chiark.greenend.org.uk/%7Esgtatham/puzzles/) on Nintendo DS.

8 games are ported on NDS now: Tents, Dominosa, Mines, Bridges, Light Up aka Akari), Pattern (aka Picross), Net (aka Netwalk) and Untangle (aka Planarity).

All infos there: http://www.puzzlemaniak.com/
