Hey guys, I just got bored and made a particle system demo .. in the 1st version it runs automatically and in the 2nd one you use the stylus.


Screen:
[Images not permitted - Click here to view it]

http://etk.scener.org/projects/SphereParticles.rar

Keys (ONLY for "SphereParticlesAutoSphere"!):

Up - Increase Speed
Down - Decrease Speed

Right - Increase Radius
Left - Decrease Radius

A - Enable/Disable Gravity
B - Enable/Disable Bouncing particles
Y - Enable/Disable Color Change


---------------------------------------------
Keys (ONLY for "SphereParticles"!):

Stylus - Create Particles
*Move stylus fast to create particles moving fast

A - Hold Button A to enable gravity


Note: I've only tried it on dualis. It may not work on hw..
