#150463 - Jesse - Mon Feb 04, 2008 11:01 pm

Hyena is an AudioGame player for multiple platforms.

Inspired by the AudioBooks, Hyena allows game-books to be played with only sound and just one button. This makes it possible to play games while your body is occupied with other things, like driving or out running (or while sitting in class :)).

Hyena v1.00 is bundled with:
Lone Wolf - Flight From The Dark, by Joe Dever and Gary Chalk - http://www.projectaon.org/en/Main/FlightFromTheDark
and with speech synthisis generated by NeoSpeech - http://www.neospeech.com/

Homepage & downloads at http://www.collectingsmiles.com/hyena

Enjoy!

#151820 - Jesse - Wed Mar 05, 2008 3:49 pm

New version released:
* Scans the AudioGames/ directory for game-books, and gives a choice on which to load.
* Detailed the .gamebook file specifications at http://www.collectingsmiles.com/wiki/index.php?title=Hyena_AudioGame_specifications
* Integrated information on how to play the games.
* Better error messages if files are missing.
* Removed crashes due to memory fragmentation.
* Removed .xml format in favor for plain-text.

Flight From The Dark fixes:
* Fixed numerous game-logic flaws and lockups
* Echoes your Combat Skill whenever it has changed
* Echoes your amount of Gold whenever it has changed

I'm seem to have more problems where the sound-engine shuts down when you boot the game. But since it's not that often and it doesn't affect the game-experience I haven't bothered to track it down. If you have no audio when you start the game, just reboot.

#151926 - Jesse - Thu Mar 06, 2008 8:52 pm

I got surprised as well when I heard the this speech synthesis the first time. It's generated as mp3 offline on my PC and as you guessed, stored in the big data file. Having this kind of quality speech synthesis would probably not be possible on a DS since the speech engine is a couple of 100 MB and I also doubt that the DS would have the processing power needed. Also, it's worth noting that this particular voice is a couple of years old, and I've actually heard a few newer voices that sounds even better. I haven't been able to get hold of one of those for use with SAPI5.1 and a license that makes it usable. Working on it though. :)

But as to if it can be reused: The PC version of Hyena generates all this audio from the game-book itself, so as long as there is a game-book in Hyena format, the voice comes basically for free. I would prefer someone recoding the voice though. :)
