Thanks for the kind words, nice to see there are people around who still remember LineWars II. :-)

It was a lot of fun porting the code (the original LW2 was coded in 100% assembler, and LineWars DS is 100% C language, so I ported it to the opposite direction to what is normally done, I guess) and it actually was easier than I thought. The libnds and devkitARM are really nice to work with.

Looking forward to coding more stuff for NintendoDS in the future. :-)
