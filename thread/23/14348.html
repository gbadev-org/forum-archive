#143577 - tepples - Wed Oct 24, 2007 12:35 am

This feels different from Setsuzoku:
  • AcChen has solid north and south walls, so you can't just route around the top.
  • AcChen has no delay for clearing a pair of pieces, unlike Setsuzoku which has a delay proportional to the distance between them.
  • AcChen has multiple layers for pieces (symbolized by shades of a piece's background), and the path between the two pieces appears to be calculated on the higher layer, making it easier to find matches across layers.
  • Later levels have more distinct piece types.
  • The infamous bonus round, where clearing two pieces adds four.
