Ice Slider is a puzzle game, with the objective of collecting 10 coins on each level. However, there is a catch, once you start moving, you cannot stop until you hit an obstacle.

Obstacles include brick walls which cannot be passed, ice walls that break when bumped into, and directional walls which you can only move through in the indicated direction.

Controls are D-Pad to move, X to retry a level, and Y to exit to the menu.

All 80 levels have been tested and are all completable.
