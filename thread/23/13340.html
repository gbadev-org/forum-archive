DS homebrew announcements > Ka-BoOm v0.3

#130437 - stravingo - Sun Jun 03, 2007 12:06 pm

The version 0.3 of Ka-BoOm is available on my devblog. Some modifications in this version: when the DS is closed it pauses the game, some little bugs are removed, graphics are cleaned of remaining pink pixels (transparency color), however this is only minor modifications. The main improvement of this new version is to let you know your best scores to the entire world! :)
Indeed, thanks to Sumigushi (and thanks to Mollusk), it is now possible to send your best scores on leaderboard.palib.info, and they will appear on this page. For that, you must have configured WiFI on your DS and you have to be closed to a WiFi Access Point to the Internet.
