I've just released my homebrew project MaelstromDS (a version of Tempest). This is a re-working of the same demo I did for GBA a few years ago, and includes a feature that I wanted to add to the original: the ability to rotate the view freely, which is now dead easy thanks to the touch screen!

The demo is just a little tech demo really. To begin with, you are invulnerable. Press "Select" to become vulnerable.

Let me know what you think!
