#164537 - benjifs - Thu Nov 06, 2008 2:05 am

well this doesnt use a database mainly because of the problems with people messing around with it and stuff. Its basically just imaged based since the font was way too small for the look i wanted. It's just alot of work getting everything there thats why it takes so long.
I'm also a pokemon fan. since it first came out. I'm 20.

I have seen the pokedex for psp. I thought it was good but unless it was developed by the same person its almost an exact copy of one that was released for pc. I still have that one in my computer and when i saw the psp version i thought they were way too alike.

I'm going for more of a actual game look pokedex. What I'm thinking of adding once I'm done with all the pokemon is when you touch on a pokemon in the evolution you go to that pokemon. This might help if you're in pichu which is number 172 and want to get to pikachu number 25.

In the meantime if anyone has any suggestions, features or bugs just let me know.
