You will have to excuse my poor english before i start.

At the begining, there was a unique entity without form , but with a soul. This entity was call Hann. After weeks, hann decided to create his own desires, throught 6 creators.

(The creators BG is fully detailed in french BG)

Oxana, Malarh?a, Dioxis, Equishene, Ferwen, M?liador.

All this creations was called to "Order".
To equilibrate the world, Hann created chaos, the exact opposite of the actual world.

During 1000 years, each one of this god created 6 race.

(The same, they are fully detailed in french BG)

After wars between the jealous chaos and pure gods, the 6 creators diseapered, abandoning 6 artefacts on earth.

Every 2000 year, a special planet alignement allow powefull wizards to open a gate to chaos world.
This one try to spread on Kirya (the actual world) and to take it's vital energy. As the prophecy says, every 2000 year, a man is choosen to fight them.
He is the descendant of the first man who received from Hann itself the power to send chaos back to shadow world.

As i sayd, the french one is really fully detailed. The scenario takes place when a brother and his sister are going to the ceremony to open the gate to shadow world.
