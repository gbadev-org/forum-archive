#163939 - Jayenkai - Wed Oct 15, 2008 11:02 pm

"UP or X also to launch the ball"
Already does..

"Start 3 lives instead of just 1 life"
You're already getting an extra ball at the start of a round, if you'd already lost it.. So, there's really no need for extra lives..

"Make the pallet size revert to normal at the beginning"
It only seems "Easy with a big paddle" because there's nothing to dodge yet! If you play the PC version, and make it to level 4, you'll notice that some of the pods are red flashing death pods!! Hit one of those, and your little paddle takes a beating. Hit it 4 times, your paddle explodes, game over..
So, once those are in, the longer paddle's become less useful, and it's more of a challenge to get a paddle at just the right size.

Scoring
1 hit of a Pod = 5 points, then 10, then 25, and last 50.. If you get to the "Drop the Pods" part, keep 'em all bouncing and you can collect plenty more points..

I haven't scored the pickups yet.. Mostly because I've a few more of them to add, first!

I'm not keen on having negative scores for the balls, since if you've a load of balls flying around, it could get really annoying having to then additionally avoid the things! They're just balls, and should stay that way.
