#136680 - preussie - Fri Aug 03, 2007 8:47 pm

First of all I would like to say THANK YOU to all the people who are involved in NDS lib development and the homebrew community in general for sharing their knowledge! Without all the examples and explanations people like me could not develop any stuff on the DS.

Here is the first version of my DS port of AcChen. AcChen is a simple to learn ?find the matching tile? game. It is more Arcade-Style than MahJongg. It contains a lot of different levels. Each level takes only a couple of minutes to finish.

More about AcChen ( screenshots / description / download ) can be found here:
http://preussie.miscstuff.de/ds/

I only had the chance to test the binary on my DS Lite with a R4 Slot1 card. Any comments about working/not working hardware are appreciated!

preussie
