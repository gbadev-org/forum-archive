#142128 - Dark Knight ez - Thu Oct 04, 2007 9:37 pm

I sort of suggested working together when our projects both started (around the same time). He didn't want to join forces on a single RTS project.
He chose to take a completely different approach to coding an RTS engine, using software rendering. I chose to rely on the DS 2D engine myself, using actual tiles and sprites.
Combining the two engines is not an option for me due to how different they are, and I'd consider it pretty much pointless seeing as how my own engine is far more complete than the C&C-DS engine.

However, my engine isn't really suited for something like C&C as it is. The diversity in environment and in structures probably means it won't all fit in VRAM at once, meaning it'll have to be streamed in when needed. That's not something my engine supports. Not sure if I'll ever look into adding that feature myself. We'll see. First I want to finish the engine as it is and release new RTSes alongside of it.
