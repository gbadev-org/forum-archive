Space Impakto DS

Download:
http://rel.phatcode.net/junk.php?id=101

GameType:
Bullet-Hell SHMUP (Space shooter/Shoot 'em up) for your DS
5 Stages of insane bullet patterns

Features:
Code:
   * 2d gameplay
   * 3d background
   * Optimized texture use via texture packing
   * Oldskool 8-bit/4bit sprites
   * Newskool bullet hell gameplay
   * Source included
   * Replay system
   * Button config
   * Varied and animated Bosses
   * Proceduraly generated backgrounds
   * Proceduraly generated 3d models
   * 3 types of ships
   * 2 Game Modes (Normal and Boss Rush)
