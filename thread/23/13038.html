After playing this game for a while, I came to notice that it accepts abbreviations (several I found include 'doc' and 'sub'), which I personally don't think should be allowed. It is also unclear as to when plural versions of words are allowed, it seems to be arbitrary. I like the inclusion of Old-English words, but I think there were some missing (including I believe, 'olde'). And then there were regular words that were downright missing (only one I can remember is 'meld').
I don't know where you got your world list from, and I know it would be a huge pain to go through and correct any changes you might wish to make to it, but maybe an entirely different source for acceptable words could be found.

I also noticed that it was possible to 'cheat' a little bit. The list of found words at the top are in alphabetical order, from least letters in the word to most. So you can sometimes know what letter(s) a word starts with that you have not found yet. I do not know if this was intentional or not.

I agree with zAlbee's ideas, and I have also encountered that same freeze, but I don't recall what mode I was in when it happened, just that it was after solving a wrap and moving on to the next.
