Mechabouncer, you're the only one I heard this from yet. From my own experience the most annoying thing about DPG playback are the new block artifacts and slightly "warbled" (looks like the picture morphs around) video. Sound playback actually improved a lot and so did color representation.

I toyed around a bit with the new DPGTools and it still encodes DPG the same way as previous versiosn (here's the mencoder commandline: C:\dpgtls\dpgencplugin\mencoder.exe -v - -o "C:\Documents and Settings\user\Desktop\nedivx-tnines-sample.dpgm1v" -noautosub -nosound -frames 1502 -demuxer rawvideo -rawvideo fps=24:w=256:h=128:format=bgr24:size=98304 -ovc lavc -lavcopts vcodec=mpeg1video:vbitrate=512:keyint=60:intra_matrix=[kvcd intra matrix, breaks forum layout] -ofps 24 -of rawvideo, audio is still encoded to MP2 with twolame), only difference is that the header now says its DPG4, but with no difference actual content I wouldn't expect any playback issues if your old Videos are encoded with BatchDPG or dpgconv.

I haven't tested MP3 playback or any of the formats you mentioned myself, though, but if there were any issues (especially with MP3) I'm sure you'd hear about it. No idea what causes your problems. :(
