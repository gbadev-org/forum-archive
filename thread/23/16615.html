#169299 - another world - Mon Jun 29, 2009 5:30 pm

the ak2i works fine with wifi. use the akaio firmware and make sure your router is set for wep. the ak2i runs in ds mode and can only use wep encryption as with a normal nds. also the broadcasting of the ssid needs to be on, this is required of homebrew.

i've started an irc chan where i idle with the akaio devs. /server irc2.gbatemp.net:5190 /join #acekard. you can always ask them if you don't want to take my word for it.
