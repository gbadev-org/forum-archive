#18780 - Gopher - Mon Apr 05, 2004 6:44 am

with the f2a you can't tell it not to install the boot loader, but you CAN specify what rom to use as a bootloader. Set your rom to the boot loader in the config screen and ignore the warning that you're only installing a loader and no files. It'll work just fine.
