#8535 - tepples - Sun Jul 13, 2003 5:47 am

tangl_99 wrote:
But, What is the Transparent Color in BG?

For backgrounds in indexed color modes (0, 1, 2, and 4), color 0 is transparent, and entry 0 in the palette controls what "sky" color appears behind everything else. However, there seems to be no way to send a sprite behind the background in 15-bit RGB modes (3 and 5).
