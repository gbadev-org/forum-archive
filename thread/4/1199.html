If you realised that an RPG might be a bit too much for you, why not make a different genre? Or if you really want to make an RPG, keep it very limited: Not a huge world, just a couple of screens, no incredibly advanced point system, no trillion different fighting moves... #5955 - DekuTree64 - Tue May 13, 2003 7:55 pm

Yea, I'm making an RPG, and it is a lot more work than it seems. The engine programming itself isn't that hard, but making a map editor, and editors for all your character/monster data and stuff takes a while. Then after all that's done, the amount of maps and stuff you need for a full game is the real challenge. Unless you're planning to do a short story, or are more into the making of the game than the programming, an RPG is a bad idea.
The engine part is really fun if you're a beginner though, since it's pretty straightforward and you learn a lot when you're not stuck on little things all the time.
