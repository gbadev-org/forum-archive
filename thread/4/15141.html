#152021 - tepples - Sat Mar 08, 2008 1:32 pm

Code:
attr0 = (attr0 & 0xFF00) | (y & 0x00FF);
attr1 = (attr1 & 0xFE00) | (x & 0x01FF);

To learn how this works, see Beginners' FAQ and search the page for "off the top".
