#5013 - niltsair - Thu Apr 17, 2003 3:20 pm

It's by using the Size_X in conjonction to the shaping attribute of the sprite.

#####SIZE_8===SIZE_16===SIZE_32===SIZE_64
SQUARE#8x8--------16x16---------32x32--------64x64
TALL###8x16--------8x32---------16x32--------32x64
WIDE##16x8---------32x8---------32x16--------64x32

I took this from the PernProject website, excellent for gettings the fundamentals of GBA programming. Here's the page :
