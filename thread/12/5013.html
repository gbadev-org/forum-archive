If you know Pascal, C and C++, maybe you can be of some help in a little initiative we having brewing over at PGD(Pascal Game Development).

We are trying to add a GBA target for the Free Pascal compiler. It already has ARM support and with some more work and interest the author is willing to add the ARM7 support aswell once we have something workable. I have done minor work myself and gotten as far as to make some tweeks adding the actual target to the compiler, but being as I do not know the compiler too well and some aspects of the conversion are beyond me, I could use the help of others.

I will be creating a page shortly for all of this and even to host the source/port of FPC for GBA.

You can visit PGD and read about our efforts in this thread

The idea is to make a native Pascal compiler that supports the GBA platform.
