You have 1 week to try to figure out what kind of cartridges are pictured above. Click to get the full-size image (with numbered carts). There are 7 different carts and you have to tell me what kind of cartridges they are.

An example; If you think that cartridge #1 is an official GBA gamepak cartridge, and #2 is a wireless link-adapter you would write:

Easy, isnt it? ;)

The first person who can correctly identify all the 7 cartridges will win a nice prize.
