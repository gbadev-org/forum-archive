#98315 - schoebey - Mon Aug 14, 2006 5:14 pm

hey everyone,

after a long time in development, our "little" adventure-game "The Last Seal" is finally done.

Originally, we wanted to go commercial with it and already were in contact with some companies, but after having run several market analysis, they decided that there wasn't enough money to be made with this kind of game on the GBA. So we decided to release it for free instead of letting it rot on our harddisks.

There still are a lot of bugs in it, but as you can imagine, our motivation hasn't exactly peaked when we made the decision to release it as freeware, so we didn't spend too much time chasing after all those bugs (there shouldn't be (m)any big ones, just tiny glitches here and there, it should still be playable)

Grab it from http://www.ancor.ch/tls and have fun playing it ;)
