For the last few weeks members of #DSDEV have been actively discussing with a number of developers the creation of a Central Hub "home" for Nintendo DS homebrew.

Dev-Scene.com is the result. Dev-Scene is dedicated to NDS homebrew releases and developement with upto-date news/Forums/Local Mirrors/Feeds and first and only complete homebrew catalog.

For first timers it's worth reading http://www.dev-scene.com/Welcome_Message

What Is Dev-Scene and what can we do for you?

A Central Hub for DS homebrew - A living community built on the contributions of homebrewers.

Homebrew End-User
* Dev-Scene NDS Homebrew News Keep upto-date with NDS homebrew news. Updated daily
* Dev-Scene Forum A forum is a forum is a forum.. Discuss everything homebrew related and things not Smile
* Dev-Scene Homebrew Catalogue A grand collection of the most popular homebrew - preview/review.. Talk to developers in the discussion tab.
* Dev-Scene Library Download all your homebrew at a single location. Updated daily.
* Planet Dev-Scene "Flexible feed aggregator" - Stay upto-date with NDS development and releases with an real-time NDS blog news cast.

Homebrew Developers
* Dev-Scene NDS Homebrew News This service allows you to post your OWN updates on our live news feeds. Updated almost automatically for the world to see, Don't wait for someone else to write about your homebrew.
* Dev-Scene Forum A forum is a forum is a forum.. Discuss everything homebrew related and things not Smile
* Dev-Scene Homebrew Catalog Tell the world about your release and keep in-touch with the users.
* Dev-Scene Library offers a permanent mirror/backup of all your software. Offering you security incase your host shuts down or your releases are lost.
* Planet Dev-Scene If your running a blog for your NDS news and releases. Keep users and the community upto date in real time.


If your an active developer and looking for hosting? We can help you with that too - sub domains/domain hosting is available with PHP/MYSQL/FTP/EMAIL.

P.S. You are welcome to join us at #Dev-Scene on irc.blitzed.org! ;)
