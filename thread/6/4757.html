There are several perfect GBA picture viewers available, but they do not satisfy all my needs. So I wrote my own one. May be you will also find the viewer usefull.

Main features:
1. Any image size
2. Fast and smooth scrolling
3. Zoom out to x2, x4, x8
4. Hold pozition of the center while zooming

The ROM with pictures must be created with ROM maker (Windows)
(bmp, jpg and gif are supported):

www.msh-tools.com/GBA

Pictures are uncompressed in the ROM, and this is stand-alone ROM, (not plugin)
