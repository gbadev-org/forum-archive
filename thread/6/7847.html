Announcements And Comments > VBAstep (Mac debugger) version 0.2 released

#64401 - ribrdb2 - Thu Dec 22, 2005 1:12 am

A new version of VBAstep is available at http://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=4590

This version includes a faster core, improved gdb and gprof support, a new Tile Viewer, and improvements to the debug log. Please test it and give me feedback and feature requests.

Planned features for the next release:
* Sound support
* Ability to launch and automatically connect to gdb
* Throttling
* Automatic palette selection for the Tile Viewer

Currently it only runs on Mac OS X. Linux support is on hold until 1) I have access to a Linux box with Gorm installed and 2) there's a demand for it.
