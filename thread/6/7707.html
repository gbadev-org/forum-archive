For lack of a suggestions section, I'm going to post this here.

A Wiki Space has been set up for this site after much general approval from it's members. The domain for the site is gbadev.wikispaces.com

This space should eventually be a collection of open source tutorials for various topics concerning GBA development. The idea is that those of you who ask for very soecific tutorials can, when a solution is found, add one to the Wiki so that others can share the same solution.

The only limit on the Wiki is that you must be a member of the Wiki to add/change the site. This is to reduce the chances that someone could go on there and totally wipe out stuff for fun, or just add stuff at random as seems to happen at a lot of other sites. PM me or check out the site for info on becomming a member.

Admins: I ask, if it is in your favor, that this announcement be mirrored on the main page to increase awareness. Thanks!
