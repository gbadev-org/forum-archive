This is a hacked Pokemon savegame which allows to boot multiboot roms from a pokemon ruby or sapphire savegame-flash by exploiting a freature which allows to run small ingame scripts from sav.
(tested on real hardware and visual boy advance)

Once you have the sav on your pokemon cart you will not need any additional hardware. Just start Pokemon load the save, and talk to the guy in front of you.

A demonstration of this (booting mpzpong):
http://web264.server46.rhs-it.de/nb/Pokeloader.zip
to change the demo replace the section 0x10000+ (upper 64k) with your demo.

I hope you enjoy it.
