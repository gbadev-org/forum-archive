#9679 - cosmic4z - Wed Aug 13, 2003 5:06 pm

hi antysix,

The restrictions are shown on the download page (www.gbatools.com/lm_download.htm).

They are:

Map size is limited to 96 * 96 tiles.
Undo / redo functions are disabled.
Importing layers from 8bit bitmap (.BMP) files is disabled.
Exporting layers as 8bit bitmap (.BMP) files is disabled.
Exporting maps as 16bit bitmap (.TGA) files is disabled.
Saving of tile attributes is disabled.
Saving of the brush container is disabled.
