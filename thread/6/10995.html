Hello,

A friend and me finally got around to release an unfinished GBA side-project we worked on some years ago.
It is available for download here (binary & full sources included):
http://www.miracleworld.net/spacetapper/

[Images not permitted - Click here to view it] [Images not permitted - Click here to view it]

Code:
SPACE TAPPER
You are Roby, intergalactic bartender, and with each succesful job at keeping the customers at bay you are promoted to more exotic, demanding locales.

The game is rip.. inspired from the classic arcade Tapper, released 1982 by Bally/Midway.

Features: broken beers, screen shaking, alien customers that behave differently, insanely difficult levels, disappointingly missing ending, and the coolest bartender ever!

ALCOHOL IN A GAME FOR A NINTENDO CONSOLE !
(this is our best selling point)
(alright, we're not licensed..)

Hope you can enjoy it.
Please note that the game is pretty difficult, and control pretty tight. There's various little way to improve your techniques. Kudos to whoever can reach level 7 in hard mode.
