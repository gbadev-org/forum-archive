#101109 - tepples - Sat Sep 02, 2006 5:52 pm

Non-literal copying is still copying. It takes a judge to distinguish non-literal copying (likely infringement) from appropriation of ideas (not infringement). Capcom v. Data East (the Fighter's History case; use Google) was the latter.

Apply a texture so that the model doesn't look just like Link, and Nintendo will have to sue Rankin-Bass over its Christmas specials before Nintendo sues you.
