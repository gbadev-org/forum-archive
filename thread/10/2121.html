#11019 - tepples - Tue Sep 23, 2003 3:39 pm

The GBA uses raw 8-bit signed PCM, which is generally too big to store music.

It's possible to interpret .mid (format specs) on the GBA and control a mixer program, but it seems most people use more console-friendly tracked formats such as .s3m instead.
