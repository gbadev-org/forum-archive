#103222 - tepples - Wed Sep 20, 2006 2:35 am

If you have the melody and stuff from a .mid file, it's not difficult to transcribe it into a .xm file. In fact, Modplug Tracker can probably do most of the work for you.

One problem with using .mid in your program is that you have to have samples for all General MIDI instruments and all General MIDI drums, which can take a lot of space.
