#66902 - tepples - Sat Jan 14, 2006 6:47 am

pure_ev1l wrote:
but got stuck on the bit where modplug conversion truncates after 64

I think you're supposed to split long patterns into two.

Quote:
has mod got a limit of 4 channels?

The Amiga hardware had four HW sample playback channels. Later modplayers extended the format for up to 32 channels or so.

Quote:
mod blows.

In practice, .mod blows mostly because of lack of support for doing a volume effect at the same time as another effect. The .s3m format, on the other hand, has a dedicated column for volume or panning effects.
