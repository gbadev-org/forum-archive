#98783 - tepples - Thu Aug 17, 2006 3:53 pm

Edelnutte wrote:
Is the GBA really to slow? How much Mhz would be needed?

GBA CPU is a 16.8 MHz ARM7TDMI. Helix MP3 decoder needs 26 MHz to decode a stereo MP3 at 44.1 kHz, provided 57 KiB of zero-wait-state memory is available. This is true on the DS (with its cache) but not on the GBA (which has only 32 KiB of IWRAM). I haven't looked into MP2 though.
