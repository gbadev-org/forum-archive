#158177 - keldon - Fri Jun 06, 2008 7:40 am

No you cannot make them in logic in your normal manor. The mod format uses one sample per channel with one note polyphony on each so you don't have the same freedom that you have in a typical music arrangement environment.

Check out renoise for something to work with.
