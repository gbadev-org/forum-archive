#12951 - tepples - Tue Dec 02, 2003 5:13 am

There are two kinds of audio player for Game Boy: those that decode MP3 in dedicated hardware on the cartridge (such as SongPro) and those that recompress the MP3s into a different format that the GBA's CPU can actually decode in real time. Because you mention a flash cart, it looks like you're looking for the latter. Try hacking something up with my 8ad codec. I'm sorry that it's not more automated for the point-and-drool types.
