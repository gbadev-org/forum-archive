#62353 - tepples - Wed Nov 30, 2005 12:03 am

If you bought a used silver DS, you most likely have the old firmware, and you can buy a PassMe and use any game.

If you bought a new silver DS, you most likely have the new firmware, and you need to buy a PassMe2 customized for a supported game. Only games with a "VHD/JED/XSVF SAV/GBA" next to them on this list are known to work; most notably, versions of Meteos released outside Japan do not.
