#61147 - tepples - Thu Nov 17, 2005 5:45 pm

FlashMe currently contains a modified copy of Nintendo's firmware, which is thought by some gbadev.org members to be a copyright infringement. Other sites distributing Xbox firmware hacked in the same way have got in legal trouble. Future versions of FlashMe may require an original copy of the firmware to avoid warez allegations.

NoFlashMe is potentially in even more hot water, as it contains an exact copy of multiple official firmware revisions.

In addition, someone wanted a dump of the iQue DS firmware to see what the extra data represents. I'm guessing it might be an ideographic font.
