#88451 - josath - Mon Jun 19, 2006 5:13 pm

Here's what I understand as far as capturing downloadable demos and turning them into something useful:

1. get a laptop with a wifi card that is compatible with wifime
2. either get the special packet capture tool by firefly, or just use one of the generic ones
3. Find the channel the DS is downloading on, and only capture that channel (no channel-hopping)
4. while keeping the laptop as close as physically possible to the transmitting DS, download the demo at least 5 times while capturing packets
5. send the data to someone who can reassemble it into a working demo, or try using the tool which does it automatically (it's somewhere in this forum, search for it)
