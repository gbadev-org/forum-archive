#63952 - m2pt5 - Sat Dec 17, 2005 11:57 pm

Get Moonshell.

After you unzip it, one of the folders is called "wintools". In that folder is "dsbuild.exe". Drag and drop your .nds files on that program (or even a shortcut to it) and it will produce a .ds.gba.

I use this method to run homebrew on my Supercard. (SC needs a loader, but needs it called .nds. So I drop it through the program, put the .ds.gba on my flash card, and rename it.)
