#126204 - dantheman - Fri Apr 20, 2007 11:51 pm

Most shops I've seen are selling it for $40 or so, and one shop I've seen even sells it for about $30, plus about $10 for a microSD card to go with it, though I also heard that it typically takes 2-3 weeks for shipping from that store.

The R4 is more compact and easier for newbies to use, but in terms of homebrew compatibility, slot-2 devices are still a slightly better choice since older homebrew that accesses the filesystem will often not work on slot-1 devices. The additional RAM is nice too.
