#121103 - dantheman - Thu Mar 08, 2007 11:31 pm

Yeah, you'd want DSFTP. Since it supports, DLDI, it should work with most major carts. The problem is that, like Lick said, it's rather slow. I usually get about 30 kb/s when writing to the cart.

However, like Tepples suggested, it's great for when you have to transfer a relatively small file and don't want to bother with the card reader. I have had issues writing many small files at once (tends to make DSFTP freeze and crash) but sending over one file at a time works well. Not very useful for anything over a few megabytes though.

It's also a good solution to have on your cart in case for some reason your DS becomes the only thing that can read your card correctly and you want to salvage your files (I've seen it happen before on SCdev)
