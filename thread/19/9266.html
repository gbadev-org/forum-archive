#79928 - chishm - Mon Apr 17, 2006 6:51 am

Uh, almost all homebrew (with the exception of a few flashcart reliant games like Tickle Girl DS) will run on GBAMP. The SMT dumper isn't exactly legal, even if you overlook it's purpose. It was built using the official SDK.

However, if you really are interested in homebrew then the GBAMP is fine. If you are interested in dumping games, then the M3 and SuperCard won't help, since they trash the DS card's header too. The good thing about the GBAMP is the loader is open source, so you can modify it to do what you want. And once you have installed FW Nitro on your DS, it won't matter if the flash cart trashes the DS header, since it can dump to CF media via GBAMP, M3 or SC directly.
