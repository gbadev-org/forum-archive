Stupid question:
I bought a passme and flashed my DS. I build me a jtag cable and think I reprogrammed my passme to a passme2. But how do I know (without another DS) if I did it correctly?

#67255 - pepsiman - Mon Jan 16, 2006 2:32 pm

zxr750j wrote:
Stupid question:
I bought a passme and flashed my DS. I build me a jtag cable and think I reprogrammed my passme to a passme2. But how do I know (without another DS) if I did it correctly?

1) Insert PassMe.
2) Insert GBA cart with homebrew in GBA slot.
3) Hold Select during boot to disable FlashMe.
Homebrew runs = still PassMe1.
White screens = PassMe2 or broken.
4) Write SRAM to GBA cart.
2) Insert GBA cart with homebrew in GBA slot.
3) Hold Select during boot to disable FlashMe.
Homebrew now runs = PassMe2.
White screens = broken.
