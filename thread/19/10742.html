DS Flash Equipment > Noflashme does not restore a DS Lite to full functionality.

#97439 - Falkentyne - Wed Aug 09, 2006 7:38 pm

Just to let you know, noflashme does not restore a DS Lite to full functionality. It will boot and will play DS and GBA games (I don't know about wifi), the Alarm clock icon and brightness icon works, but trying to change any of the options, picochat or DS download play hard locks the DSL.

I suspect the "V6" firmware that noflashme installs is for one of the old DS' that have the brightness chip hardware of the DSL (which is why some people can install flashme7 for the DSL on their DS and get 4 brightness levels).
