How do I setup moonshell and gbamp multiboot on my cf?

#68665 - HyperHacker - Thu Jan 26, 2006 9:31 am

Multiboot: Put the .gba file on your CF card. Go to FC game and run it. Don't turn your DS off until it says it's safe to shut off (only takes a few seconds). As with any firmware update it's best to use AC power.
Moonshell: Run WriteFiles_IMFS.bat, then copy _BOOT_MP.nds to your CF card.
