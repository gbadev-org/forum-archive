#48020 - Sappharad - Sat Jul 16, 2005 6:56 am

I dont see why anyone needs to contact FireFly to learn how to dump them, unless he's written a DS simulator to grab perfect dumps.

Just do it the way everyone did it before his program was released. Use the Airopeek demo if you're running Windows, or a similar porgram that allows raw wireless packet dumps to be made. Transfer and capture several times (I believe 5 is sufficent) so you know theres a good chance you don't have any missing data, and save the dumps. Worry about being able to reconstruct them later.

The nice part about dumping them this way is you don't need a card with a RALINK chipset. (Just something with drivers that let you do a raw dump)
I thought I'd point this out in case anyone has the capability to do so and didn't know it.
