#53108 - joebob180 - Tue Sep 06, 2005 5:16 am

Do you have your DS and GBAMP firmware flashed yet? because moonshell works with GBAMP v2, very well if I might add.

The GBAMP will probably be more valuble in the longrun, especially with all thats being developed for it, NESds, and ScummVm, along with moonshell, what more could you ask for (SNESds?).

But G6 sounds like just another flashcart packaged with a passme right now, so if your thinking of going the flashcard route I would suggest getting a cheap flashcart now. I think moonshell works with most flashcarts although its not quite as easy, you have to use a filesystem thats created using a batch file and appended to the rom.

You can also use flashme to dispose of passme, although to use lashme you need GBAMP or a Flashcard AND a passme or wifime.

The only reason I would get a flashcard is that you can use the larger .nds.gba files, for a GBAMP .nds file cant be more than 4 mb (although it can load from CF card).

The firmware has also been hacked for supercard now, if your interested.
