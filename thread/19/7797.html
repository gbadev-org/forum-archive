#63725 - tepples - Thu Dec 15, 2005 9:11 pm

If you make a Nintendo DS program, and you get the ARM7 and ARM9 CPUs to synchronize properly, then it should work with any PassMe2 and compatible game. However, nowadays you see more and more people who use FlashMe v5, as it's easiest to use PassMe2 only once to install FlashMe, and you can't use a GBAMP with PassMe2.
