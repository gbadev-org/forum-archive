#160689 - tepples - Sun Jul 20, 2008 12:36 pm

  1. Insert GBAMP.
  2. Remove all unlicensed cards from SLOT-1.
  3. Hold Select+Start.
  4. Power on the DS.
  5. Choose "Start GBA game".
  6. After the red on black "not licensed by Nintendo" screen, choose "Game" and then the .gba file you want.

The program has to be smaller than 250 KB or so, and it has to be linked for multiboot. If you were running it on the GBA without a flash card before, you were probably doing it through an MBV2 or XBOO cable.
