DS Flash Equipment > The new Acekard+ : FAT support, lower prices

#116747 - felix123 - Mon Jan 29, 2007 2:10 am

This was released half a month ago, but the announcement was only made today. It is identified by the string "Acekard+" on the title screen. The new price is RMB320 (~US$40) for a package of one Acekard+ and one Japan made Kingston/PNY 1G mSD card. Free replacements will be available for old Acekard users. A DLDI patch is available. It supports both FAT and the old AKFS.
