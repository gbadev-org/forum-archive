#112766 - tepples - Wed Dec 20, 2006 1:31 am

Any FAT library that supports the microSD-based SuperCard Lite will also support the SuperCard SD and miniSD, as they're pretty much the same device. But the reverse isn't necessarily true, as a few hardware changes happened between the miniSD version and the Lite version.
