#57073 - JaJa - Thu Oct 13, 2005 3:08 pm

Ok this is hard.
Find a small hex bit (some kinda ratchet bit i think) that's the right size and superglue it too the screw. Leave overnight then undo using pliers.
With luck the glue will hold.
Otherwise you'll have to dremel a new slot and use a small slot screwdriver.
I once did this to get a heatsink screw out of a laptop.
You'll need to buy a new screw, unles you can break the screw off the hex bit.
