#59395 - chishm - Tue Nov 01, 2005 1:39 pm

It can only handle a certain number of characters in the command. SO if you have too many characters in there (like when loading many demos at once) it won't work. Try renaming your demos to shorter names. Also, the DS doesn't transmit strong enough for the computer to receive replies when you are sitting too far away. The best way to fix this is to get a more sensitive antenna for your wireless card.
