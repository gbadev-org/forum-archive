#63795 - Empyrean - Fri Dec 16, 2005 9:47 am

The preprogrammed PassMe2 is just to save you some time from programming it yourself.

Make sure you preprogram it to a game you already have (original cart).

You don't NEED to FlashMe your DS, but it will save you from having to rewrite the SRAM every single time (though to be honest I didn't have to do that with my M3.)

There is a minute chance for your DS to screw up during the FlashMe process, but with a fully charged DS, you most-likely won't encounter any of that jazz. With me, the FlashMe installed just perfectly without interruption or complication.
