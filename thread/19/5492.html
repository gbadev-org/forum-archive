#40592 - Theodore104 - Wed Apr 20, 2005 7:00 pm

Your message is a little hard to understand, but I think you're saying that dsPaint works and other programs that you've tried don't?

Programs will only work with the passthrough method if they've been specifically compiled to do so. A lot of the files around are just simple binaries, so they'll work on an emulator, but they won't work with this method.

Try some of the following; unless I'm misremembering, they should all work with the passthrough method...

http://natrium42.com/downloads/
http://www.auby.no/index.php?id=15

People usually name files that will work with the passthrough ".ds.gba", and files that won't ".bin". That's not always the case, though....
