#75011 - ssysrzz - Thu Mar 09, 2006 11:59 am

I had the same problem as you. After some fiddling and some impetus from Pepsiman (a living NDS god ;), I finally got it to boot of my cart doing the following:

The flashme file is a nds file so you will need to prepend the ndsloader which I downloaded from the flashme site:

1) At command prompt:
copy /b ndsloader.bin + flashme.nds flashme.nds.gba

2) Burn the newly created flashme.nds.gba file to your cart.

3) Chuck cart into DS. And run wifime to boot the flashme app.

And to add to that: Make sure you format your cart before burning and make sure the cart software will not burn its own loader. The only thing you want on your cart is the flashme.nds.gba file.

And this from DjoeNWork of#dsdev/efnet:
<DjoeNWorK> also yo have to format the card not with the f2a ultra software but within your gba/ds itself to erase it complete (this includes the multibootloader)
<DjoeNWorK> on the f2a pages somewhere is said hwhat the button combination is to go into the selftest area (from where you can complete clean your f2aultra)

#75199 - xmas - Fri Mar 10, 2006 11:38 pm

Finally it worked! jesus..too much complicated informations for such an easy trick...i think it's not only me frustrated.. :-(

anyways,
If you are F2A Ultra user and stuck with this crazy flashme thing..
here's exact steps. i bet this works 100% if you have right flashme version for yourDS firmware.

1.http://www.ndshb.com/cgi-bin/cfiles/cfiles.cgi?0,0,0,0,10
download "passme2flashme.gba"

2.burn "passme2flashme.gba" with PowerWriter v2.61
make sure to burn only "passme2flashme.gba" to your F2AU.
you don't have to worry about bootloader or anything. just burn it!

3.******THIS IS IMPORTANT********
Insert F2A Ultra into GBA Slot.
Do NOT Insert PASSME or PASSME2 YET!!!! Only F2A Ultra.

4."passme2flashme.gba" will load as GBA mode.
*Match the code with your DS card (the one you'll put to passme)
*Make sure to select exact number. Also there might be 2 same numbers.
Just choose the top one.
*Your DS card number's shown in front.(bottom right corner)

5.If you found one, click A. you will see "SRAM Written"

6.Power Off

7.Now, Insert PASSME or PASSME2 into DS slot. Make sure to put your DS card to passme.

8.Power On..... and no more two white screens.

9.http://www.dspassme.com/FlashMe.shtml
follow the instruction and you are ready to use homebrews with F2A Ultra

hope this helps
