#50776 - crudhacker - Fri Aug 12, 2005 10:35 pm

kv83 wrote:
I know it's maybe a really n00b question, but why isn't it possible to send homemade .nds files with WiFiMe by firefly without a GBA-flashcard? I mean, they are not to big to be a 'demo', or do I do something wrong (when the demo starts, it only shows the nintendo logo)


You have to load homebrew stuff with a GBA flashcard because the DS checks for a RSA encryption. Our homebrew files don't have they arn't signed with the nintendo key. Thats why it hangs on the nintendo logo. Nintendo demos can load because they HAVE been signed with the nintendo key.
