#44012 - Maverick - Sun May 29, 2005 10:12 am

Download the latest drivers and program from here, saving to C:\DSDev\Tools\WMB:

http://users.belgacom.net/bn967347/download.htm

Install the drivers by following this tutorial:

http://www.aaronrogers.com/nintendods/wifime.php

Download the latest version of WMBFront from here, saving it to C:\DSDev\Tools\WMB:

http://headkaze.webpal.info/

Download all of the demos from this location, saving them to C:\DSDev\Tools\WMB\Data:

http://www.akkit.org/DS/E3/

Unzip all of the files you downloaded.

Open WMBFront, click Browse, select what you wish to upload, click OK, Click upload.

Hope this makes sense.
