#50447 - tssf - Tue Aug 09, 2005 8:35 pm

I actually painted my GBA Movie Player to match my DS scheme. Using a metallic silver and a glossy black for the back.

My father kept saying "use plastic paint! use plastic paint!" but..trust me, don't use plastic paint. It's too soft, it gauges easily on the plastic, and when it's hot out, the paint actually bonds to the CF card and/or Nintendo DS itself inside the slot. The metallic paint worked *GREAT* though, and matches perfectly with the DS.

When I paint my DS (I'm still looking for "Y" screw driver bits.. anyone know where to get one without having to import?) I'm planning on painting it all gold.. It'll definately be cool, but I'm having trouble taking the DS apart to even do so.

At any rate, I'm sure there are others here who have done this.. It'd be neat if people started posting pictures of their customized DS's.. My warrenty went void as soon as my bar code "rubbed off".. I pulled the plastic protective film off the barcode and now it's a completely white lable. Oops.
