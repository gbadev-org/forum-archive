Just a note to self (and maybe other noobs) that the Dell Wireless 1350 mini-pci card built-in to my Dell 600m laptop is not based on the RT2500 chipset as shown in entry #51 of the ndiswrapper list (though I haven't tried it out):

http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net/mediawiki/index.php/List

At the time of my visiting that link, here was the info for it:

"Card: Dell Wireless 1350 WLAN MiniPCI Card - Dell INSPIRON 2200
* Chipset: Broadcom Corporation BCM4318 [AirForce One 54g] 802.11g Wireless LAN Controller (rev 02)..."

Just for completeness' sake, one of the noob stickies links to the list of WMB-compatible wireless cards:

http://ralink.rapla.net/
