#162846 - another world - Sun Sep 14, 2008 4:13 am

all flash kits have this problem. usually taking it out and putting it back in makes better contact. some require you prop the board up with a small wedge of paper to force the contact. also try blowing some compressed air into your slot1. this fixed my r4 contact issue.

you do know the r4 is a dead kit now? the team is involved in a heavy lawsuit in japan, they stopped producing the kits over 4 months ago, and they have no plans to release any fixes or updates to the firmware.

perhaps you should send it back, get a refund and pick up an acekard 1.2. the ak team fixed the hardware FAT problem on the 1.2 build. my acekard rpg is the best kit i've owned (i've had 6 different flash kits so far).

anyways before you take it apart and macgyver it, did you try a different msd card? the one you have might be having problems. if you don't own a second its time to pick up a 2gb for 8 bucks.
