#117031 - tepples - Wed Jan 31, 2007 9:56 pm

Noflashme counts backwards from 100% down to 0%. If your DS loses power between 100% and 5%, you can recover using the failsafe code that FlashMe installed; otherwise, you're hosed.

Are you trying to unflash for warranty purposes, to make the DS appear not flashed to Nintendo repair techs? If so, you can use FlashMe Stealth instead.
