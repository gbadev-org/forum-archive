USB wifi cards are not compatible, you need a PCI (desktop) or PCMCIA (laptop) card.

You don't need a flashcard of any kind for DS homebrew, most people are now using one of the SD or CF devices. The gba movie player is the cheapest device but isn't suitable for use with passme2 or GBA homebrew which doesn't run from RAM ( i.e. > 256K )
