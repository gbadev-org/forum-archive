#46453 - Sektor - Sat Jun 25, 2005 3:30 am

Boot.nds is not included with the frontend or wifime.zip on firefly's site.

1. Download Wireless Multiboot Application 1.4 BETA 1
2. Download Wifime data
3. Download WMB frontend
4. Extract Wireless Multiboot Application 1.4 BETA 1 to c:\wmb
5. Extract frontend to c:\wmb\
6. Extract wifime.zip to c:\wmb\data\WiFiMe\
7. Run c:\wmb\WMBFront.exe
8. In the Multiboot data box type wifime (don't click the browse button)
9. Click upload
