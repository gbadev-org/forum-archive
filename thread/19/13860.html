#136769 - calcprogrammer1 - Sat Aug 04, 2007 7:39 pm

For $30 you can get a Datel Games n' Music in local retailers like Wal Mart or Best Buy. Homebrew support of devices with removable flash memory is much better than built-in, and it is easier to swap out cards than use proprietary built-in media. And, with microSD cards, no extra adapters or things need to stick out of the DS, since the microSD slot is small enough to incorporate into a DS cartridge sized device. The Games n' Music comes with everything you need to get started, the GnM device, a USB microSD reader/writer, and a 128MB (you can get up to 2GB) microSD card.

With DLDI most everything works on it, except DSOrganize requires the bootme method (?) or maybe there's a way to use it without, but I haven't found it yet.
