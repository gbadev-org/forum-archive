#87162 - Grey - Mon Jun 12, 2006 1:18 pm

I don't have any links or what have you handy, but I read over on scdev.org that the easiest way to recover is with a 2nd ds lite: open em both up, take the good bios (it's socketed in the lite) and put it into the bad, boot up flashme with your pass device, take out the good bios and put in the bad, start the flashme sequence.

Depending on if you've burnt your fuses you may need to short those.

You'll obvioulsy want to go root around on the scdev.org forums, register because some of the forums are not visible to unregistered people.
