A good place for PassMe2 is www.dspassme.com (or if there's someone you know who owns one, you could ask him to borrow it to flash your firmware). I ordered my PassMe from DSPassme (Lynx) and it arrived on time and in perfect condition.
You can also find a schematic to build your own PassMe at www.natrium42.com

A GBA Flash Cart, Supercard or such thing could be found on Google fairly easily.

Here is a Canada store that sells GBA Flash Carts and Supercard:

EBay is also a good place to look.
