#50595 - wgz4life - Thu Aug 11, 2005 12:19 am

sneef posted on the unofficial Supercard site messageboard

hi.

There are no technical downsides to flashing your DS, however, we cannot be 100% certain that it will not have some effect on future network play.

However, Because a passthrough device (like passme) is needed to run "Flashme", and a passthrough device is relatively inexpensive, It seems kind of unlikely that Nintendo would seek to penalize those who flashed their firmware. (being that a passme is easy to come by, and that flashme is SECONDARY to having a passme)

Just in case, I am not going to flash my firmware until after Nintendo's wifi network service launches this Fall, and I can be certain that flashme works with it; or until i can be certain that it is possible to restore original firmware.
