#71413 - The 9th Sage - Sun Feb 12, 2006 7:23 pm

huba wrote:
to flash DS with NDS-FW-V6 firmware do i need prepend ndsloader.bin or not


You don't have to...some GBA flash cart software (if you are using a 'regular' flashcart) will expect a GBA header and try to 'fix' the GBA header of the NDS file, even though it doesn't have one, messing up the file. If it does that, you can just put ndsloader.bin on there and it'll work.
