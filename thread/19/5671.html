#42292 - josath - Mon May 09, 2005 2:28 am

there's only 2 ways that a supercard could work w/ ds homebrew: (as far as i can see)
1. you put your homebrew program in place of the loader/firmware (not on a CF card)
2. you write your own firmware/loader that runs in DS mode and loads from the CF card, and replace the built in loader/firmware with this one.

number 2 would be fairly difficult, you'd have to reverse engineer the current firmware to see how it accesses the CF card, as i don't believe there is any documentation on it
