#35431 - Dib - Fri Feb 04, 2005 4:13 pm

That's true. I own Feel the Magic, and I managed to use my EZFA to spoof the actual games and unlock the bonus content. Though gbadev doesn't condone commercial game roms since they're illegal, so you stating that you plan to upload the games to your cart doesn't bode well on these forums (not that I'm speaking on behalf of any admins, of course).

Therefore note that it is possible, as NMcCoy mentioned, to simply spoof the header of the game (which can be researched on any GBA fan site like PIRACY-DOT-COM, PIRACY-DOT-NET, etc).

In the case of Feel the Magic, it turned out this was unnecessary since you get the same content for beating Hell mode. Other games may vary though. Consider the Mario Golf for the GBA, which you can only unlock the bonus content by owning the Gamecube counterpart. Otherwise you suffer.

Additionally, as a concerned DS enthusiast I would feel irresponsible if I didn't strongly caution you against purchasing Feel the Magic. And I'll leave it at that.
