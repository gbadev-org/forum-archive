#75039 - waruwaru - Thu Mar 09, 2006 7:16 pm

Some people don't have an access point (single computer connecting to internet for example). By converting the computer to an AP, then their DS can connect to the computer and then connect to the internet (or connect to other devices on your wireless network). There isn't a gigabit ethernet port for the DS yet. ;)
