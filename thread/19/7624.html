#62292 - tepples - Tue Nov 29, 2005 3:13 am

First buy a CompactFlash memory card and a PassMe2 compatible game. Notice that some titles may be compatible in one region but not compatible in another. Make sure that both work when used in their intended manner. Then get a PassMe2 (loaded with the appropriate game-specific firmware) and either an M3 or a SuperCard.
