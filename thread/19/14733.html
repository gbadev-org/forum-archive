#147799 - tepples - Fri Dec 28, 2007 3:55 am

modchamp wrote:
Well my friend is looking to get into ds homebrew after I was telling him some of the stuff that I could do with my ds. But after messing around with my Games N Music card I can't seem to get it to play things like Colors, Trivia, etc, anything that needs use of a filesystem.

Did you make sure to use a DLDI patcher to put gmtf.dldi into each .nds program that reads files from the memory card? Tip: Version 2 of the DLDI doesn't work with all microSD cards; use version 1 if you have problems.

Quote:
2. If not, what card would be the cheapest that can do everything?

R4, SuperCard DS One, and CycloDS Evolution appear to work. The big advantages of these over the GnM are 1. they automatically patch .nds programs with DLDI when loading them, and 2. they read and write files faster than the GnM.

Quote:
3. What is the approximate price range for 1-2 gb memory card for the answer to number 2.

All three of these take microSD cards.
