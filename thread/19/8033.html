#66114 - Veg - Sun Jan 08, 2006 10:08 am

Ok, well like I said, I can't remember exactly how I got it to work, but I'll give some of the settings I have set up here.

First of all, it's a fairly old version of Zone Alarm Pro - 3.0.118

The DS wireless adaptor is set up on 192.168.7.0/255.255.255.0, Entry Type : Network, Trusted Zone

The Trusted Zone settings are:
Medium
Nothing is blocked in the Medium settings for Trusted Zone.

In the 'Advanced' box, under 'Internet connection Sharing', I have 'This computer is an ICS/NAT gateway' ticked, and my local address entered (192.168.7.3, though that probably won't be relevent to you).

Far as I can tell, that's everything relevent that I've ticked. Of course, the Nintendo Wifi Connector program has full Access and Server privileges.
