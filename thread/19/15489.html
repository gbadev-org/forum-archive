#155952 - SevenString - Tue May 06, 2008 1:37 am

I found this on the net for re-formatting and installing the latest boot files:

Quote:

1. Slot in the Fire Card and connect your NDS to the computer via the Fire Link.
2. Switch on NDS and wait for the Health Warning page to load.
3. Press and hold Left Trigger and Down button and hold them down, it should now load to the main menu.
4. Press and hold Right Trigger and A and B buttons, in a few seconds, it would load the formating screen.
5. Wait for it to format completely, and for the removable drive to appear on your computer.
6. When done, copy the software files from the manufacturer's site and restart your NDS. These files are:
xmenu.dat
Fire_eng_1_45.nds
7. All done!
