I bought one of those in Den Den town here in Osaka. I paid 6000 yen for it (together with a kingston 1gb mini-sd that comes up to 10000 yen). It can't play GBA games, even though the firmware is the same as the version that can. So you can get into the GBA menu, but it just won't run any roms or games.

The rest of the device is essentially the same as the normal MiniSD version. My friend has that one, and the only noticeable difference is that his has a different background.

So if you can get a good price, go for it.
