Depends on the version of your DS. Use forum search to find the pictochat color trick (short version, go into pictochat and either wave a magnet near the ABXY switches, or eject the game cartridges). If the screen turns yellow or purple, you will need a passme2. Green and greyish blue give you a clean bill of health for using wifime to boot code off of the supercard and then run flashme.
