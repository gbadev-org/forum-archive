#127319 - crono - Tue May 01, 2007 5:55 pm

You need the supercard inserted while booting to get into the supercard menu. So you need it everytime you boot. However, you can also flash your ds firmware with FlashMe. With FlashMe you can boot to the supercard menu without the need of superkey. I have done this myself.

#127333 - tepples - Tue May 01, 2007 7:45 pm

Insert SuperKey only: You probably get a white screen.

Insert SuperCard only: SuperCard menu appears in Game Boy Advance mode. You can run .gba files. (EDIT: Not with rumble)

Insert SuperCard + SuperKey: SuperCard menu appears in DS mode, with menu on top screen and logo on touch screen. You can run sc.nds files.
