#120787 - tepples - Tue Mar 06, 2007 2:26 am

NoPass refers to a card that tells the DS to run code from the SLOT-2 (the GBA slot) in DS mode. MAX Media Launcher is Datel's most basic brand of NoPass card. The card included with the MAX Media Dock accessory and the Action Replay DS accessory are also NoPass cards.

DSLinux's problems with MAX Media Dock come from 1. the defective bootloader on the MAX Media Dock and 2. lack of drivers for the MAX Media Dock's ATA controller (used to access the CF card).
