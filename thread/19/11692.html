#108936 - felix123 - Mon Nov 13, 2006 9:22 am

You need the following to play homebrew:
NoPass OR FlashMe AND
(Supercard + SD card + SD card reader) OR (GBAMP CF + CF card + CF card reader)

GBAMP can play movies, games and apps. Supercard can play movies with Moonshell.

And as Mr. Picklesworth said we don't talk about ROMS here.
