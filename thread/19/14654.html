#146880 - Potent1 - Mon Dec 10, 2007 6:51 pm

I have a Games n Music card, so here's been some of my problems, maybe they can help you. Make sure you have the right DLDI patcher. Chism made 2 versions, and the newest one doesn't work. I haven't heard of a single card it worked for, no offense. But the first one works great. If it worked before and you haven't changed anything, this shouldn't be the problem. Second, make sure you're not replacing programs with newer versions, just backup your card, clear it and put the new stuff on. Third, Reformat your card. Most of the time this is the problem, just reformat it, and redo it. Sometimes the Games n Music card goes screwy, so just reformat it and start over. This is why you should constantly be backing up your card, in case you want to keep your save states.
