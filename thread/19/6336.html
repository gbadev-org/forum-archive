simple instructions i guess for hacking your ds wide open....

firstly vopy the mp hacking gba file to the CF, and download the update.e18 from liksangto the CF too... dump it in the gbamp and run it...

now turn off, remove the battery cover, and pull off the small square sticker...jam summat metal in there to short the two points - i used a screw since it was easy to keep in the hole witout holding it...

now get the flashme NDS file and name it '_BOOT_MP.NDS' and put it on the root of your cf card... put your passme in and the gbamp on...torn on and flashme should boot and just follow instructions....

now u dont need the pass me anymore. ever. currently to boot stuff on the ds just name it '_BOOT_MP.NDS' and itll boot. hopefully a nice menu will appear soon.
