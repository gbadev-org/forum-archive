#63526 - josath - Wed Dec 14, 2005 12:42 am

1. flashme.nds goes in the 256mbit part
2. loader.bin goes in your ez3 application folder on your PC. not sure if you have to specify this as a loader in the software somewhere, but you do NOT put it on the 256mbit part
3. I don't understand what you are saying here
4. you are not using the wifi adaptor to load flashme.nds. flashme.nds is on your gba cart. you are using the wifi adaptor to launch 'wifime'. but otherwise the command is correct

And yes, once it boots, it tells you what to do on the screen (when to short, etc)
