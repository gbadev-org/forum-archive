most likly reason is that when you instal a driver, instal the cutom made driver for the pcmcia card which is wifime. To get the app and the driver. Go to http://www.aaronrogers.com/nintendods/wifime.php and get the driver and app, next, you'll need to uninstall your current wifi driver. But be warned, this takes the wifi part out, so you can't go online unless you ethir have another wifi card or ethernet cable. Now instal the driver, also before the installing the driver app, just to make sure your doing it right. Go back to http://www.aaronrogers.com/nintendods/wifime.php and follow the directions on how to instal it correctlly. If the instals a success, then your wifi cards supported, also you could just check on the listed cards part of the site to see if it is supported.
