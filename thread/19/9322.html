#80459 - 0xtob - Sat Apr 22, 2006 1:42 pm

You can't run .ds.gba files from the GBAMP, because they have an additional header that make them compatible with Flash Carts and Supercard. For the GBAMP, you need .nds files that should be provided with basically every homebrew program.
