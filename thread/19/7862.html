#64579 - tepples - Fri Dec 23, 2005 6:31 pm

Cheap Chinese devices that take SD memory will run the SD card in SPI mode, which is widely documented and thought to be free of patent encumbrance. Virtually all MMC cards support SPI mode. In fact, most MMC cards actually have SD circuitry on them, disabled by a fuse in cards that are packaged as MMC.

(source: Wikipedia)
