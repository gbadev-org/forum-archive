#55958 - tepples - Tue Oct 04, 2005 10:19 pm

The PassMe or WiFiMe jumps to the beginning of GBA ROM space. Unlike a .gba or .mb file, a .nds file isn't a raw executable image. The "NDS Loader" runs from the beginning of GBA ROM space. It parses the appended .nds file's data structures and copies the appropriate things into the appropriate parts of Nintendo DS EWRAM before execution.
