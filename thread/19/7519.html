#61121 - Dwedit - Thu Nov 17, 2005 2:38 pm

It means "insert a small metal flathead screwdriver into that hole, facing horizontally so it bridges that connection". You may have to press down with a medium to hard amount of pressure to get the % bar to advance.
Code:

_    _
_)  (_  <-SL1 (inside that hole covered up by the sticker)

 ----   <-screwdriver head, connects the two pads.
