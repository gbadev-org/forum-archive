#88732 - Dirtie - Wed Jun 21, 2006 12:57 pm

I have had a similar experience with my EFA-Linker.

The EFA client also messes up the header, so when I flashed my V4 DS and others with the pre-lite build of Flashme V7, I had to prepend the loader, but after that all was fine.

The first Lite I helped flash however, I prepended the loader as normal, but all I got was a corrupt GBA bios screen - the difference was that I was using the lite-firmware-included build Flashme V7. It was really quite embarassing, because I was trying to help this dude flash it (he probably hangs around here) but nothing would work, and had me very confused at the time.

The funny thing is, I had in fact used the Lite build of Flashme V7 earlier on a standard DS - but only to restore a messed up flash as some guy found a non-working DS lying around in the apartment he was moving into - the difference was, since I didn't need my passme 2 to restore it, I didn't have to include the EFA client's NDS loader/menu on the cart (I still prepended the actual file), because using the loader/menu is the only way you can select which sram data you want to use.

So either it just doesn't like flashing Lites, or the latest firmware itself is just incompatible with my cart (and I would suspect possibly others too) for some reason - or a combination of both.
