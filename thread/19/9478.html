#82213 - wintermute - Thu May 04, 2006 4:23 pm

The connections on the DS circuit are made to the firmware chip.

ppflash.cpp wrote:

Instructions:
Read the SPI signal definitions below for pinout.
Disconnect battery and charging cable.
Cut trace to RFU pin 28 (VCC) and connect to external power source (3.3V). This way, the rest of the DS will not be powered.
Also short the battery contacts to prevent capacitors getting charged.
