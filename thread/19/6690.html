#52174 - tepples - Sat Aug 27, 2005 11:29 pm

Bouki wrote:
but how it can be usb version if the card is integrated to my computer !!!

Many motherboards have internal USB ports called "headers", intended to be connected to wires that run to the front panel of a case. If your wireless adapter is connected to an internal USB header, then it'll look like a USB card instead of a PCI card.
