#91634 - tepples - Sat Jul 08, 2006 2:08 pm

MAX Media Dock/Player is nowhere near the level of SuperCard, and it even has disadvantages vs. GBA Movie Player v2 CF:
  1. Not yet as compatible with DS homebrew (no support for .nds files over 1000 KB or for Chishm's FAT access)
  2. Does not appear to work with GBA multiboot programs or NES or GBC emulators

SRAM has two uses: booting PassMe2 and saving. MAX Media Launcher and other NoPass cards don't need SRAM to boot, and the software on the MAX Media Dock/Player has the CF card for saving.

And in some countries (such as the United States), you can find a GBAMP for $25 including shipping if you put your mind to it.
