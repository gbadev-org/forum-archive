#50572 - ttabbal - Wed Aug 10, 2005 8:29 pm

Omnicide wrote:
With WiFime you don't need a gba cart? because I have a wireless card and a laptop i'm sure it will work with.



Don't be too sure your WiFi will work. It requires a very specific card chipset, not just any WiFi card. On the WiFiMe website, it has a list of known good cards, and even without a GBA cart, it should be able to load the driver and your DS should show the WMB server. If this works, you will NOT be able to use your WiFi card for anything else. The drivers are DS or WiFi, not both.

The need for a GBA cart shouldn't be a problem though, as you have the money and I gave you one reseller that will sell you a GBA cart without a CC. You don't need a big one, a 256Mb is more than big enough to start with. You need bankswitching to use more than that anyway, and that's more than you want to start with, I would think. Or you could get a GBA-MP (Movie Player) and a Compact Flash card. I've seen CF cards in the grocery store!

Oh, I see you've been answered about the cart requirement. If you really don't want a cart, see if you have a friend with one to do the FlashMe with. If not, well, you're stuck. Stop complaining and go buy a cart allready!
