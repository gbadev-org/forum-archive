#84112 - tepples - Sat May 20, 2006 4:46 pm

Dan2552 wrote:
Was my GBAMP meant to have a menu come up [in DS mode]?

If you've installed NDSMP on your GBAMP v2 CF and have put Mighty Max's loader or MoonShell or DSOrganize as /_boot_mp.nds on your CF card, then yes. If you're using a SuperCard or M3 or G6 or MAX Media Player, then yes.

Quote:
When I have no _boot..... file, the max media launcher loads the GBAMP in GBA mode...

Same happened when I used PassMe1 and no /_boot_mp.nds was present. NDSMP falls back to GBA mode if it can't find the file.

Quote:
BUT with things on the side of the screens???

This is GBA mode with a custom border loaded into the frame buffer.

Quote:
this doesnt happen when I use a commercial GBA game

The Nintendo DS firmware is more careful about making a polished runtime environment for GBA mode. NDSMP, with its smaller code size, can't be as careful as the firmware when it falls back to GBA mode.
