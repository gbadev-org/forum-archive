#55900 - Maverick - Mon Oct 03, 2005 10:28 pm

Once you have the packets captured and the sniffers own headers removed from the capture file, convert the capture into its own header.bin, beacons.bin, rsa.bin, arm7.bin and arm9.bin and compile into an nds file.

The capture will just look like random numbers until you study and work it out.

All this can be done with the use of a hex editor. To get you started, you can try to read and understand the beacons layout on my website: http://downtou.ne1.net/ Its in the DS section under Docs & Tuts, Research, Multiboot Initiation.

Dependant on what card you will be using to sniff and how much you are willing to spend on the software, i would mosl likely recommend Airopeek. Demos can be downloaded from the website.
