#48555 - tepples - Wed Jul 20, 2005 7:36 pm

Commercial Nintendo DS games that are intended to run on DS cards look for asset data on the DS card. They would have to be patched to read assets from the GBA ROM rather than from the DS card, the same way pirated GameCube games are (auto-)patched to read from the broadband adapter rather than from the disc.
