#52624 - tepples - Thu Sep 01, 2005 1:32 pm

If the program is not already a multiboot program (*.mb.gba), then you'll have to either recompile the GBA program as multiboot (which will involve manual porting work) or port it to the Nintendo DS (which will also involve manual porting work), either way streaming assets from the CF card as needed. If you do not have the source code, or you are not willing to work on a port, then get a SuperCard instead.
