#93106 - telephasic - Mon Jul 17, 2006 1:06 am

I've seen the recalibration loop happen on another DS Phat. It has nothing to do with the flashme as far as I am aware as it occured on a non-flashed DS.

The problem was solved by turning the DS off and checking very carefully around the edge of the screen (the gap between the screen surface and the surrounding plastic) and using a pin to clear out little flecks of dirt. One was big enough to be pressing on the screen and interfering with the calibration.

Let us all know how it goes.
