DS Flash Equipment > FWNitro v1.6c problems[edit: I found a way to fix!]

#120455 - gm112 - Sat Mar 03, 2007 10:22 pm

FWNitro v1.6c flashed directly on my DS is not letting me boot any of my games on the SCSD. I tried to noflashme but I still get the white screen of death. Argh!! Please, somebody help me!


EDIT:
Alright, for those who have the same problem as me do the following. While turning the DS on, hit A+B+START+SELECT and it'll bring you to your flashcarts menu. There, boot Flashme. Just did this and everything is working again!
