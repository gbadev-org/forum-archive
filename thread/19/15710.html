Actually, it sounds to me that his old DS Lite had FlashMe installed and the new one doesn't, so on the new one he's only booting in GBA mode, which does not show *.nds files in the Games menu at all.

You need a passthrough device like a Superkey to boot it in DS mode so you can launch DS homebrew files.
