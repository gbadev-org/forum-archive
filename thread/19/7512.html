OK, just one more reply. I've spent about an hour fooling around with this. My conclusion? It's a server problem.

When you connect for the first time to the Tony Hawk servers, you DS / cart are "paired" and that info is sent to the server. Well, if the server screws it up, then you DS can and probably will crash, resulting in your save being lost.

I waited about 30 minutes and tried again after all my original trouble, and managed to get past the "pairing" stage and into the actual Wifi server menu. I immediatetly disconnected, powered down, and powered back up. My save was still intact, and I could easily connect again. I actually tried connecting about 5 or 6 times with no problems.

Bottom line: Looks to not be a flashme problem at all, which makes sense because Mario Kart works great. I also expect Metroid Prime Hunters and Animal Crossing to work great as well.
