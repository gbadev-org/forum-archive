#67083 - chishm - Sun Jan 15, 2006 11:55 am

You probably used the repair utility when trying to install a GBAMP firmware hack (accidentally pressed start instead of select).
Put update.e19 in the root of your CF card and boot your GBAMP with that (in GBA mode). Press Start to update when prompted. This should hopefully get you a working GBAMP, which you can then try reapplying the hack to.
