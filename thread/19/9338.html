Yup.. all is correct. Natrium designed the PassMe I sell (dspassme.com) and for a time, I was selling Super PassKey's from Supercard because they were easier to resell then to build my own, but after PassMe2 came out, I went back to building them by hand.. Don't really know why. Natrium is doing the same thing, he is purchasing M3 adapters clone of his PassMe and reprogramming it.

So, the only real difference as mentioned is the packaging. Both have the same "innards" but the M3 version has a nice case around it.

Oh.. the other main difference between the M3 and PassMe (and other direct PassMe clones for that matter) is that it wraps around and the original DS game ends up underneith the NDS, where the PassMe sticks directly out the back.
