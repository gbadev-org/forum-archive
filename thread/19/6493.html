#50027 - tepples - Thu Aug 04, 2005 5:57 pm

You'll usually be able to tell which of the three following situations applies by looking at pictures of the product.

If it flashes through an external writer and allows you to turn off the multi-ROM loader, it'll work with *Me.
  • XG2 Turbo cards in an XG2 Slim Loader
  • Many cards supported by LittleWriter in a Visoly writer
  • Visoly Flash Advance cards in a Visoly writer

If it flashes through a port on the card itself and allows you to turn off the multi-ROM loader, it'll work with *Me.
  • EFA-Linker

If it flashes through a USB-to-GBA cable and allows you to turn off the multi-ROM loader, and you own a GBA, a GBA SP, or a Game Boy Player, it'll work with *Me.
  • Flash2Advance
