I can't find the pictures, but I ran across a better explanation of why DSLites are shutting down while flashing. Very close to SL1, on the left (the opposite side from what everyone thought was shorting) there's a tiny bit of metal hanging over SL1. (It's probably part of the GBA slot assembly.) That is very likely what's causing people's Lites to shut off. Don't touch it and you're golden.
