#93975 - MrD - Sat Jul 22, 2006 4:19 am

Have you got the most up to date kernel for your SC, and has it been installed?

It's possible that the latest kernel for SC causes it to pretend to be a GBA cart with the DSBooter game-name header, and your current kernel doesn't do this. If this isn't present, you can force a FlashMe'd DS to use NDS code from the GBA slot, try holding A+B+X+Y on bootup.
