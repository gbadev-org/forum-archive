#65439 - caitsith2 - Tue Jan 03, 2006 8:49 am

Green screens from the pictochat thingie, means you have firmware version 3. (assuming its not an IQUE DS). Firmware versions 1-3 are passme1 compatible, and firmware version 4 requires passme2.

There is also an NJH403 serial number in the listing. The ***** is the 5 remaining digits masked out, and the last digit is the check digit for the serial number barcode. The only NJH403 entry in that list does say it will work with passme1 as well.
