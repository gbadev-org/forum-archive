Quote:
Will reprograming my DS affect how it plays regular games?

No. DS Game Cards will work on a homebrew-enabled DS.

Quote:
Will it inibit from playing regular DS games, or its WiFi connections?

No. You can still use Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection and homebrew on the same DS. Nintendo doesn't ban DS homebrewers from Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection the way Microsoft bans Xbox homebrewers from Xbox Live.

Quote:
If the answer is no for the above question, then could you please tell me where i can find this code, and any instructions pertaining to how I can implement it.

Do you want a list of DS homebrew programs, or do you want to know how to write your own? Either way, first you'll need a memory card to store the player software and the MP3s. Do you want just DS homebrew, or do you want both DS and Game Boy Advance homebrew?
