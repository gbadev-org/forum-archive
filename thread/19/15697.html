#158885 - dantheman - Fri Jun 20, 2008 5:56 am

What sort of things are you talking about? Some homebrew programs have adopted the convention of also accepting data files in a "DATA" folder on the root of your card. For instance, DSOrganize will look for the "DSOrganize" folder both on the root of the card and inside the "DATA" folder. I believe a version of DS Doom does as well. Look up your homebrew apps and see which ones of them have adopted this convention.
