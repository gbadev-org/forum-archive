#158348 - RaiDesu - Mon Jun 09, 2008 3:29 pm

The M3 Real is the current generation line of M3 Slot-1, so it is different from a M3 Simply/R4.

M3 Real seems like a good selection, although I suggest the GBA Expansion Pack, as the Ezflash 3in1 ram pack I prefer. The latest firmware is M3 Sakura, which fixes slower speeds of the GUI and adds more eye candy. You don't get much from the ram pack, while with the GBA Expansion you get the ability to boot GBA files from the slot-1 menu.

Since it supports SDHC, I suggest getting a 4GB card. You'll need it.
