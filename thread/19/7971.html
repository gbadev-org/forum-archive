#65442 - AnalogMan - Tue Jan 03, 2006 9:10 am

NDS files are normally homebrew. People make them not the official companies. There are two versions. .nds and .ds.ga. The supercard uses the '.ds.gba' type, but you then just rename it to '.nds' no special software required as long as it ends in .nds. For official NDS games, you run the game through the converter software and you get a .dsq or .dsi file.

So what I'm saying is... you can have as many files on the card as it'll hold and you just pick the one you want to load from a list.
