#80161 - tepples - Wed Apr 19, 2006 1:28 pm

FlashMe stealth won't have any visible effect apart from removing the RSA check from DS Download Play and allowing you to boot from the GBA card in DS mode.

On a regular DS, bricking won't happen as long as you keep SL1 shorted to at least 5% or so and don't drop the battery. But I wouldn't recommend it on a DS lite just yet because of 1. the bricking problem and 2. the lack of brightness control in the public version of FlashMe.

#80163 - JaJa - Wed Apr 19, 2006 1:30 pm

If you use FlashMe the health and safety screen is gone (as is the startup chime).
Also it will auto boot the GBA slot device if it has the "PASS" header.
It's on the website.
Quote:

http://ds.gcdev.com/dsfirmware/
The button combination A+B+X+Y, gamecode "PASS" or title "DSBooter" on the GBA cartridge automatically starts program from GBA cartridge (starting from offset 0xC0).
Backlight fades in at startup.

Effectively removes the health screen, making it less annoying to boot.

Remove RSA signature check for Wireless MultiBooting (WMB) homebrew NDS files.

#80190 - JaJa - Wed Apr 19, 2006 8:39 pm

The first few hundred bytes of many files, describe what it is and can contain information such as dimensions, palettes or the start address of the audio within a file.

You don't really need to worry as all homebrew comes with PASS already in the header, as ndstool puts it there.

Layout of the header (technical) if you are interested is located here:
http://www.bottledlight.com/ds/index.php/FileFormats/NDSFormat
