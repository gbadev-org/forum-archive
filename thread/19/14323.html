That's a slot-2 SuperCard...not a SuperKey...

You can't boot DS mode stuff from Slot-2 without a PassMe, NoPass, WiFiMe, FlashMe, or Slot-1 device with pass software. You'll need to buy a SuperKey, NoPass, PassCard, or (probably the cheapest, easiest), Games n' Music slot-1 card. I say Games n' Music (which is a flash cart) because it's cheap ($20 to $35) and available at Wal Mart, Best Buy, etc.
