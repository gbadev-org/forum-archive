#112658 - Lynx - Tue Dec 19, 2006 8:06 am

It really sounds like the _BOOT_MP.nds file isn't being detected.. It could be that your CF card isn't being recognized in NDS mode. When the DS boots in DS mode, Chishm't firmware hack tries to load the _BOOT_MP.nds file. If it can't find one, it boots into GBA mode.

Try defragging the CF card.

Last resort, follow Natrium42's instructions at the bottom of this page and see if that helps.

I've had this problem in the past.. but it was because of something really stupid I did, or forgot to do.. But I can't remember anymore, as I have installed GBAMP on to many GBA MP's to keep anything strait.
