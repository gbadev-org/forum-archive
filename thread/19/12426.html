#118227 - dantheman - Sun Feb 11, 2007 6:43 am

That equipment looks good. If you don't want to have any part of the Supercard stick out (for instance, if you like keeping your DS in your pocket while listening to music), then you could spend the extra few dollars to get the miniSD version. The media isn't all that much more expensive either. I was able to get a 2 GB miniSD card for $20 by looking at the newspaper ads. Of course, if it doesn't matter, then you should be fine with the SD. Just make sure you do not get the Rumble series.
