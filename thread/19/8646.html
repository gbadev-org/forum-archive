#72613 - MaHe - Mon Feb 20, 2006 2:02 pm

1. Ask Natrium42 if he can sell you custom programmed PassME2 (for EUR version of MP:H demo).
2. You have three choiches:

- SuperCard

The cheapest option. You can pick up a SD or CF version.
Some homebrew may cause trouble, so beware.

- M3 Perfect adapter

Will work with every homebrew. Probably. I recommend the CF version, since SD can cause some trouble

- the classic FlashCarts

They fit to GBA slot perfectly though they're expensive and quite useless.
You won't be happy with 'em, believe me.
