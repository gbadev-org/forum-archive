#55995 - Sektor - Wed Oct 05, 2005 7:10 am

It doesn't have the right chipset. I don't think any cards with speed booster/108Mb work. You need another PCI card.

http://ralink.rapla.net

This is the closest card on the list to yours and some versions of the card do not have rt2560:

Linksys WMP54G v4 *

* - chipset depends on card revision/version (could be Accton, ADMtek, Agere, Atheros, Atmel, Broadcom, Conexant/GlobespanVirata/Intersil/Harris/PRISM, Envara, Inprocomm, Intel, Marvell, Realtek, SiS, Texas Instruments, VIA, ZyDAS or some other).

I suggest you find a card that doesn't have an * next to it, less chance of it being the wrong chipset.
