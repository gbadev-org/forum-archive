#72726 - m2pt5 - Tue Feb 21, 2006 5:52 am

LOst? wrote:
I have V3 installed. I want to upgrade to V6 but I am too afraid my original PassMe 1 won't work later because most of you guys have the newer DS and that's probably what V6 is built for? I have not found any info on the site.

You should upgrade to at least FlashMe v5, if not v6. Playing (or download-playing) any game that uses Nintendo's wi-fi service will brick (destroy) the firmware of earlier versions. (You can recover with the recovery code and A+B+Sel+Start, but it's better to reflash now and not have to do that later.)
