#68709 - Lynx - Thu Jan 26, 2006 1:55 pm

Well, first you can attampt the recovery, by leaving flashme.nds in the GBA port, "Hold A+B+select+start, THEN turn on your DS"

If not, the next option would be PPFlash.. This one can be ugly if you don't have soldering skills, but if your DS is dead, how much worse can you really make it?

Finally hope, is to e-bay it.. But, I wouldn't let it go for cheap, as it's not really broke (unless there were other issues with it prior to flashing) and anyone with soldering skills can fix it.
