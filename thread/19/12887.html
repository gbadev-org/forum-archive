kaka123 wrote:
Recently i purchased "R4 revolution for the DS" and it came with a disc in chinese

You did buy the English version, right?

Quote:
unforunately i cant read it, and it had a program called moonshl 1.6 and i have no idea how to install it.

You have to copy the files "_DS_MENU.DAT" and "_DS_MSHL.nds" and the folders "moonshl" and "_system_" to the root level of your microSD card.

Quote:
Also, after i set up R4 do i need to do anything to my wireless router if i wanna go Wi-Fi on Mario Kart or Bleach 2nd

Eject your R4 and plug in an authentic DS Game Card, and the games should connect fine. (Read the announcement at the top of the DS Flash Equipment if you disagree.)
