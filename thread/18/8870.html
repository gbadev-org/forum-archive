#75214 - tepples - Sat Mar 11, 2006 1:04 am

To run homebrew on the DS lite or any other DS with firmware version 4 or later, you need a PassMe2-compatible game, a PassMe2 adapter customized for that game, and an M3, SuperCard, or traditional GBA flash card. If you're planning on buying an M3 CF, then do it now.

With FlashMe v6, you will also lose backlight brightness adjustment.
