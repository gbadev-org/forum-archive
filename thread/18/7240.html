I've noticed a few moans here and there in the forums about libnds and devkitARM which on occasion I only see by chance or someone is kind enough to point me to a specific post. When bugs are reported via the mailing list or tracker they are fixed as soon as possible. When they're reported as a reply to some topic which may not even be related to a compiler or library issue then they tend to be missed.

For the best chance of getting bug reports to the devkitPro team please use the tracker, mailing list or reply to the threads in Announcements and Comments.
