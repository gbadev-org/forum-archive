DS development > Why does devkitpro put DTCM at 0B000000? NO$GBA hates that.

#161413 - Dwedit - Sun Aug 03, 2008 3:05 am

Why does devkitARM place DTCM at 0B000000? There's a bug in NO$GBA that makes all data stored there appear as FF's.

However, if you use a modified linkscript and crt0 to move ITCM and DTCM, NO$GBA is able to view them fine.
For example, I moved DTCM to 0x02800000, and ITCM to 0x01FF8000.
