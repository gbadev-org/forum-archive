#38240 - Lynx - Thu Mar 24, 2005 2:12 pm

Well, I'm *hoping* you either build the 1 layer version, or insulated the back on the 2 layer version. Now, when you were at the white screen, and you said you didn't need to touch the screen to get it, your running the new DarkFader code, and as far as I know, almost all (if not all) demos have been converted. Now, when you get the two white screens, it means it is seeing the NDS cart. Now that your getting the boot menu, it is not. So, I'd start by moving the PCB around inside the DS untill you get the two white screens again. That'll let you know that your PassMe is working fine. Next, you will need to troubleshoot your Flash Cart, and or the demo your trying to use.

Hope this helps.
