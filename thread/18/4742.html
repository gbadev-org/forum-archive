I wouldnt swear to it not being possible to stream video over wireless on the DS but it would need a very clever codec and as such still some sort of software downloaded to it. Now if it would be possible to do that while having a low enough latency for sending controls back to the host DS is another thing.

Still, I think we will find some interesting tricks pulled on the DS once homebrew gets going. In many ways the fact that its such a limited system means it will have more interesting uses. The harder it is to do something the more motivation there is to do it. I for one suspect the PSP will have very basic stuff like emulators, nothing close to the innovation the DS will get.
