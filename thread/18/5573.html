#41833 - mymateo - Mon May 02, 2005 9:03 am

FourScience

If you were talking about my site, then I will respond thusly:

I would love to put in automatic submission, but 2 main problems with that. (1) No immediate control over the content (porn, foul language, commercial ROMs not necessarily for DS, etc) and (2) I don't know how.

An RSS feed would be nice, but only 2 main problems. (1) I don't stay up-to-date enough to be a site people would go to for the latest breaking news and (2) I don't know how.


I still don't think I'd like an automatic submission because of the reason listed above, but if someone would like to help me out then a system whereby I can enter in a game and it would automatically be sorted by date, name, author, or by rom type (PassMe, BIN, source, etc) that would be very cool. I don't have a whole lot of free time, but that's something I will be looking into eventually anyways.

As for an RSS feed, I hve no particular desire to learn how to create one OR update it on a daily basis (every 2 or 3 days yes, but if I have to track down information constantly then it becomes a real chore). But if someone wanted me to host it, I could probably arrange that (except if it's going to cost me extra money -- then it depends on the cost as well).

Anyways, there you have it. I hope to make this site into something useful, and suggestions like these (even if they were directed to someone else) are helpful.
