#41472 - josath - Thu Apr 28, 2005 10:04 pm

get a nice camera on a tripod, affix your DS so it can't move, and the DS screens are perfectly parallel to the camera's lense. take pictures/videos at a high resolution. then bring down the resolution so the width of the display is 256 pixels. That should get you pretty good quality.
