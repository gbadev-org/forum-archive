Hello everyone, I'm the administrator of emubase, the society that is responsible for hosting nocash (aside from many other sites on this topic). I'll say some words about the situation of nocash and the site.

It is true, that Martin has vanished for some time now. I can confirm that his last change to the webspace was made on the 23rd of January this year and I've seen no traces from him on the net. Several people requested that I visit him personally to confirm his current status (not an impossible request since we live in the same city), but I'm a bit reluctant to do so, since he has taken long "pauses" before and maybe needs the time without the sometimes-not-so-nice internet annoying him (if you have read his email section you know what I mean). His current absence is noted much more than the ones before mainly because of no$gba being one of the (if not THE ONE) prime NDS emus out there (not even taking the debugger into equatation, which is unmatched imho).

If the site is down, like this time, don't worry too much, mostly it's because our hosting provider got some problems (in this case the whole provider was down, because an external technician manipulated the central backup power unit unplanned).

If Martin were ever to delete his whole site, I would place a notice on nocash.emubase.de which explains the situation. If he would give his agreement, we (the emubase society) will keep up his whole site as an archive as long as we provide our hosting service (which I hope will be forever or at least for a long, long time), even he "shuts down" his services.

In the case you got any futher questions regarding this issue you can find me on #emulation@irc.euirc.net with this nick.
