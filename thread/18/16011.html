#162518 - jackman - Thu Sep 04, 2008 7:56 am

The BigN Logo, "Nintendo DS" banner and the "Health and Safety Screen" are part of the Nintendo DS Firmware. The Logo (not the "Nintendo DS" banner) is only displayed, if there's a valid DS Cart in Slot 1.

The Default Firmware can be overwritten with custom Firmware (common known as FlashMe), so I think that you can also overwrite the Logo, banner and the "Health and Safety Screen" when overwriting the Firmware.
