#164724 - tepples - Sun Nov 16, 2008 2:16 am

If you want to cheat at Pokemon games, use Pokesav.

On an NES, you'd replace the ROM chip with a NOR flash chip and do a little bit of rewiring. This does not work on a DS because a single IC package contains both the ROM and the crypto-processor needed to talk on the DS Game Card bus.
