#50392 - josath - Tue Aug 09, 2005 3:23 am

1. A couple homebrews wont work with gbamp because they try to load data from the flash cart. They can be recoded to load the data from the CF card instead, but this involves a little work. Most homebrew will work though.
Also, the commercial demos don't work with gbamp. But this is being worked on, and they will probably be fixed.

2. Yes, you need to use passme every time, UNLESS you patch your firmware with 'FlashMe.' Then you don't need passme any more (but you need passme to install it in the first place). Search forums for more info on flashme

3. Agree with above post

4. gbamp and passme will be enough to run most ds homebrew. (see point 1. though)
