It's Japanese. Any time you see curvy characters like
の は を
then those are hiragana, a syllabary used mostly for writing the case-endings on Japanese words.

And any time you see
ニンテンドー
that's Ni n te n do : in katakana, a syllabary used to spell foreign words and (when written above kanji) to indicate pronunciation.
