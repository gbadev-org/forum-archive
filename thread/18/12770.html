#122522 - simonjhall - Mon Mar 19, 2007 11:15 pm

Oh my god not another one! I think we need a stock reply to this. Any chance of making a sticky with *big letters* in it?

Anyway (sigh), the program you'll need is lmp-ng - this is the music-playing software you'll need. Do a search on the forum for this.

To get this onto your DS you'll need a booting device. I recommend a slot-1 card, such as the M3 Simply. To actually store your music, by a big microSD card to plug into the Simply.
Things to search for: flashme, nopass, wifime.

You may also find http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NDSh an interesting read too, and it has a big bit on the types of booting device you can buy.

When you buy stuff, consult the retailer feedback forum for good places to buy. Btw, this forum is for actual development - have a look in the flash equipment thread for more info on the hardware you'll need.
