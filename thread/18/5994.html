There are lots of fades :)
- Backlight fade (like the firmware patch does)
- Fade to black (like what firmware does when it shuts down after asking for it)
- crystal fade (like what happens if the system is powered off immediately or battery is removed)

Different brands of displays might result in different behavior when powering down. Layer thickness, density, transistor characteristics, driving circuitry.
I doubt it's a firmware thing. Test if PassMe still works. If it doesn't... then it's a new version :)
