#88483 - nerd of nerds - Mon Jun 19, 2006 6:28 pm

gbc was NOT emulated on the gba iirc. The gba actually has the Z80 core in it but it is turned off in gba mode.

the gba cart connector has a switch that can tell when a gba pack is inserted (the little notch out of the cart is what doesn't trip the switch) or when a gbc/gb pack is inserted (the cart doesn't have a notch, therefore trips the switch).

But all that being said, a gba emulator for ds should be entirely possible, but it wouldn't exactly be an emulator, more of an interpreter. This is what the (good) gba emulator for the gp32 did.
