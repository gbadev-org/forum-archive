spunky wrote:
I read that you can just assign which of the two screens are assigned main and sub screen, which is something I really need.. I need the framebuffer mode on the touchscreen.. how can I assign them?

You don't need the framebuffer mode on the touchscreen, as you could use a 16bit extended rotation background in mode 5 instead.

If you don't want to do that, then the ndslib macro you want is lcdSwap().
