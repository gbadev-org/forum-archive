Users:

First and foremost; This is a development forum. Even though we have a section about flash equipment does not mean that you should ask about how to get X game running on your gba flash setup. We do not care if you own the games and just want to keep them on a single cart. It's not what this forum is about.
If you still post asking such a question, do not get upset when your post is removed without warning. Consider it strike 1. Strike 2 will get you banned.

When starting a new thread, it is very important that the subject of your post clearly depict the contents of your post. Ie, subjects such as "Is this possible..." will either be deleted or renamed depending on how much time the moderators have available.

No promotion of any retailer outside the Retailer Feedback forum. NO affiliate links are allowed.

No links to sites offering/relating to piracy/warez.

See a post that you think is in violation with the forum rules? DO NOT start bashing/flaming the poster. DO contact a moderator.

Try your best to use correct spelling, grammar & punctuation please. If you know you have some problems with any of these, there are spell checkers available (see google).

Do not "bump" posts. EVER. "bump posts" will be deleted on sight.

Posting "can someone make an (insert example of game) clone/port/sequel for the GBA/DS please?" is frowned upon and if you're new to the forum you are strongly recommended not to post such a thing. You have been warned.

Keep your signature short. We do not allow images on our forum so you don't have to bother trying to get one in your signature.

We will be very happy if you stick to using black text. Thanks!

If you have any questions please contact a moderator. Complaints regarding a moderator? Notify the admin

We reserve the right to delete any posts & users for any reason at any time. Did your post disappear without a trace? Contact a moderator BEFORE you post the same thing again. Was your account deleted? Go elsewhere.

That said, we really are a friendly forum and common courtesy will get you far.


Moderators:

-Move topics without leaving a shadow.
-Use the Lock Thread function with causion. A lot of the time Delete Thread is much better. Only Lock when there is interesting information in the thread not available elsewhere.
-Report users posting about running commercial games (etc) to the admin. Delete any such threads. This is a development forum and even if the poster owns the game in question it doesnt belong here.
-Replace links to retailers with a link to the retailer feedback section.
