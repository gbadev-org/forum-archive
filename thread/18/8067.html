teitoku wrote:
I got them to move enough to where I could free the top half of the DS from the bottom half, but the hinges still won't come out. Do you just keep pushing and force them out or what?

it seem to be a mechanism that it only comes out at a certain angle, which means...i think you need to put the top and the bottom together again, keep the 2 little metal cylinders ALL THE WAY IN, this is important, since if they are not all the way in, they wont come out even if at the right angle. yes i know they can be pushed out a couple millimeters, but ignore that fact, in fact, avoid doing that. only push out the two cylinders when you are snapped at the usual play angle, and make sure they are all the way in (cant stress that enough)
