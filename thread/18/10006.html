#89041 - Sausage Boy - Thu Jun 22, 2006 9:29 pm

I think there are two causes of your problem.

Firstly, each major DevkitARM release tends to break the emu's, this will probably be fixed in the next release of the emu.

The other problem is that Dualis expects the FIFO_NOP command to actually use one u32 of data, when it's really not supposed to need any. I've notifyed Mic about the problem, and he will most likely fix that in the next release as well. Just wait and see.
