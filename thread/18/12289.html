Ok, this was quick and dirty port of AineBot 0.8.12, so maybe not everything works fine... it's just a showcase, don't expect much, she acts stupid sometimes (especially at the beginning), so please be patient with her... and please don't annoy her much, or she may stop talking to you.
I am not responsible if she destroys your DS, memory card, flashcart, eat your children, burn your house... Try at your own responsability...
Extract the archive somewhere
Patch for your flashcart using dlditool
Copy all files to card (make sure that "data" and "log" directories are in root directory on card)
Run.

Before turning off your DS, type exit or quit (it will freeze then), that way all files will be closed, and no corruption can occur.

http://www.soliter.org/Amy.zip
