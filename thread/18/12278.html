Spider Solitaire DS


What?

Spider Solitaire DS is a Nintendo DS version of the One-player card game that has gained popularity since being included as a default game on the Windows Operating System.

The basic idea of the game is to get piles of cards from K to A of the same suit.
There are 104 cards with the possibility of playing with either 1 suit, 2 suits, or 4 suits.


[Images not permitted - Click here to view it]


Some may remember the original release mid-last year.
The major differences between that and this are;

* Cards are now highlighted when selected
* Added Cheating (Press A)
* Added Undo (Press B)
* Dragging cards now only 'jump piles' when dragged wholely over an adjacent pile. This improves the accuracy of dropping cards.
