#51233 - agentq - Wed Aug 17, 2005 12:36 pm

The GBA MP doesn't work with ScummVM because NDS files load all their resources into RAM. The old demo of Monkey Island 1 works because it is incredibly small. Unless you want to play some of the very old games like Manic Mansion you're not going to get anything inside of 4Mb

Eventually, ScummVM may be able to read the compactflash card directly and load only the resources it needs, and then it will work. But I don't have a GBA MP, so it's not going to be straight away.
