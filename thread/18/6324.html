#48119 - TJ - Sun Jul 17, 2005 7:51 am

No, not for booting homebrew.

At some point in the future, once the WiFi hardware is reversed and a TCP/IP stack is implemented, you could communicate with a DS over the network with a wireless router for file transfers and the like.

But you would still need some method to actually boot the homebrew software, so there will always be the need for a PassMe-like device or PCI wireless card.
