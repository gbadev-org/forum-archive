DS development > Forum Rules (updated Apr 29)

#32230 - tepples - Sun Dec 19, 2004 7:10 pm

This forum is for Nintendo DS development discussions only. Discuss coding for the Nintendo DS in this forum.

Discuss the various "passthrough" devices used to run unsigned code from a GBA flash cart, such as PassMe and WifiMe, in the DS Flash Equipment forums.

"Wishful thinking" proposals for games or emulators running on Nintendo DS belong in the DS Chat forum.

Do not post information available only under non-disclosure agreement (such as the official Nintendo SDK), and do not request information available only under non-disclosure agreement.

About subject lines: Several users have posted questions with topic titles that look like titles of topics that announce breakthroughs. If you're creating a new topic to announce that somebody has made a significant discovery, please use an exclamation mark (!) at the end. If you're creating a new topic to ask a question, please use a question mark (?) at the end.

Thank you for your cooperation, and everybody say "Yatta"!
