#71031 - tepples - Fri Feb 10, 2006 1:21 am

  1. In your paint program, convert the image into indexed mode. Make sure that color 0 of the palette is the one you want to be transparent (invisible, or displayed around edges).
  2. Convert the image into tiles (using, say, bmp2tiles).
  3. Include the tiles in the program (using either bin2s or GBFS, both of which come with devkitPro).
  4. In the program, copy the tiles into VRAM, and have one or more OAM entries refer to the tiles.
