#41037 - linus - Sun Apr 24, 2005 12:28 pm

In theory i think the only way you could get the DS to communicate with a DS at the moment is with custom firmware, which is possible for the Linksys WAG54G and WRT54G. But even with this I dont know whether the wireless card inside has the level of control needed for communicating with a DS. And if you could communicate with it there would be nothing worth doing really (without being a god at customizing the firmware), its much more advisable to get one of the cards here http://ralink.rapla.net/ and a GBA flashcart if you wanted to put other peoples (or your own) games on the DS.

As far as getting games at the moment playable over the net, well search for tunneling im sure you'll find lots of things. Basically there have been a few people who claimed to have done it but hadnt and some people say its impossible and some think it will happen one day. Well thats more than enough from me.
