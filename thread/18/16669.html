if your
Code:
BG_Maps_bin_size

is more than 32KB, you'll reach the place where
Code:
BG_TILE_RAM(1)

begins, so your tiles will overwrite (partially) your map...

btw I see no code to replace the part of your BIG map you want to use in your 32x32 map...
