#102789 - JimmyL - Sat Sep 16, 2006 11:39 pm

You can check out The Freesound Project, everything there is under an attribution creative commons license, so basically as long as you give credit, you can do whatever you want to(and with) the sound effects. There's a link to ccmixter on that site, a creative commons music site. I'd never seen it until about two minutes ago, but it looks like the music on that site is under a variety of CC licenses so you'll have to pay more attention if you find something you like there.
