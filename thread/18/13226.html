phpBB's built-in search engine has several highly annoying misfeatures:
  • It is preset to list topics, not posts within topics.
  • It is preset to look for topics containing any of the given words, not all of the given words.
  • There appears to be no way to search for an exact phrase.
  • It has a list of stop words. Too many forums put all three-letter words, including three-letter initialisms, on the stop word list.
  • It enforces a mandatory waiting period between searches, making it more difficult to incrementally formulate queries.
  • It uses HTTP's POST method, not the GET method, to perform the queries. This prevents users from linking to search result pages, which in turn makes it more difficult for users to show other users how to formulate effective search queries.
