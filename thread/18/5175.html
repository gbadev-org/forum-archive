#37609 - sandymac - Mon Mar 14, 2005 5:23 am

Maverick wrote:
What is the best prog to use for this?

Anything that captures to the libpcap format is good enough. Just make sure you turn off channel hopping so that you don't miss data.

After that I like to look at the captured data in Ethereal.

As for guides I'd suggest you look at the driver source to a free stumbler app. I have a Mac so I'm using KisMAC but I'm sure you can find more info with Google.
