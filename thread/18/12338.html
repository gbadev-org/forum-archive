#117312 - tepples - Sat Feb 03, 2007 8:02 pm

Get the V7 that's larger than 400 kilobytes.

The "can't restore" displays appear because NoFlashMe does not contain the newest versions of DS firmware. But the only reason that people seem to want to restore the original firmware is in order to send the DS back to Nintendo in order to have it repaired under warranty. The "stealth" version of FlashMe is enough for that.
