STOP! Don't assume that the diagram has anything to do with the pinout!
That symbol to the right of 5.2V is the international symbol for DC current!

According to the pinout on the charger:
Code:
   _____
  | + - |

This means positive left, negative right, with the curved side up.
This appears to be the polarity for the jack on the DS. The plug on the charger is of course a mirror image.
