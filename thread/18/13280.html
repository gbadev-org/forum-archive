#129761 - gabebear - Sat May 26, 2007 5:13 am

Blender will import .3DS and can export .MD2. Blender's MD2 exporter sucks, so you may need to fix it after exporting. After exporting from Blender run the MD2 through "Quake 2 Modeler" and it will fix the model so that it works correctly.

You can get "Quake 2 Modeler" from http://zippy-egoboo.sourceforge.net/tools.htm
