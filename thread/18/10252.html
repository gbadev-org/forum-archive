This is gonna sound a bit dumb, but do you use Logitech hardware on your computer?

I had a very similar problem on my work machine *for months* when using cygwin and other unix-y programs after the installation of a Logitech Quickcam 5000. I didn't put two and two together until ages later, but anyway the tasks they spawn interfere with many people's machines.

If you've got em, try killing off LVCOMSX.exe (and I'm sure there's another LV- or LS-like program) with your task manager.
