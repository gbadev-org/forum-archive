I had nothing better to do with my time... Quite sad, really.
And I found two things of interest on the components list.

Firstly, for the description of slot 2, it says precisely this: "Game Pak Slot- For Game Boy Advance Game Paks or Nintendo DS accessories."
This proves that Nintendo is, in fact, planning to use that slot for system expandability.

The port to which the battery charger plugs in is referred to as the "External Extension Connector." The description is as follows: "Allows connection of the NDS AC Adatpter for recharging the battery or operation of the DS using household power. The GBA Headphone Adapter can also be connected.
Thus arises the question: What the heck does this have to do with the AC adapter?! Thus: What else will that slot do?
(PS: I am aware that that thing has existed since the SP, so my question is is probably answered).
