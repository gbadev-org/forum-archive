#122253 - Tantrum - Sun Mar 18, 2007 3:25 am

The 3in1 is not hardware compatible with the Opera RAM expansion. For use with Opera you'd need to patch the ROM. AFAICT this is what the EZ5 slot-1 card does automagically (thus the claim that the 3in1 is 'compatible', even tho strictly speaking it isn't). Same with rumble. :|

On the plus side someone may yet (has?) make a patch for NitroHax for patching original carts to be able to use the 3in1 RAM and Rumble instead of having to use the official (but separate) RAM and Rumble.
