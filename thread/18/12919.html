#124435 - Lick - Thu Apr 05, 2007 11:45 pm

For the subscreen, use BG_TILE_RAM_SUB(#), BG_MAP_RAM_SUB(#) and BG_BMP_RAM_SUB(#).

Note that there is no ..._SUB when setting the BASE in the BG_CR.
