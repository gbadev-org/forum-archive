Enter this key sequence to take a screenshot of the current active window. Should work on any version of windows with mspaint installed (which should be pretty much all of them, unless you did something weird and deleted it):

Code:
Alt+Prntscreen
Win+R
mspaint
[ENTER]
Ctrl+E
1
[TAB]
1
[ENTER]
Ctrl+V

then save it or crop it or do whatever (note that cropping in mspaint is somewhat annoying to do)
