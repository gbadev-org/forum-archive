#123663 - dantheman - Fri Mar 30, 2007 1:09 am

I'm unaware of any homebrew application named "DSOrganizer" though if you mean "DSOrganize" you can check the stickied thread in the Misc. NDS forum.

Long story short though, you need to copy over the "DSOrganize" folder that came with your download to the root of your card. DLDI patching is good, but you need to have the folders there or else it won't function correctly. Download version 2.61 and copy the folder over before adding the alpha versions to your card, overwriting any old files it finds.

#123787 - HyperHacker - Sat Mar 31, 2007 3:44 am

Don't just copy over the DSOrganize folder directly if you have an old version installed, as that will overwrite your calendar/day planner/scribble/etc files. I just go into each folder and copy all the files that aren't those (including config, because who knows what's changed in it).
